# 1. Hypothesis Testing | 假设检验

## Definition | 定义

Hypothesis testing uses sample evidence to evaluate a statement about one or more population parameters. It asks whether an observed sample statistic is reasonably consistent with a hypothesized population value. The conclusion is probability-based, not certain.

假设检验利用样本证据评估关于一个或多个总体参数的陈述，判断观测到的样本统计量是否与假设的总体参数值合理一致。结论基于概率，而非绝对确定。

## Null and Alternative Hypotheses | 原假设与备择假设

- The null hypothesis, $H_0$, is treated as true unless sample evidence is sufficiently strong to reject it.  
  原假设 $H_0$ 在没有充分样本证据推翻前被视为成立。
- The alternative hypothesis, $H_a$, is supported when $H_0$ is rejected.  
  当 $H_0$ 被拒绝时，证据支持备择假设 $H_a$。
- $H_0$ and $H_a$ must be mutually exclusive and collectively exhaustive.  
  $H_0$ 与 $H_a$ 必须互斥且完全穷尽所有可能情况。
- Hypotheses concern population parameters; sample statistics provide the evidence.  
  假设针对总体参数；样本统计量提供检验证据。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- The equality sign belongs in $H_0$.  
  等号应放在 $H_0$ 中。
- Say **“fail to reject $H_0$,”** not “accept $H_0$.” Failure to reject means insufficient evidence, not proof that $H_0$ is true.  
  应表述为**“无法拒绝 $H_0$”**，而不是“接受 $H_0$”。无法拒绝仅表示证据不足，并不证明 $H_0$ 为真。

# 2. Six-Step Hypothesis Testing Process | 假设检验六步骤

## Process | 流程

1. **State $H_0$ and $H_a$.**  
   **陈述 $H_0$ 与 $H_a$。**
2. **Identify the appropriate test statistic and its probability distribution.**  
   **确定适当的检验统计量及其概率分布。**
3. **Specify the significance level $\alpha$.**  
   **设定显著性水平 $\alpha$。**
4. **State the decision rule using the critical value or p-value.**  
   **使用临界值或 p-value 陈述决策规则。**
5. **Collect data and calculate the test statistic.**  
   **收集数据并计算检验统计量。**
6. **Make the statistical decision and state it in context.**  
   **作出统计决策，并结合问题背景解释。**

## Test Statistic | 检验统计量

A test statistic is calculated from a sample and is compared with critical value(s) from its probability distribution. A result in the rejection region is statistically significant.

检验统计量根据样本计算，并与其概率分布对应的临界值比较。若统计量落入拒绝域，则结果具有统计显著性。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

Statistical significance does not automatically imply economic significance. A small effect can be statistically significant in a large sample but economically unimportant.

统计显著并不必然意味着经济显著。大样本中很小的效应也可能统计显著，但经济意义很弱。

---

# 3. One-Tailed and Two-Tailed Tests | 单尾与双尾检验

## Definition | 定义

The alternative hypothesis determines the tail(s) of the test.

备择假设决定检验采用单尾还是双尾。

| Alternative Hypothesis | Test Type | Rejection Region |
|---|---|---|
| $H_a:\theta>\theta_0$ | Right-tailed | Right tail |
| $H_a:\theta<\theta_0$ | Left-tailed | Left tail |
| $H_a:\theta\neq\theta_0$ | Two-tailed | Both tails |

| 备择假设 | 检验类型 | 拒绝域 |
|---|---|---|
| $H_a:\theta>\theta_0$ | 右尾检验 | 右尾 |
| $H_a:\theta<\theta_0$ | 左尾检验 | 左尾 |
| $H_a:\theta\neq\theta_0$ | 双尾检验 | 两端尾部 |

For a two-tailed test, total significance level $\alpha$ is split equally, with $\alpha/2$ in each tail. A one-tailed test places all $\alpha$ in one tail.

双尾检验将总显著性水平 $\alpha$ 平分至两端，每端为 $\alpha/2$；单尾检验将全部 $\alpha$ 放在一端。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- “Different” or “changed” $\Rightarrow$ two-tailed.  
  “不同”或“发生变化” $\Rightarrow$ 双尾。
- “Greater,” “exceeds,” or “improved” $\Rightarrow$ right-tailed.  
  “更大”“超过”或“提高” $\Rightarrow$ 右尾。
- “Less,” “below,” or “reduced” $\Rightarrow$ left-tailed.  
  “更小”“低于”或“下降” $\Rightarrow$ 左尾。

# 4. Significance, Confidence, Critical Values, and p-Value | 显著性、置信度、临界值与 p-Value

## Significance and Confidence | 显著性与置信度

The significance level $\alpha$ is the probability of rejecting a true null hypothesis. Its complement is the confidence level.

显著性水平 $\alpha$ 是错误拒绝真实原假设的概率；其补数是置信水平。

$$
\mathrm{Confidence\ level}=1-\alpha
$$

For example, $\alpha=5\%$ corresponds to a $95\%$ confidence level.

例如，$\alpha=5\%$ 对应 $95\%$ 的置信水平。

## Critical Value Rule | 临界值规则

Reject $H_0$ when the calculated test statistic is more extreme than the relevant critical value. Otherwise, fail to reject $H_0$.

若计算出的检验统计量比相应临界值更极端，则拒绝 $H_0$；否则无法拒绝 $H_0$。

## p-Value Rule | p-Value 规则

The p-value is the smallest significance level at which $H_0$ would be rejected using the observed test statistic.

p-value 是依据当前观测到的检验统计量能够拒绝 $H_0$ 的最小显著性水平。

| Comparison | Decision |
|---|---|
| p-value $\leq\alpha$ | Reject $H_0$ |
| p-value $>\alpha$ | Fail to reject $H_0$ |

| 比较 | 决策 |
|---|---|
| p-value $\leq\alpha$ | 拒绝 $H_0$ |
| p-value $>\alpha$ | 无法拒绝 $H_0$ |

## Confidence-Interval Rule | 置信区间规则

For a two-tailed test at significance level $\alpha$, reject $H_0$ if the hypothesized parameter value lies outside the corresponding $(1-\alpha)$ confidence interval.

对于显著性水平为 $\alpha$ 的双尾检验，若假设参数值位于对应的 $(1-\alpha)$ 置信区间之外，则拒绝 $H_0$。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

The p-value is **not** the probability that $H_0$ is true. It measures how incompatible the observed result is with $H_0$ under the test model.

p-value **不是** $H_0$ 为真的概率；它衡量在检验模型下，观测结果与 $H_0$ 的不相容程度。

# 5. Type I Error, Type II Error, and Power | 第一类错误、第二类错误与检验功效

## Decision Outcomes | 决策结果

| Decision | $H_0$ True | $H_0$ False |
|---|---|---|
| Fail to reject $H_0$ | Correct: $1-\alpha$ | Type II error: $\beta$ |
| Reject $H_0$ | Type I error: $\alpha$ | Correct; power: $1-\beta$ |

| 决策 | $H_0$ 为真 | $H_0$ 为假 |
|---|---|---|
| 无法拒绝 $H_0$ | 正确：$1-\alpha$ | 第二类错误：$\beta$ |
| 拒绝 $H_0$ | 第一类错误：$\alpha$ | 正确；检验功效：$1-\beta$ |

## Definitions | 定义

- **Type I error = false positive:** reject a true $H_0$.  
  **第一类错误 = 假阳性：** 错误拒绝真实的 $H_0$。
- **Type II error = false negative:** fail to reject a false $H_0$.  
  **第二类错误 = 假阴性：** 未能拒绝错误的 $H_0$。
- **Power:** correctly reject a false $H_0$.  
  **检验功效：** 正确拒绝错误的 $H_0$。

## Formula | 公式

$$
\mathrm{Power}=1-\beta
$$

## Trade-Off | 权衡

Holding sample size and effect size constant, lowering $\alpha$ reduces Type I error risk but increases $\beta$ and lowers power. Increasing sample size can reduce both error probabilities and increase power.

在样本量和效应大小不变时，降低 $\alpha$ 会降低第一类错误风险，但提高 $\beta$ 并降低检验功效。增加样本量可以同时降低两类错误概率并提高功效。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Smaller $\alpha$ $\Rightarrow$ harder to reject $H_0$ $\Rightarrow$ higher Type II error risk, all else equal.  
  更小的 $\alpha$ $\Rightarrow$ 更难拒绝 $H_0$ $\Rightarrow$ 第二类错误风险更高，其他条件不变。
- Power concerns the test's ability to detect a false $H_0$.  
  检验功效衡量检验发现错误 $H_0$ 的能力。

# 6. Test Selection Summary | 检验选择汇总

## Parametric Test Map | 参数检验速查

| Objective | Test Statistic | Distribution | Degrees of Freedom |
|---|---|---|---:|
| Single mean, $\sigma$ unknown | $t$ | $t$ | $n-1$ |
| Difference between two independent means, equal unknown variances | $t$ with pooled variance | $t$ | $n_1+n_2-2$ |
| Mean of paired differences | $t$ | $t$ | $n-1$ |
| Single variance | $\chi^2$ | Chi-square | $n-1$ |
| Difference between two independent variances | $F$ | $F$ | $n_1-1,n_2-1$ |
| Correlation | $t$ | $t$ | $n-2$ |
| Independence of categorical variables | Chi-square statistic | Chi-square | $(r-1)(c-1)$ |

| 检验目标 | 检验统计量 | 分布 | 自由度 |
|---|---|---|---:|
| 单总体均值，$\sigma$ 未知 | $t$ | $t$ | $n-1$ |
| 两个独立总体均值之差，未知但等方差 | 合并方差 $t$ | $t$ | $n_1+n_2-2$ |
| 配对差值的均值 | $t$ | $t$ | $n-1$ |
| 单总体方差 | $\chi^2$ | 卡方 | $n-1$ |
| 两个独立总体方差之差 | $F$ | $F$ | $n_1-1,n_2-1$ |
| 相关系数 | $t$ | $t$ | $n-2$ |
| 分类变量独立性 | 卡方统计量 | 卡方 | $(r-1)(c-1)$ |

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

Before comparing two means, first ask whether observations are **independent or paired**. Before testing risk, ask whether the question concerns **one variance or a ratio of two variances**.

比较两个均值前，先判断样本是**独立还是配对**；检验风险前，先判断问题涉及**单一方差还是两个方差之比**。

# 7. Test of a Single Mean | 单总体均值检验

## Formula | 公式

For a normally distributed population with unknown variance:

对于总体服从正态分布且方差未知的情况：

$$
t=\frac{\bar{X}-\mu_0}{s/\sqrt{n}}
$$

$$
df=n-1
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $\bar{X}$ | Sample mean | 样本均值 |
| $\mu_0$ | Hypothesized population mean | 假设总体均值 |
| $s$ | Sample standard deviation | 样本标准差 |
| $n$ | Sample size | 样本量 |

If population standard deviation $\sigma$ is known, use a $z$-statistic. In practice, $\sigma$ is usually unknown, so the $t$-test is common.

若总体标准差 $\sigma$ 已知，则使用 $z$ 统计量。实务中通常不知道 $\sigma$，因此常用 $t$ 检验。

## Worked Example 1: Willco | 计算示例 1：Willco

Management claims that mean annual net income exceeds USD24 million. Given $\bar{X}=30$, $s=10$, and $n=6$:

管理层声称年均净利润超过 USD24 million。已知 $\bar{X}=30$、$s=10$、$n=6$：

$$
H_0:\mu\leq24
$$

$$
H_a:\mu>24
$$

$$
t=\frac{30-24}{10/\sqrt{6}}=1.470
$$

With $df=5$ and critical value $2.015$, $1.470<2.015$; fail to reject $H_0$. There is insufficient evidence that mean annual net income exceeds USD24 million.

自由度为 5，临界值为 2.015。由于 $1.470<2.015$，无法拒绝 $H_0$；没有充分证据证明年均净利润超过 USD24 million。

## Worked Example 2: Sendar Fund | 计算示例 2：Sendar 基金

Given $\bar{X}=1.50\%$, $\mu_0=1.10\%$, $s=3.60\%$, $n=24$, and $\alpha=5\%$:

已知 $\bar{X}=1.50\%$、$\mu_0=1.10\%$、$s=3.60\%$、$n=24$、$\alpha=5\%$：

$$
H_0:\mu=1.10\%
$$

$$
H_a:\mu\neq1.10\%
$$

$$
t=\frac{1.50-1.10}{3.60/\sqrt{24}}=0.544
$$

With $df=23$ and critical values $\pm2.069$, fail to reject $H_0$.

自由度为 23，临界值为 $\pm2.069$，因此无法拒绝 $H_0$。

The corresponding 95% confidence interval is:

对应的 95% 置信区间为：

$$
\bar{X}\pm t_c\frac{s}{\sqrt{n}}
$$

$$
1.50\%\pm2.069\left(\frac{3.60\%}{\sqrt{24}}\right)=(-0.0204\%,3.0204\%)
$$

Because $1.10\%$ lies inside the interval, the confidence-interval method gives the same decision.

由于 $1.10\%$ 位于区间内，置信区间法给出相同结论。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

The numerator measures distance from the hypothesized mean; the denominator is the standard error. A larger absolute $t$ indicates stronger evidence against $H_0$.

分子衡量样本均值与假设均值的距离，分母是标准误。$|t|$ 越大，反对 $H_0$ 的证据越强。

# 8. Difference Between Two Independent Means | 两个独立总体均值之差

## Definition | 定义

Use this test when the two samples contain unrelated observations. If both populations are approximately normal with unknown but equal variances, estimate the common variance by pooling the two sample variances.

当两个样本的观测值彼此无关时使用该检验。若两个总体近似正态，方差未知但相等，则合并两个样本方差以估计共同方差。

## Formula | 公式

$$
s_p^2=\frac{(n_1-1)s_1^2+(n_2-1)s_2^2}{n_1+n_2-2}
$$

$$
t=\frac{(\bar{X}_1-\bar{X}_2)-(\mu_1-\mu_2)_0}{s_p\sqrt{\frac{1}{n_1}+\frac{1}{n_2}}}
$$

$$
df=n_1+n_2-2
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $s_p^2$ | Pooled variance estimate | 合并方差估计值 |
| $(\mu_1-\mu_2)_0$ | Hypothesized difference in population means | 假设的总体均值之差 |
| $n_1,n_2$ | Sample sizes | 两个样本的样本量 |

## Worked Example | 计算示例

For two independent ACE High Yield Index periods:

对于 ACE 高收益指数的两个独立期间：

| Period | Mean Return | Standard Deviation | $n$ |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| 1 | 0.01775% | 0.31580% | 445 |
| 2 | 0.01134% | 0.38760% | 859 |

$$
t\approx0.301
$$

The statistic is not sufficiently extreme at the 5% two-tailed level, so fail to reject equal population means.

在 5% 双尾显著性水平下，该统计量不够极端，因此无法拒绝两个总体均值相等的原假设。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

Do not use the pooled-variance test unless samples are independent and the equal-variance assumption is appropriate.

只有在样本相互独立且等方差假设合理时，才能使用合并方差检验。

---

# 9. Paired Comparisons Test | 配对比较检验

## Definition | 定义

Use paired comparisons when observations are naturally matched, such as two indexes measured on the same dates or before-and-after observations for the same firms. Test the mean of the within-pair differences.

当观测值天然配对时使用配对比较检验，例如同一日期的两个指数，或同一公司的前后数据。检验对象是每一对差值的总体均值。

## Formula | 公式

$$
d_i=X_i-Y_i
$$

$$
t=\frac{\bar{d}-\mu_{d,0}}{s_d/\sqrt{n}}
$$

$$
df=n-1
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $d_i$ | Difference for pair $i$ | 第 $i$ 对观测值的差值 |
| $\bar{d}$ | Mean sample difference | 样本平均差值 |
| $\mu_{d,0}$ | Hypothesized population mean difference | 假设的总体平均差值 |
| $s_d$ | Standard deviation of paired differences | 配对差值的标准差 |

## Interpretation | 解释

Pairing removes variation caused by the shared date, firm, or other common element. When pairing is valid, this test is generally more powerful than treating the samples as independent.

配对可以消除由共同日期、公司或其他共同因素造成的变异。若配对合理，该检验通常比把样本视为独立样本更有功效。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

The test uses the standard deviation of the **differences**, not the separate standard deviations of the two samples.

该检验使用**差值的标准差**，而不是两个样本各自的标准差。

---

# 10. Test of a Single Variance | 单总体方差检验

## Definition | 定义

For a single normally distributed population, test a hypothesized variance using a chi-square statistic.

对于单一正态总体，使用卡方统计量检验假设方差。

## Formula | 公式

$$
\chi^2=\frac{(n-1)s^2}{\sigma_0^2}
$$

$$
df=n-1
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $s^2$ | Sample variance | 样本方差 |
| $\sigma_0^2$ | Hypothesized population variance | 假设总体方差 |

## Worked Example: Sendar Fund | 计算示例：Sendar 基金

Test whether standard deviation is less than $4\%$. Given $n=24$ and sample standard deviation $s=3.60\%$:

检验标准差是否低于 $4\%$。已知 $n=24$，样本标准差 $s=3.60\%$：

$$
H_0:\sigma^2\geq16
$$

$$
H_a:\sigma^2<16
$$

Variance units in the calculation are percentage squared.

计算中的方差单位为百分比平方。

$$
\chi^2=\frac{(24-1)(12.96)}{16}=18.63
$$

With $df=23$ and a left-tail critical value of $13.0905$, $18.63>13.0905$; fail to reject $H_0$. There is insufficient evidence that standard deviation is below $4\%$.

自由度为 23，左尾临界值为 13.0905。由于 $18.63>13.0905$，无法拒绝 $H_0$；没有充分证据证明标准差低于 $4\%$。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Convert a standard-deviation hypothesis into a variance hypothesis by squaring.  
  检验标准差时，应先平方转换为方差假设。
- Keep units consistent: decimals squared or percentage points squared.  
  单位必须一致：全部使用小数平方或百分比点平方。
- A variance test assumes a normally distributed population.  
  方差检验假设总体服从正态分布。

---

# 11. Test of Two Independent Variances | 两个独立总体方差检验

## Definition | 定义

For two independent random samples from normally distributed populations, compare variances using an $F$-test.

对于来自两个正态总体的独立随机样本，使用 $F$ 检验比较方差。

## Formula | 公式

$$
F=\frac{s_1^2}{s_2^2}
$$

$$
df_1=n_1-1
$$

$$
df_2=n_2-1
$$

The numerator determines $df_1$; the denominator determines $df_2$.

分子样本决定 $df_1$；分母样本决定 $df_2$。

## Worked Example | 计算示例

For variance before and after a market disruption:

对于市场冲击前后的方差：

| Period | $n$ | Variance |
|---|---:|---:|
| Before | 120 | 22.367 |
| After | 120 | 15.795 |

$$
H_0:\sigma_1^2=\sigma_2^2
$$

$$
H_a:\sigma_1^2\neq\sigma_2^2
$$

$$
F=\frac{22.367}{15.795}=1.4161
$$

With $df_1=df_2=119$ and two-tailed critical values $0.6969$ and $1.4349$, the statistic lies inside the non-rejection region. Fail to reject equality of variances.

自由度 $df_1=df_2=119$，双尾临界值为 0.6969 和 1.4349。统计量位于非拒绝域内，因此无法拒绝方差相等的原假设。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- The $F$ distribution is not symmetric.  
  $F$ 分布并不对称。
- Match degrees of freedom to the numerator and denominator in the stated order.  
  必须按照分子、分母的顺序匹配自由度。
- “Variance changed” means a two-tailed test; “variance increased” means a right-tailed test when the later variance is in the numerator.  
  “方差发生变化”表示双尾检验；若后期方差置于分子，“方差提高”表示右尾检验。

---

# 12. Parametric vs. Nonparametric Tests | 参数检验与非参数检验

## Definition | 定义

A parametric test concerns a population parameter or relies on specific distributional assumptions. A nonparametric test does not concern a parameter or makes minimal assumptions about the population.

参数检验针对总体参数，或依赖特定分布假设。非参数检验不针对总体参数，或仅对总体作最低限度的假设。

## Comparison | 对比

| Feature | Parametric Test | Nonparametric Test |
|---|---|---|
| Focus | Parameters such as mean or variance | Ranks, signs, medians, randomness, or distributional form |
| Assumptions | Stronger distributional assumptions | Minimal distributional assumptions |
| Data scale | Usually stronger measurement scale | Can use ordinal or ranked data |
| Outlier sensitivity | Often more sensitive | Often more robust |
| Power | Usually higher if assumptions hold | Often lower if parametric assumptions hold |

| 特征 | 参数检验 | 非参数检验 |
|---|---|---|
| 关注对象 | 均值、方差等参数 | 秩、符号、中位数、随机性或分布形式 |
| 假设 | 较强的分布假设 | 最低限度的分布假设 |
| 数据尺度 | 通常要求较高测量尺度 | 可使用顺序或排名数据 |
| 对异常值的敏感度 | 通常更敏感 | 通常更稳健 |
| 检验功效 | 假设成立时通常更高 | 参数假设成立时通常较低 |

## When to Use Nonparametric Tests | 何时使用非参数检验

1. Data violate the distributional assumptions of a parametric test.  
   数据不满足参数检验的分布假设。
2. The sample contains influential outliers.  
   样本包含影响较大的异常值。
3. Data are ranks or measured on an ordinal scale.  
   数据是排名或顺序尺度。
4. The hypothesis does not concern a population parameter.  
   假设不涉及总体参数。

## Test Alternatives | 检验替代方案

| Objective | Parametric Test | Nonparametric Alternative |
|---|---|---|
| Single mean/location | $t$-test or $z$-test | Wilcoxon signed-rank test |
| Two independent samples | Independent-samples $t$-test | Mann–Whitney U / Wilcoxon rank-sum test |
| Paired samples | Paired $t$-test | Wilcoxon signed-rank test or sign test |
| Randomness of a sequence | Not a parameter test | Runs test |

| 目标 | 参数检验 | 非参数替代方法 |
|---|---|---|
| 单总体位置 | $t$ 检验或 $z$ 检验 | Wilcoxon 符号秩检验 |
| 两个独立样本 | 独立样本 $t$ 检验 | Mann–Whitney U / Wilcoxon 秩和检验 |
| 配对样本 | 配对 $t$ 检验 | Wilcoxon 符号秩检验或符号检验 |
| 序列随机性 | 不属于参数检验 | 游程检验 |

## Investment Applications | 投资应用

- Use a rank-based test for investment-manager rankings.  
  对投资管理人排名使用基于秩的检验。
- Use a median-based nonparametric test when outliers distort the mean.  
  当异常值扭曲均值时，使用基于中位数的非参数检验。
- Use a runs test to examine whether stock price changes follow a random sequence and whether past changes predict future changes.  
  使用游程检验判断股价变化是否具有随机性，以及过去变化能否预测未来变化。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

If parametric assumptions are satisfied, prefer the parametric test because it generally has greater power. Nonparametric tests extend inference when those assumptions fail or the data are ranks, signs, or heavily affected by outliers.

若参数检验假设成立，通常优先选择参数检验，因为其功效更高。当假设不成立，或数据为秩、符号或受异常值严重影响时，非参数检验可扩展统计推断的适用范围。

# CFA Memory Points | CFA 记忆重点

## 1. Core Decision Rule | 核心决策规则

Let $p$ denote the p-value. | 令 $p$ 表示 p-value。

$$
p\leq\alpha\Rightarrow\mathrm{Reject}\ H_0
$$

Smaller p-value means stronger evidence against $H_0$.

p-value 越小，反对 $H_0$ 的证据越强。

## 2. Error Relationships | 错误关系

$$
P(\mathrm{Type\ I\ error})=\alpha
$$

$$
P(\mathrm{Type\ II\ error})=\beta
$$

$$
\mathrm{Power}=1-\beta
$$

Smaller $\alpha$ increases $\beta$ and reduces power, all else equal; increasing $n$ can reduce both errors.

其他条件不变，降低 $\alpha$ 会提高 $\beta$ 并降低功效；增加 $n$ 可以同时降低两类错误。

## 3. Test-Selection Cue | 检验选择提示

- One unknown-variance mean $\Rightarrow$ $t$.  
  单总体未知方差均值 $\Rightarrow$ $t$。
- One variance $\Rightarrow$ $\chi^2$.  
  单总体方差 $\Rightarrow$ $\chi^2$。
- Two independent variances $\Rightarrow$ $F$.  
  两个独立总体方差 $\Rightarrow$ $F$。
- Two dependent means $\Rightarrow$ paired $t$ on differences.  
  两个相关总体均值 $\Rightarrow$ 对差值使用配对 $t$。

## 4. Common Mistakes | 常见错误

- Do not “accept” $H_0$; fail to reject it.  
  不要“接受” $H_0$，应表述为无法拒绝。
- Do not interpret p-value as the probability that $H_0$ is true.  
  不要把 p-value 解释为 $H_0$ 为真的概率。
- Do not confuse statistical significance with economic significance.  
  不要混淆统计显著性与经济显著性。
- Do not use an independent-samples test for paired observations.  
  不要对配对观测使用独立样本检验。

## 5. Exam Shortcut | 考试技巧

Read the alternative hypothesis first: $>$ means right tail, $<$ means left tail, and $\neq$ means two tails. Then identify the parameter: mean $\rightarrow t$, one variance $\rightarrow\chi^2$, two variances $\rightarrow F$.

先读备择假设：$>$ 为右尾，$<$ 为左尾，$\neq$ 为双尾。再识别参数：均值 $\rightarrow t$，单方差 $\rightarrow\chi^2$，双方差 $\rightarrow F$。
