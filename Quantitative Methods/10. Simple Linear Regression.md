# 1. Simple Linear Regression Model | 简单线性回归模型

## Definition | 定义

Simple linear regression explains variation in one dependent variable using one independent variable. It quantifies a linear relationship, permits hypothesis tests about that relationship, and supports prediction.

简单线性回归使用一个自变量解释一个因变量的变动。它量化线性关系，可用于检验该关系并进行预测。

## Population Model | 总体模型

$$
Y_i=b_0+b_1X_i+\varepsilon_i
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $Y_i$ | Dependent or explained variable | 因变量或被解释变量 |
| $X_i$ | Independent or explanatory variable | 自变量或解释变量 |
| $b_0$ | Population intercept | 总体截距 |
| $b_1$ | Population slope | 总体斜率 |
| $\varepsilon_i$ | Population error term | 总体误差项 |
| $n$ | Number of paired observations | 成对观测数量 |

## Estimated Model | 估计模型

$$
\widehat{Y}_i=\widehat{b}_0+\widehat{b}_1X_i
$$

$$
e_i=Y_i-\widehat{Y}_i
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $\widehat{Y}_i$ | Fitted or predicted value | 拟合值或预测值 |
| $\widehat{b}_0,\widehat{b}_1$ | Sample estimates of the regression coefficients | 回归系数的样本估计值 |
| $e_i$ | Sample residual | 样本残差 |

## Error vs. Residual | 误差项与残差

The error term belongs to the unknown true population relationship. The residual is observable after estimating the sample regression and is the vertical distance from an observation to the fitted line.

误差项属于未知的真实总体关系；残差在样本回归估计后可计算，是观测值到拟合线的垂直距离。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- **Explain $Y$ using $X$** means regress $Y$ on $X$: $Y$ is vertical-axis dependent variable; $X$ is horizontal-axis independent variable.  
  **用 $X$ 解释 $Y$** 表示将 $Y$ 对 $X$ 回归：$Y$ 是纵轴因变量，$X$ 是横轴自变量。
- One independent variable $\Rightarrow$ simple regression; more than one $\Rightarrow$ multiple regression.  
  一个自变量 $\Rightarrow$ 简单回归；多个自变量 $\Rightarrow$ 多元回归。

# 2. Ordinary Least Squares Estimation | 普通最小二乘估计

## Least Squares Criterion | 最小二乘准则

OLS chooses the intercept and slope that minimize the sum of squared vertical residuals, also called the sum of squares error (SSE) or residual sum of squares.

OLS 选择使垂直残差平方和最小的截距和斜率。该平方和也称误差平方和（SSE）或残差平方和。

$$
SSE=\sum_{i=1}^{n}e_i^2=\sum_{i=1}^{n}(Y_i-\widehat{Y}_i)^2
$$

## Slope and Intercept | 斜率与截距

Define the variation and co-variation sums:

定义变动和共同变动之和：

$$
S_{XX}=\sum_{i=1}^{n}(X_i-\bar{X})^2
$$

$$
S_{XY}=\sum_{i=1}^{n}(X_i-\bar{X})(Y_i-\bar{Y})
$$

The OLS slope is:

OLS 斜率为：

$$
\widehat{b}_1=\frac{S_{XY}}{S_{XX}}=\frac{s_{XY}}{s_X^2}
$$

The OLS intercept is:

OLS 截距为：

$$
\widehat{b}_0=\bar{Y}-\widehat{b}_1\bar{X}
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $\bar{X},\bar{Y}$ | Sample means | 样本均值 |
| $S_{XX}$ | Total variation in $X$ | $X$ 的总变动 |
| $S_{XY}$ | Co-variation of $X$ and $Y$ | $X$ 与 $Y$ 的共同变动 |
| $s_{XY}$ | Sample covariance | 样本协方差 |
| $s_X^2$ | Sample variance of $X$ | $X$ 的样本方差 |

## OLS Properties with an Intercept | 含截距 OLS 的性质

$$
\sum_{i=1}^{n}e_i=0
$$

$$
\overline{\widehat{Y}}=\bar{Y}
$$

The residuals sum to zero, but OLS minimizes the **sum of squared** residuals, not their simple sum.

残差之和为零，但 OLS 最小化的是**残差平方和**，而非残差的简单总和。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- The denominator of the slope is the variance of $X$; the denominator of correlation is the product of the standard deviations of $X$ and $Y$.  
  斜率分母是 $X$ 的方差；相关系数分母是 $X$、$Y$ 标准差的乘积。
- The sign of both slope and correlation is determined by covariance.  
  斜率和相关系数的符号均由协方差决定。
- Residuals have the same units as $Y$.  
  残差与 $Y$ 的单位相同。

# 3. Interpreting Regression Coefficients | 回归系数解释

## Intercept | 截距

$\widehat{b}_0$ is the predicted value of $Y$ when $X=0$. The interpretation may be economically meaningless if zero is impossible or far outside the observed range of $X$.

$\widehat{b}_0$ 是 $X=0$ 时 $Y$ 的预测值。若 $X=0$ 不可能出现或远离样本范围，则该解释可能没有经济意义。

## Slope | 斜率

$\widehat{b}_1$ is the predicted change in $Y$ for a one-unit increase in $X$.

$\widehat{b}_1$ 是 $X$ 增加一个单位时 $Y$ 的预测变动量。

$$
\Delta\widehat{Y}=\widehat{b}_1\Delta X
$$

- Positive slope: $X$ and predicted $Y$ move in the same direction.  
  正斜率: $X$ 与预测的 $Y$ 同向变动。
- Negative slope: $X$ and predicted $Y$ move in opposite directions.  
  负斜率: $X$ 与预测的 $Y$ 反向变动。

## Worked Example: ROA and CAPEX | 计算示例：ROA 与 CAPEX

Given | 已知：

$$
\bar{Y}=12.5,\quad \bar{X}=6.1,\quad S_{XY}=153.30,\quad S_{XX}=122.64
$$

Calculation | 计算：

$$
\widehat{b}_1=\frac{153.30}{122.64}=1.25
$$

$$
\widehat{b}_0=12.5-1.25(6.1)=4.875
$$

Answer | 答案：

$$
\widehat{ROA}=4.875+1.25(CAPEX)
$$

- At $CAPEX=0$, predicted ROA is $4.875\%$.  
  当 $CAPEX=0$ 时，预测 ROA 为 $4.875\%$。
- A one-percentage-point increase in CAPEX predicts a $1.25$-percentage-point increase in ROA.  
  CAPEX 每增加 1 个百分点，预测 ROA 增加 1.25 个百分点。

For Company C, $CAPEX=5$ and observed $ROA=15$:

对于公司 C, $CAPEX=5$，观测 $ROA=15$：

$$
\widehat{Y}=4.875+1.25(5)=11.125
$$

$$
e=15-11.125=3.875
$$

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

Always state units. “One unit” could mean one dollar, one million dollars, one decimal unit, or one percentage point.

必须明确单位。“一个单位”可能是一美元、一百万美元、一个小数单位或一个百分点。

# 4. Cross-Sectional and Time-Series Regressions | 横截面与时间序列回归

## Comparison | 对比

| Data Type | Structure | Example | Common Notation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cross-sectional | Many entities at one time | Company P/E explained by forecast EPS growth | $i=1,\ldots,n$ |
| Time-series | One entity across many periods | Interest rate explained by inflation over time | $t=1,\ldots,T$ |

| 数据类型 | 结构 | 示例 | 常用下标 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 横截面 | 同一时点的多个实体 | 用预测 EPS 增长解释不同公司的 P/E | $i=1,\ldots,n$ |
| 时间序列 | 同一实体的多个期间 | 用通胀率解释不同时期的利率 | $t=1,\ldots,T$ |

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

Time-series regressions require particular attention to independence because nearby observations may be autocorrelated. Cross-sectional regressions often require close attention to unequal residual variance across entities.

时间序列回归需特别关注独立性，因为相邻观测可能自相关；横截面回归常需关注不同实体间残差方差不相等。

# 5. Four Assumptions of Simple Linear Regression | 简单线性回归的四项假设

## Assumption Summary | 假设汇总

| Assumption | Meaning | Desired Residual Pattern | Warning Sign |
|---|---|---|---|
| Linearity | Mean relationship between $Y$ and $X$ is linear; $X$ is treated as non-stochastic | Random around zero versus $X$ | Curvature or systematic over-/underprediction |
| Homoskedasticity | Residual variance is constant | Similar vertical spread throughout | Funnel shape or clusters/regimes with different spreads |
| Independence | Observation pairs and residuals are uncorrelated across observations | No time-order pattern | Seasonality, cycles, runs, or autocorrelation |
| Normality | Residuals are normally distributed | Approximately normal residual histogram | Strong skewness, heavy tails, or influential outliers |

| 假设 | 含义 | 理想残差表现 | 警示信号 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 线性 | $Y$ 与 $X$ 的均值关系为线性；将 $X$ 视为非随机 | 相对 $X$ 在零附近随机分布 | 曲线形状或系统性高估/低估 |
| 同方差 | 残差方差恒定 | 各处垂直离散程度相近 | 漏斗形或不同方差的群组/状态 |
| 独立性 | 成对观测及残差跨观测不相关 | 按时间无规律 | 季节性、周期、连续同号或自相关 |
| 正态性 | 残差服从正态分布 | 残差直方图近似正态 | 严重偏度、厚尾或高影响异常值 |

## Mathematical Form | 数学形式

Linearity | 线性：

$$
E(Y_i\mid X_i)=b_0+b_1X_i
$$

Homoskedasticity | 同方差：

$$
\mathrm{Var}(\varepsilon_i\mid X_i)=\sigma^2
$$

Independence | 独立性：

$$
\mathrm{Cov}(\varepsilon_i,\varepsilon_j)=0\quad i\neq j
$$

Normality | 正态性：

$$
\varepsilon_i\sim N(0,\sigma^2)
$$

## Interpretation | 解释

- A curved residual pattern indicates that a linear functional form is inappropriate.  
  残差呈曲线规律，说明线性函数形式不合适。
- Different residual bands across regimes indicate heteroskedasticity and possibly a structural change.  
  不同状态下残差带宽不同，表明异方差，并可能存在结构变化。
- Seasonal residuals indicate autocorrelation and violation of independence.  
  残差呈季节性，表明自相关并违背独立性。
- Normality applies to residuals, not necessarily to $X$ or $Y$. Large samples may permit approximate inference through asymptotic results.  
  正态性要求针对残差，而非必然针对 $X$ 或 $Y$。大样本时可借助渐近结果进行近似推断。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

Residual plots should look random. A high $R^2$ does not prove that regression assumptions are satisfied.

残差图应表现为随机散布。较高的 $R^2$ 并不能证明回归假设成立。

# 6. Decomposition of Variation | 变异分解

## Formula | 公式

Total variation | 总变异：

$$
SST=\sum_{i=1}^{n}(Y_i-\bar{Y})^2
$$

Explained variation | 已解释变异：

$$
SSR=\sum_{i=1}^{n}(\widehat{Y}_i-\bar{Y})^2
$$

Unexplained variation | 未解释变异：

$$
SSE=\sum_{i=1}^{n}(Y_i-\widehat{Y}_i)^2
$$

$$
SST=SSR+SSE
$$

## Interpretation | 解释

The regression attempts to convert total variation in $Y$ into explained variation. Better fit means higher SSR and lower SSE relative to the same SST.

回归的目标是把 $Y$ 的总变异转化为已解释变异。对于相同的 SST，更好的拟合意味着 SSR 较高、SSE 较低。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- **SST = total; SSR = regression/explained; SSE = error/unexplained.**  
  **SST = 总变异；SSR = 回归/已解释；SSE = 误差/未解释。**
- The sample variance of $Y$ is $SST/(n-1)$.  
  $Y$ 的样本方差为 $SST/(n-1)$。

---

# 7. Coefficient of Determination | 决定系数

## Formula | 公式

$$
R^2=\frac{SSR}{SST}=1-\frac{SSE}{SST}
$$

In simple linear regression with an intercept:

在含截距的简单线性回归中：

$$
R^2=r_{XY}^2
$$

## Interpretation | 解释

$R^2$ is the percentage of variation in the dependent variable explained by the independent variable. It ranges from 0 to 1 in the standard model with an intercept.

$R^2$ 表示自变量解释的因变量变异比例。在标准的含截距模型中，其范围为 0 至 1。

## ROA Example | ROA 示例

$$
R^2=\frac{191.625}{239.50}=0.8001
$$

CAPEX explains $80.01\%$ of the sample variation in ROA.

CAPEX 解释了样本中 ROA 变异的 $80.01\%$。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- $R^2$ is descriptive, not a hypothesis test.  
  $R^2$ 是描述性指标，不是假设检验。
- A high $R^2$ does not establish causality, correct specification, or valid assumptions.  
  高 $R^2$ 不能证明因果关系、模型设定正确或假设有效。
- $R^2$ is a relative measure; the standard error of estimate is an absolute measure in units of $Y$.  
  $R^2$ 是相对指标；估计标准误是以 $Y$ 为单位的绝对指标。

# 8. ANOVA, F-Test, and Standard Error of Estimate | ANOVA、F 检验与估计标准误

## ANOVA Structure | ANOVA 结构

| Source | Sum of Squares | Degrees of Freedom | Mean Square |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Regression | $SSR$ | 1 | $MSR=SSR$ |
| Error | $SSE$ | $n-2$ | $MSE=SSE/(n-2)$ |
| Total | $SST$ | $n-1$ | — |

## F-Test of Model Fit | 模型拟合的 F 检验

$$
H_0:b_1=0
$$

$$
H_a:b_1\neq0
$$

$$
F=\frac{MSR}{MSE}
$$

$$
df=(1,n-2)
$$

The rejection region is in the right tail because stronger fit means explained variation is large relative to unexplained variation.

拒绝域位于右尾，因为更强的拟合意味着已解释变异相对未解释变异较大。

## Standard Error of Estimate | 估计标准误

$$
s_e=\sqrt{MSE}=\sqrt{\frac{SSE}{n-2}}
$$

The standard error of estimate, also called standard error of regression or root mean square error, measures the typical distance of observed $Y$ values from the fitted line. Lower is better, all else equal.

估计标准误也称回归标准误或均方根误差，衡量观测 $Y$ 与拟合线之间的典型距离。其他条件相同，越低越好。

## Worked Example: ROA ANOVA | 计算示例：ROA 的 ANOVA

| Source | Sum of Squares | $df$ | Mean Square | $F$ |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| Regression | 191.625 | 1 | 191.625 | 16.0104 |
| Error | 47.875 | 4 | 11.96875 | — |
| Total | 239.500 | 5 | — | — |

$$
s_e=\sqrt{11.96875}=3.4596
$$

At $\alpha=5\%$, the critical $F$ value is $7.71$. Because $16.0104>7.71$, reject $H_0$ and conclude that the slope differs from zero.

在 $\alpha=5\%$ 时，临界 $F$ 值为 $7.71$。由于 $16.0104>7.71$，拒绝 $H_0$，认为斜率不等于零。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

ANOVA arithmetic is a chain: sums of squares $\rightarrow$ mean squares $\rightarrow F$ and $s_e$.

ANOVA 的计算链为：平方和 $\rightarrow$ 均方 $\rightarrow F$ 与 $s_e$。

# 9. Hypothesis Tests of Regression Coefficients | 回归系数假设检验

## Slope Standard Error and Test | 斜率标准误与检验

$$
s_{\widehat{b}_1}=\frac{s_e}{\sqrt{S_{XX}}}
$$

$$
t=\frac{\widehat{b}_1-B_1}{s_{\widehat{b}_1}}
$$

$$
df=n-2
$$

## Intercept Standard Error and Test | 截距标准误与检验

$$
s_{\widehat{b}_0}=s_e\sqrt{\frac{1}{n}+\frac{\bar{X}^2}{S_{XX}}}
$$

$$
t=\frac{\widehat{b}_0-B_0}{s_{\widehat{b}_0}}
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $B_1$ | Hypothesized population slope | 假设总体斜率 |
| $B_0$ | Hypothesized population intercept | 假设总体截距 |
| $s_{\widehat{b}_1}$ | Standard error of estimated slope | 估计斜率的标准误 |
| $s_{\widehat{b}_0}$ | Standard error of estimated intercept | 估计截距的标准误 |

## ROA Test Results | ROA 检验结果

Given | 已知:

$$
\widehat{b}_1=1.25
$$

$$
s_{\widehat{b}_1}=0.312398
$$

| Hypothesis | Calculated $t$ | Critical Value(s) | Decision |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| $H_0:b_1=0$ vs. $H_a:b_1\neq0$ | 4.001 | $\pm2.776$ | Reject $H_0$ |
| $H_0:b_1=1$ vs. $H_a:b_1\neq1$ | 0.800 | $\pm2.776$ | Fail to reject $H_0$ |
| $H_0:b_1\leq0$ vs. $H_a:b_1>0$ | 4.001 | $2.132$ | Reject $H_0$ |

For the intercept test $H_0:b_0\leq3$ versus $H_a:b_0>3$, the calculated $t=0.7905<2.132$; fail to reject $H_0$.

对于截距检验 $H_0:b_0\leq3$ 对 $H_a:b_0>3$，计算得到 $t=0.7905<2.132$，无法拒绝 $H_0$。

## Key Equivalence in Simple Regression | 简单回归中的关键等价关系

For testing $H_0:b_1=0$ in simple linear regression:

在简单线性回归中检验 $H_0:b_1=0$ 时：

$$
F=t^2
$$

The zero-slope test and zero-correlation test produce the same $t$ statistic.

零斜率检验与零相关检验得到相同的 $t$ 统计量。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- More variation in $X$ lowers the slope standard error, all else equal.  
  其他条件相同, $X$ 的变动越大，斜率标准误越低。
- Most software coefficient p-values test a two-tailed null that the coefficient equals zero.  
  大多数软件输出的系数 p-value 对应“系数等于零”的双尾检验。
- Compare p-value with $\alpha$: p-value $\leq\alpha$ means reject $H_0$.  
  将 p-value 与 $\alpha$ 比较：p-value $\leq\alpha$ 时拒绝 $H_0$。

# 10. Indicator Variables | 指示变量

## Definition | 定义

An indicator or dummy variable takes the value 0 or 1 according to whether an observation has a specified characteristic.

指示变量或虚拟变量根据观测值是否具有特定特征取 0 或 1。

## Model and Interpretation | 模型与解释

$$
Y_i=b_0+b_1D_i+\varepsilon_i
$$

When $D=0$:

当 $D=0$：

$$
\widehat{Y}_0=\widehat{b}_0
$$

When $D=1$:

当 $D=1$：

$$
\widehat{Y}_1=\widehat{b}_0+\widehat{b}_1
$$

Therefore:

因此：

$$
\widehat{b}_1=\widehat{Y}_1-\widehat{Y}_0
$$

The intercept is the mean of the reference group ($D=0$); the slope is the difference between the group means.

截距是基准组（$D=0$）的均值；斜率是两组均值之差。

## Earnings-Announcement Example | 盈利公告示例

| Quantity | Estimate | Interpretation |
|---|---:|---|
| Intercept | 0.5629% | Mean return in non-announcement months |
| Slope | 1.2098% | Announcement-month mean minus non-announcement-month mean |
| Predicted mean when $D=1$ | 1.7727% | Mean return in announcement months |

The slope $t$ statistic is $10.4435$, so the difference in group means is statistically significant at the stated 5% level.

斜率的 $t$ 统计量为 $10.4435$，因此两组均值之差在给定的 5% 水平下具有统计显著性。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

The 0 category is the reference group. Changing which group is coded 0 changes the coefficient interpretation and sign, but not the fitted group means.

取值为 0 的类别是基准组。改变哪个组编码为 0 会改变系数解释和符号，但不会改变两组的拟合均值。

# 11. Prediction and Prediction Intervals | 预测与预测区间

## Predicted Value | 预测值

For a forecast value $X_f$:

对于给定的预测自变量值 $X_f$：

$$
\widehat{Y}_f=\widehat{b}_0+\widehat{b}_1X_f
$$

## Standard Error of Forecast | 预测标准误

For an individual future observation:

对于单个未来观测值：

$$
s_f=s_e\sqrt{1+\frac{1}{n}+\frac{(X_f-\bar{X})^2}{S_{XX}}}
$$

## Prediction Interval | 预测区间

$$
\widehat{Y}_f\pm t_{c}s_f
$$

$$
df=n-2
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $X_f$ | Forecasted independent-variable value | 自变量预测值 |
| $\widehat{Y}_f$ | Predicted dependent-variable value | 因变量预测值 |
| $s_f$ | Standard error of forecast | 预测标准误 |
| $t_c$ | Critical $t$ value | 临界 $t$ 值 |

## Directional Relationships | 方向关系

- Lower $s_e$ $\Rightarrow$ lower $s_f$.  
  更低的 $s_e$ $\Rightarrow$ 更低的 $s_f$。
- Larger $n$ $\Rightarrow$ lower $s_f$.  
  更大的 $n$ $\Rightarrow$ 更低的 $s_f$。
- $X_f$ closer to $\bar{X}$ $\Rightarrow$ lower $s_f$.  
  $X_f$ 越接近 $\bar{X}$ $\Rightarrow$ $s_f$ 越低。
- $X_f$ farther from $\bar{X}$ $\Rightarrow$ wider prediction interval.  
  $X_f$ 越远离 $\bar{X}$ $\Rightarrow$ 预测区间越宽。

## Worked Example: NPM Forecast | 计算示例：NPM 预测

Given | 已知：

$$
\widehat{NPM}=16.5-1.3(RDR)
$$

$$
n=8,\quad \bar{X}=7.5,\quad S_{XX}=30,\quad s_e=1.8619,\quad t_c=2.447
$$

Calculation and Answer | 计算与答案：

| $RDR_f$ | $\widehat{NPM}_f$ | $s_f$ | 95% Prediction Interval |
|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| 5 | 10.00% | 2.1499% | $[4.7391\%,15.2609\%]$ |
| 15 | -3.00% | 3.2249% | $[-10.8913\%,4.8913\%]$ |

The forecast at $RDR=15$ is farther from $\bar{X}=7.5$ and outside the observed sample range, so uncertainty is larger and the economic plausibility of extrapolation should be questioned.

$RDR=15$ 距离 $\bar{X}=7.5$ 更远，且超出样本观测范围，因此不确定性更大，并应质疑外推结果的经济合理性。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- A point forecast is not complete without considering forecast uncertainty.  
  点预测必须结合预测不确定性解释。
- The prediction interval is narrowest near $\bar{X}$, not necessarily at $X=0$.  
  预测区间在 $\bar{X}$ 附近最窄，而不一定在 $X=0$ 时最窄。
- Avoid mechanically trusting extrapolations beyond the observed $X$ range.  
  不要机械相信超出已观测 $X$ 范围的外推。

# 12. Functional Forms | 函数形式

## Why Transform Variables? | 为什么变换变量

Economic and financial relationships are often nonlinear in their original variables. Transforming $Y$, $X$, or both may linearize the relationship, improve fit, and remove patterns from residuals.

经济和金融变量在原始形式下常呈非线性关系。对 $Y$、$X$ 或两者进行变换，可能使关系线性化、改善拟合并消除残差规律。

## Functional-Form Summary | 函数形式汇总

| Form | Model | Slope Interpretation |
|---|---|---|
| Lin-lin | $Y=b_0+b_1X+\varepsilon$ | One-unit increase in $X$ $\Rightarrow$ $b_1$-unit change in $Y$ |
| Log-lin | $\ln Y=b_0+b_1X+\varepsilon$ | One-unit increase in $X$ $\Rightarrow$ approximately $100b_1\%$ change in $Y$ |
| Lin-log | $Y=b_0+b_1\ln X+\varepsilon$ | 1% increase in $X$ $\Rightarrow$ approximately $b_1/100$-unit change in $Y$ |
| Log-log | $\ln Y=b_0+b_1\ln X+\varepsilon$ | 1% increase in $X$ $\Rightarrow$ approximately $b_1\%$ change in $Y$ |

| 形式 | 模型 | 斜率解释 |
|---|---|---|
| 线性—线性 | $Y=b_0+b_1X+\varepsilon$ | $X$ 增加 1 单位 $\Rightarrow$ $Y$ 改变 $b_1$ 单位 |
| 对数—线性 | $\ln Y=b_0+b_1X+\varepsilon$ | $X$ 增加 1 单位 $\Rightarrow$ $Y$ 约改变 $100b_1\%$ |
| 线性—对数 | $Y=b_0+b_1\ln X+\varepsilon$ | $X$ 增加 1% $\Rightarrow$ $Y$ 约改变 $b_1/100$ 单位 |
| 对数—对数 | $\ln Y=b_0+b_1\ln X+\varepsilon$ | $X$ 增加 1% $\Rightarrow$ $Y$ 约改变 $b_1\%$ |

## Log-Lin Forecast Back-Transformation | Log-Lin 预测的反变换

If the fitted model is:

若拟合模型为：

$$
\ln\widehat{Y}=\widehat{b}_0+\widehat{b}_1X
$$

then convert back to the level of $Y$ using:

则应使用以下公式还原到 $Y$ 的水平值：

$$
\widehat{Y}=e^{\widehat{b}_0+\widehat{b}_1X}
$$

Example | 示例：

$$
\ln\widehat{Y}=-7+2(2.5)=-2
$$

$$
\widehat{Y}=e^{-2}=0.135335
$$

## Elasticity | 弹性

In a log-log model, the slope is elasticity: the percentage change in $Y$ associated with a 1% change in $X$.

在 log-log 模型中，斜率就是弹性：$X$ 改变 1% 时，$Y$ 的百分比变动。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Identify which variable is logged before interpreting the slope.  
  解释斜率前，先判断哪个变量取了对数。
- A predicted $\ln Y$ must be exponentiated to obtain predicted $Y$.  
  预测的 $\ln Y$ 必须取指数才能得到预测的 $Y$。
- Do not directly compare $R^2$, $F$, or $s_e$ across models whose dependent variables are on different scales, such as $Y$ versus $\ln Y$.  
  当模型因变量尺度不同（例如 $Y$ 与 $\ln Y$）时，不应直接比较 $R^2$、$F$ 或 $s_e$。

# 13. Selecting and Validating the Model | 模型选择与验证

## Selection Criteria | 选择标准

Evaluate the model using several complementary checks:

应使用多项互补检查评估模型：

- Higher $R^2$ when models have the same dependent-variable scale.  
  当因变量尺度相同时，较高的 $R^2$ 较好。
- Larger statistically significant $F$ statistic.  
  统计显著且较大的 $F$ 统计量。
- Lower standard error of estimate when measured on the same dependent-variable scale.  
  当因变量尺度相同时，较低的估计标准误。
- Random residuals with stable spread and approximate normality.  
  残差随机、离散程度稳定且近似正态。
- Economic logic and plausible out-of-sample behavior.  
  符合经济逻辑，并具有合理的样本外表现。

## Functional-Form Example | 函数形式示例

For operating profit margin regressed on unit sales, replacing $X$ with $\ln X$ raised $R^2$ from $55.10\%$ to $97.17\%$ and lowered $s_e$ from $2.2528$ to $0.5629$. Because $Y$ stayed on the same scale, these measures can be directly compared and favor the lin-log model.

在营业利润率对销量的回归中，将 $X$ 替换为 $\ln X$ 后，$R^2$ 从 $55.10\%$ 升至 $97.17\%$，$s_e$ 从 $2.2528$ 降至 $0.5629$。由于 $Y$ 的尺度不变，可直接比较这些指标，结果支持 lin-log 模型。

## Data-Quality Warning | 数据质量警示

One incorrect extreme observation in the source example raised correlation from $0.824$ to $0.996$ and $R^2$ from $0.6784$ to $0.9921$. Apparent fit can therefore be driven by a data error or influential outlier.

材料示例中，一个错误的极端观测值使相关系数从 $0.824$ 升至 $0.996$，$R^2$ 从 $0.6784$ 升至 $0.9921$。因此，表面上的高拟合度可能由数据错误或高影响异常值驱动。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

The “best” model is not the one with the highest fit statistic alone. Confirm assumptions, residual behavior, data quality, coefficient meaning, and economic plausibility.

“最佳”模型不能只看最高拟合指标，还必须确认假设、残差表现、数据质量、系数含义及经济合理性。

# CFA Memory Points | CFA 记忆重点

## 1. OLS Coefficients | OLS 系数

$$
\widehat{b}_1=\frac{\sum(X_i-\bar{X})(Y_i-\bar{Y})}{\sum(X_i-\bar{X})^2}
$$

$$
\widehat{b}_0=\bar{Y}-\widehat{b}_1\bar{X}
$$

Slope is change in predicted $Y$ per one-unit change in $X$; intercept is predicted $Y$ at $X=0$.

斜率是 $X$ 每改变一个单位时预测 $Y$ 的变动；截距是 $X=0$ 时预测的 $Y$。

## 2. Variation and Fit | 变异与拟合

$$
SST=SSR+SSE
$$

$$
R^2=\frac{SSR}{SST}=1-\frac{SSE}{SST}
$$

$$
s_e=\sqrt{\frac{SSE}{n-2}}
$$

Remember: total = explained + unexplained.

记忆：总变异 = 已解释变异 + 未解释变异。

## 3. Test Relationships | 检验关系

$$
t=\frac{\widehat{b}_1-B_1}{s_{\widehat{b}_1}}
$$

$$
F=\frac{MSR}{MSE}
$$

For a zero-slope test in simple regression, $F=t^2$ and $df_t=n-2$.

简单回归的零斜率检验中，$F=t^2$，且 $t$ 检验自由度为 $n-2$。

## 4. Four Assumptions | 四项假设

**L-H-I-N: Linearity, Homoskedasticity, Independence, Normality of residuals.**

**L-H-I-N：线性、同方差、独立性、残差正态性。**

## 5. Forecast | 预测

$$
\widehat{Y}_f=\widehat{b}_0+\widehat{b}_1X_f
$$

$$
s_f=s_e\sqrt{1+\frac{1}{n}+\frac{(X_f-\bar{X})^2}{S_{XX}}}
$$

Forecast uncertainty is smallest near $\bar{X}$ and rises as $X_f$ moves away from the sample center.

预测不确定性在 $\bar{X}$ 附近最小，并随 $X_f$ 远离样本中心而上升。

## 6. Functional-Form Shortcut | 函数形式技巧

- Lin–lin: units to units.  
  线性—线性：单位对单位。
- Log–lin: one unit in $X$ to percent change in $Y$.  
  对数—线性：$X$ 一单位对应 $Y$ 的百分比变动。
- Lin–log: 1% in $X$ to unit change in $Y$.  
  线性—对数：$X$ 变动 1% 对应 $Y$ 的单位变动。
- Log–log: percent to percent; slope is elasticity.  
  对数—对数：百分比对百分比；斜率为弹性。
