# 1. Portfolio Expected Return | 投资组合期望收益

## Definition | 定义

Portfolio expected return is the weighted average of the expected returns of its component assets. The weights are each asset's proportion of total portfolio value.

投资组合期望收益是各组成资产期望收益的加权平均数，权重为各资产占投资组合总价值的比例。

## Formula | 公式

$$
E(R_p)=\sum_{i=1}^{n}w_iE(R_i)
$$

$$
\sum_{i=1}^{n}w_i=1
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $E(R_p)$ | Expected portfolio return | 投资组合期望收益 |
| $w_i$ | Portfolio weight of asset $i$ | 资产 $i$ 的投资组合权重 |
| $E(R_i)$ | Expected return of asset $i$ | 资产 $i$ 的期望收益 |
| $n$ | Number of assets | 资产数量 |

## Example | 例题

Given | 已知：

| Asset | Weight | Expected Return |
|---|---:|---:|
| S&P 500 | 0.50 | 13% |
| US long-term corporate bonds | 0.25 | 6% |
| MSCI EAFE | 0.25 | 15% |

Calculation | 计算：

$$
E(R_p)=0.50(13\%)+0.25(6\%)+0.25(15\%)
$$

Answer | 答案：

$$
E(R_p)=11.75\%
$$

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Expected return depends on asset weights and expected returns, not on covariance or correlation.  
  期望收益只取决于权重和各资产的期望收益，与协方差或相关系数无关。
- Use consistent percentage or decimal inputs.  
  百分比或小数均可，但必须保持单位一致。

# 2. Variance and Standard Deviation | 方差与标准差

## Definition | 定义

Variance measures expected squared deviations from the mean; standard deviation is its square root. Both upside and downside deviations increase variance, so it is a symmetric risk measure.

方差衡量收益偏离均值的平方期望；标准差是方差的平方根。高于和低于均值的波动都会增加方差，因此它是对称风险指标。

## Formula | 公式

$$
\mathrm{Var}(R_p)=\sigma_p^2=E[(R_p-E(R_p))^2]
$$

$$
\sigma_p=\sqrt{\mathrm{Var}(R_p)}
$$

## Interpretation | 解释

Higher variance or standard deviation means greater return uncertainty. Standard deviation has the same unit as return and is therefore easier to interpret.

方差或标准差越高，收益的不确定性越大。标准差与收益率单位相同，因此更便于解释。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Variance is expressed in squared units; standard deviation is expressed in return units.  
  方差采用平方单位，标准差采用收益率单位。
- Do not confuse portfolio variance with the weighted average of individual asset variances; covariance terms must also be included.  
  投资组合方差不是个别资产方差的加权平均，还必须计入协方差项。

# 3. Covariance | 协方差

## Definition | 定义

Covariance measures the linear co-movement of two returns. It is the probability-weighted average of the products of each return's deviation from its expected value.

协方差衡量两个收益率的线性共同变动，是两项收益分别偏离其期望值后的乘积的概率加权平均。

## Formula | 公式

Population covariance | 总体协方差：

$$
\mathrm{Cov}(R_i,R_j)=E[(R_i-E(R_i))(R_j-E(R_j))]
$$

Sample covariance | 样本协方差：

$$
s_{ij}=\frac{\sum_{t=1}^{T}(R_{i,t}-\bar{R}_i)(R_{j,t}-\bar{R}_j)}{T-1}
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $R_i,R_j$ | Returns on assets $i$ and $j$ | 资产 $i$ 和 $j$ 的收益率 |
| $T$ | Number of historical observations | 历史观测值数量 |
| $\bar{R}_i,\bar{R}_j$ | Sample mean returns | 样本平均收益率 |
| $s_{ij}$ | Sample covariance | 样本协方差 |

## Interpretation | 解释

| Covariance | Return Relationship | 收益关系 |
|---:|---|---|
| $>0$ | Returns tend to be above or below their means together | 两项收益倾向于同时高于或低于各自均值 |
| $=0$ | No linear relationship | 不存在线性关系 |
| $<0$ | One return tends to be above its mean when the other is below | 一项收益高于均值时，另一项倾向于低于均值 |

Own covariance equals variance:

自身协方差等于方差：

$$
\mathrm{Cov}(R_i,R_i)=\mathrm{Var}(R_i)=\sigma_i^2
$$

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- The order does not matter: $\mathrm{Cov}(R_i,R_j)=\mathrm{Cov}(R_j,R_i)$.  
  协方差与变量顺序无关。
- A positive covariance indicates a positive relationship, not low risk.  
  正协方差表示正向关系，不表示风险较低。
- Zero covariance means no linear relationship; it does not necessarily imply independence.  
  协方差为零仅表示无线性关系，不一定意味着相互独立。

# 4. Portfolio Variance and Diversification | 投资组合方差与分散化

## Formula | 公式

General $n$-asset portfolio | 一般 $n$ 资产投资组合：

$$
\sigma_p^2=\sum_{i=1}^{n}\sum_{j=1}^{n}w_iw_j\mathrm{Cov}(R_i,R_j)
$$

Two-asset portfolio | 两资产投资组合：

$$
\sigma_p^2=w_1^2\sigma_1^2+w_2^2\sigma_2^2+2w_1w_2\mathrm{Cov}(R_1,R_2)
$$

Using correlation | 使用相关系数：

$$
\sigma_p^2=w_1^2\sigma_1^2+w_2^2\sigma_2^2+2w_1w_2\rho_{12}\sigma_1\sigma_2
$$

## Covariance Matrix | 协方差矩阵

- An $n$-asset covariance matrix contains $n^2$ entries.  
  $n$ 资产协方差矩阵共有 $n^2$ 个元素。
- It contains $n$ variances on the diagonal and $n(n-1)$ off-diagonal covariance entries.  
  对角线上有 $n$ 个方差，非对角线上有 $n(n-1)$ 个协方差元素。
- Because the matrix is symmetric, only the following number of distinct covariances must be estimated:  
  因矩阵对称，只需估计以下数量的不同协方差：

$$
\frac{n(n-1)}{2}
$$

## Interpretation | 解释

Lower covariance or correlation reduces portfolio variance without changing expected return, all else equal. This is the diversification benefit.

其他条件不变时，较低的协方差或相关系数可降低投资组合方差，却不会改变期望收益，这就是分散化收益。

## Worked Example | 计算示例

Given | 已知：

| Stock | Weight | Expected Return | Standard Deviation |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| 1 | 0.40 | 4% | 6% |
| 2 | 0.60 | 8% | 15% |

Correlation $\rho_{12}=0.30$.

Calculation | 计算：

$$
\mathrm{Cov}(R_1,R_2)=0.30(0.06)(0.15)=0.0027
$$

$$
E(R_p)=0.40(4\%)+0.60(8\%)=6.40\%
$$

$$
\sigma_p^2=(0.40)^2(0.06)^2+(0.60)^2(0.15)^2+2(0.40)(0.60)(0.0027)
$$

$$
\sigma_p^2=0.009972
$$

Answer | 答案：

$$
\sigma_p=\sqrt{0.009972}=9.99\%
$$

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Count each distinct covariance once, but the two-asset variance formula includes the covariance contribution twice through the factor $2$.  
  每个不同协方差只估计一次，但两资产方差公式通过系数 $2$ 将其贡献计入两次。
- As correlation approaches $+1$, diversification benefits decrease.  
  相关系数越接近 $+1$，分散化收益越低。
- Negative covariance produces greater risk reduction than zero covariance.  
  负协方差比零协方差带来更大的风险降低。

# 5. Correlation | 相关系数

## Definition | 定义

Correlation is a standardized, unit-free measure of linear association. It ranges from $-1$ to $+1$.

相关系数是标准化、无量纲的线性关系指标，取值范围为 $-1$ 至 $+1$。

## Formula | 公式

$$
\rho_{ij}=\frac{\mathrm{Cov}(R_i,R_j)}{\sigma_i\sigma_j}
$$

$$
\mathrm{Cov}(R_i,R_j)=\rho_{ij}\sigma_i\sigma_j
$$

## Interpretation | 解释

| Correlation | Interpretation | 中文解释 |
|---:|---|---|
| Near $+1$ | Strong positive linear relationship | 强正线性关系 |
| Near $0$ | Weak linear relationship | 弱线性关系 |
| Near $-1$ | Strong negative linear relationship | 强负线性关系 |

The strength of a linear relationship depends on $|\rho|$, not its sign. For example, $-0.67$ is stronger than $+0.33$.

线性关系的强弱取决于 $|\rho|$，而不是符号。例如，$-0.67$ 的线性关系强于 $+0.33$。

## Example | 例题

Given $\mathrm{Cov}(R_B,R_E)=38\%^2$, $\sigma_B=9\%$, and $\sigma_E=21\%$:

已知 $\mathrm{Cov}(R_B,R_E)=38\%^2$、$\sigma_B=9\%$、$\sigma_E=21\%$：

$$
\rho_{BE}=\frac{38}{(9)(21)}=0.201\approx0.20
$$

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Correlation is unit-free; covariance is not.  
  相关系数无量纲，协方差有单位。
- The diagonal of a correlation matrix contains $1$s because an asset is perfectly correlated with itself.  
  相关矩阵对角线均为 $1$，因为资产与自身完全相关。
- Compare relationship strength using absolute correlation.  
  比较关系强弱时，应比较相关系数的绝对值。

# 6. Covariance from a Joint Probability Function | 使用联合概率函数计算协方差

## Definition | 定义

A joint probability function $P(R_A,R_B)$ gives the probability that specified values of $R_A$ and $R_B$ occur together.

联合概率函数 $P(R_A,R_B)$ 表示 $R_A$ 与 $R_B$ 的特定取值同时发生的概率。

## Formula | 公式

$$
E(R_A)=\sum_i\sum_j P(R_{A,i},R_{B,j})R_{A,i}
$$

$$
E(R_B)=\sum_i\sum_j P(R_{A,i},R_{B,j})R_{B,j}
$$

$$
\mathrm{Cov}(R_A,R_B)=\sum_i\sum_j P(R_{A,i},R_{B,j})[R_{A,i}-E(R_A)][R_{B,j}-E(R_B)]
$$

## Worked Example | 计算示例

Given | 已知：

| Condition | Probability | $R_A$ | $R_B$ |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Good | 0.20 | 25% | 20% |
| Average | 0.50 | 12% | 16% |
| Poor | 0.30 | 10% | 10% |

Expected returns | 期望收益：

$$
E(R_A)=0.20(25\%)+0.50(12\%)+0.30(10\%)=14\%
$$

$$
E(R_B)=0.20(20\%)+0.50(16\%)+0.30(10\%)=15\%
$$

Covariance calculation | 协方差计算：

| Condition | $(R_A-14)(R_B-15)$ | Probability-Weighted Product |
|---|---:|---:|
| Good | $(25-14)(20-15)=55$ | $0.20(55)=11$ |
| Average | $(12-14)(16-15)=-2$ | $0.50(-2)=-1$ |
| Poor | $(10-14)(10-15)=20$ | $0.30(20)=6$ |

Answer | 答案：

$$
\mathrm{Cov}(R_A,R_B)=11-1+6=16\%^2
$$

## Independence and Uncorrelatedness | 独立与不相关

If $X$ and $Y$ are independent:

若 $X$ 与 $Y$ 相互独立：

$$
P(X,Y)=P(X)P(Y)
$$

Independence implies zero covariance, but zero covariance does not generally imply independence. For uncorrelated variables:

独立必然意味着协方差为零，但协方差为零通常不能反推独立。对于不相关变量：

$$
E(XY)=E(X)E(Y)
$$

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Weight every deviation cross-product by its joint probability, then sum.  
  每个偏差乘积都要乘以对应的联合概率，再求和。
- Independence is stronger than uncorrelatedness because correlation captures only linear association.  
  独立比不相关更强，因为相关系数只衡量线性关系。

# 7. Mean–Variance Analysis | 均值—方差分析

## Definition | 定义

Mean–variance analysis evaluates reward using expected return and risk using variance. It holds exactly for risk-averse expected-utility investors when returns are normally distributed or utility is quadratic; it may remain approximately useful when these assumptions are violated.

均值—方差分析用期望收益衡量回报、用方差衡量风险。对于以期望效用最大化为目标的风险厌恶投资者，当收益服从正态分布或效用函数为二次函数时，该方法严格成立；假设不完全满足时仍可能近似有效。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

Standard deviation treats favorable and unfavorable deviations symmetrically. When only downside risk matters, shortfall-based measures may be more appropriate.

标准差对有利和不利偏差一视同仁。若投资者只关心下行风险，缺口风险指标可能更合适。

# 8. Shortfall Risk and Roy's Safety-First Criterion | 缺口风险与 Roy 安全第一准则

## Definition | 定义

Shortfall risk is the risk that portfolio return or value falls below a minimum acceptable level over a specified horizon. Roy's safety-first criterion selects the portfolio that minimizes $P(R_p<R_L)$.

缺口风险是指在特定期间内，投资组合收益或价值低于最低可接受水平的风险。Roy 安全第一准则选择使 $P(R_p<R_L)$ 最小的投资组合。

## Formula | 公式

$$
\mathrm{SFRatio}=\frac{E(R_p)-R_L}{\sigma_p}
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $R_L$ | Minimum acceptable return or shortfall level | 最低可接受收益率或缺口水平 |
| $E(R_p)-R_L$ | Distance from expected return to the threshold | 期望收益与门槛收益之间的距离 |
| $\sigma_p$ | Portfolio standard deviation | 投资组合标准差 |

If returns are normally distributed:

若收益服从正态分布：

$$
P(R_p<R_L)=\Phi(-\mathrm{SFRatio})
$$

## Decision Rule | 决策规则

1. Calculate the safety-first ratio for every portfolio.  
   计算每个投资组合的安全第一比率。
2. Choose the portfolio with the highest ratio.  
   选择安全第一比率最高的投资组合。

Higher SFRatio $\Rightarrow$ Lower shortfall probability $\Rightarrow$ Better under Roy's criterion.

安全第一比率越高 $\Rightarrow$ 缺口概率越低 $\Rightarrow$ 按 Roy 准则越优。

## Worked Example 1 | 计算示例 1

Given $R_L=2\%$:

| Portfolio | $E(R_p)$ | $\sigma_p$ | SFRatio |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| 1 | 12% | 15% | $(12-2)/15=0.667$ |
| 2 | 14% | 16% | $(14-2)/16=0.750$ |

Portfolio 2 is optimal because it has the higher SFRatio.

投资组合 2 的安全第一比率更高，因此最优。

$$
P(R_2<2\%)=\Phi(-0.75)=1-0.7734=22.66\%\approx23\%
$$

## Worked Example 2: Required Withdrawal | 计算示例 2：计划提款

A CAD800,000 portfolio must fund a CAD30,000 withdrawal without reducing initial principal.

一个 CAD800,000 的投资组合需支付 CAD30,000，同时不侵蚀初始本金。

$$
R_L=\frac{30{,}000}{800{,}000}=3.75\%
$$

| Allocation | $E(R_p)$ | $\sigma_p$ | SFRatio |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| A | 25% | 27% | 0.7870 |
| B | 11% | 8% | **0.9063** |
| C | 14% | 20% | 0.5125 |

Allocation B is safety-first optimal.

配置 B 的安全第一比率最高，因此最优。

$$
P(R_B<3.75\%)=\Phi(-0.9063)\approx18.2\%
$$

## Relationship to the Sharpe Ratio | 与夏普比率的关系

When $R_L$ equals the risk-free rate $R_f$, the safety-first ratio becomes the Sharpe ratio:

当 $R_L$ 等于无风险利率 $R_f$ 时，安全第一比率等于夏普比率：

$$
\frac{E(R_p)-R_f}{\sigma_p}
$$

Under normality, maximizing the Sharpe ratio minimizes the probability that portfolio return falls below the risk-free rate.

在正态分布假设下，最大化夏普比率等同于最小化投资组合收益低于无风险利率的概率。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- For a planned withdrawal without invading principal, calculate $R_L$ as withdrawal divided by beginning portfolio value.  
  若提款后不得侵蚀本金，则 $R_L=$ 提款额 $\div$ 期初投资组合价值。
- Do not automatically choose the portfolio with the highest expected return or lowest standard deviation; choose the highest SFRatio.  
  不要机械选择期望收益最高或标准差最低的组合，应选择安全第一比率最高者。
- The normality assumption is required to convert the SFRatio into an exact shortfall probability.  
  将安全第一比率转换为精确缺口概率时，需要正态分布假设。

# 9. VaR, Stress Testing, and Scenario Analysis | VaR、压力测试与情景分析

## Definitions | 定义

Value at risk (VaR) is a money measure of the minimum loss expected over a specified horizon at a stated tail probability. A 95% one-day VaR of EUR5 million means a 5% probability of losing EUR5 million or more in one day, assuming the model is correct.

风险价值（VaR）是在给定尾部概率和持有期下，预计损失至少达到某一金额的风险指标。95% 的单日 VaR 为 EUR5 million，表示在模型正确的前提下，单日损失达到或超过 EUR5 million 的概率为 5%。

Stress testing estimates losses under rare and extremely unfavorable combinations of events. Scenario analysis evaluates outcomes under a base case and alternative favorable or adverse scenarios.

压力测试估计罕见且极端不利事件组合下的损失；情景分析比较基准情景与其他有利或不利情景下的结果。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

The variance–covariance approach to VaR assumes normally distributed returns, while stress testing is designed to examine extreme scenarios that may not be captured well by normal models.

VaR 的方差—协方差法假设收益服从正态分布；压力测试则用于考察正态模型可能无法充分捕捉的极端情景。

# CFA Memory Points | CFA 记忆重点

## 1. Expected return | 期望收益

$$
E(R_p)=\sum_{i=1}^{n}w_iE(R_i)
$$

## 2. Portfolio variance | 投资组合方差

$$
\sigma_p^2=\sum_{i=1}^{n}\sum_{j=1}^{n}w_iw_j\mathrm{Cov}(R_i,R_j)
$$

## 3. Covariance–correlation conversion | 协方差与相关系数转换

$$
\mathrm{Cov}(R_i,R_j)=\rho_{ij}\sigma_i\sigma_j
$$

## 4. Distinct covariance count | 不同协方差数量

$$
\frac{n(n-1)}{2}
$$

## 5. Diversification relationship | 分散化关系

Lower correlation $\Rightarrow$ lower portfolio risk, with expected return unchanged.

相关系数越低 $\Rightarrow$ 投资组合风险越低，而期望收益不变。

## 6. Common mistake | 常见错误

Zero correlation does not imply independence; independence does imply zero correlation when variances exist.

零相关不代表独立；在方差存在时，独立会推出零相关。

## 7. Safety-first rule | 安全第一准则

$$
\mathrm{SFRatio}=\frac{E(R_p)-R_L}{\sigma_p}
$$

Choose the **highest** SFRatio.

选择安全第一比率**最高**的投资组合。

## 8. Exam shortcut | 考试技巧

For relationship strength, compare $|\rho|$; for Roy's criterion, rank SFRatios before calculating any normal probability.

比较关系强度时看 $|\rho|$；应用 Roy 准则时，先比较安全第一比率，通常无需先计算正态概率。
