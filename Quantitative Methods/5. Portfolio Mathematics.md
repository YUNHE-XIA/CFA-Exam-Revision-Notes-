# 1. Portfolio Expected Return | 投资组合期望收益

## Concept | 概念

Portfolio expected return is the weighted average of individual asset expected returns.

投资组合期望收益是各资产期望收益按照投资权重进行加权平均。

Formula:

$$
E(R_p)=w_1E(R_1)+w_2E(R_2)+...+w_nE(R_n)
$$

Where:

| Symbol | Meaning |
|---|---|
| $E(R_p)$ | Expected portfolio return 投资组合期望收益 |
| $w_i$ | Weight of asset i 资产权重 |
| $E(R_i)$ | Expected return of asset i 资产期望收益 |

---

# 2. Portfolio Variance | 投资组合方差

Portfolio variance measures the risk of a portfolio.

投资组合方差衡量投资组合收益的不确定性。

Definition:

$$
\sigma^2(R_p)=E[(R_p-E(R_p))^2]
$$

Portfolio risk depends on:

1. Individual asset risk  
2. Relationship between assets  

投资组合风险取决于：

1. 单个资产波动  
2. 资产之间共同变化关系

---

# 3. Covariance | 协方差

## Definition | 定义

Covariance measures how two asset returns move together.

协方差衡量两个资产收益是否共同变化。

Formula:

$$
Cov(R_i,R_j)
=
E[(R_i-E(R_i))(R_j-E(R_j))]
$$

---

## Interpretation | 解释

| Covariance | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Positive | Assets move in the same direction 同方向变化 |
| Negative | Assets move in opposite directions 反方向变化 |
| Zero | No linear relationship 无明显关系 |

---

## CFA Point

The covariance of an asset with itself equals its variance.

资产自身的协方差等于方差：

$$
Cov(R,R)=\sigma^2(R)
$$

---

# 4. Two-Asset Portfolio Variance | 两资产投资组合方差

Formula:

$$
\sigma_p^2
=
w_1^2\sigma_1^2
+
w_2^2\sigma_2^2
+
2w_1w_2Cov(R_1,R_2)
$$

Using correlation:

$$
Cov(R_1,R_2)
=
\rho_{12}\sigma_1\sigma_2
$$

Therefore:

$$
\sigma_p^2
=
w_1^2\sigma_1^2
+
w_2^2\sigma_2^2
+
2w_1w_2\rho_{12}\sigma_1\sigma_2
$$

---

# 5. Diversification Effect | 分散化效果

Portfolio risk decreases when covariance decreases.

当资产之间相关性降低，组合风险下降。

| Relationship | Diversification Benefit |
|---|---|
| Positive correlation | Low benefit 分散效果较低 |
| Zero correlation | Moderate benefit 有一定效果 |
| Negative correlation | Strong benefit 分散效果强 |

Key idea:

> Diversification reduces risk without necessarily reducing expected return.

分散化可以降低风险，同时保持期望收益。

---

# 6. Multiple Asset Portfolio Variance | 多资产组合方差

General formula:

$$
\sigma_p^2
=
\sum_{i=1}^{n}
\sum_{j=1}^{n}
w_iw_jCov(R_i,R_j)
$$

Number of unique covariance terms:

$$
\frac{n(n-1)}{2}
$$

Example:

For 5 assets:

$$
\frac{5(5-1)}{2}=10
$$

---

# 7. Covariance Matrix | 协方差矩阵

Example:

| | Asset A | Asset B | Asset C |
|-|-:|-:|-:|
| Asset A | Variance A | Cov(A,B) | Cov(A,C) |
| Asset B | Cov(B,A) | Variance B | Cov(B,C) |
| Asset C | Cov(C,A) | Cov(C,B) | Variance C |

Important:

$$
Cov(A,B)=Cov(B,A)
$$

The covariance matrix is symmetric.

协方差矩阵具有对称性。

---

# 8. Correlation | 相关系数

## Definition | 定义

Correlation standardizes covariance.

相关系数是标准化后的协方差。

Formula:

$$
\rho_{ij}
=
\frac{Cov(R_i,R_j)}
{\sigma_i\sigma_j}
$$

---

## Range | 范围

$$
-1 \leq \rho_{ij} \leq 1
$$

---

## Interpretation | 解释

| Correlation | Meaning |
|---|---|
| +1 | Perfect positive relationship 完全正相关 |
| 0 | No linear relationship 无线性关系 |
| -1 | Perfect negative relationship 完全负相关 |

---

Relationship:

$$
Cov(R_i,R_j)
=
\rho_{ij}\sigma_i\sigma_j
$$

---

# 9. Joint Probability Function | 联合概率函数

## Definition

Joint probability represents the probability of two events occurring together.

联合概率表示两个事件同时发生的概率。

Example:

$$
P(X=3,Y=2)
$$

means:

X equals 3 and Y equals 2 at the same time.

---

# 10. Covariance Using Joint Probability | 使用联合概率计算协方差

Formula:

$$
Cov(R_A,R_B)
=
\sum P(R_A,R_B)
(R_A-E(R_A))
(R_B-E(R_B))
$$

Steps:

1. Calculate expected return of each asset  
2. Calculate deviations from expected return  
3. Multiply deviations  
4. Multiply by joint probability  
5. Sum all values  

---

# 11. Independent Variables | 独立变量

Two variables are independent if:

$$
P(X,Y)=P(X)P(Y)
$$

For independent variables:

$$
E(XY)=E(X)E(Y)
$$

---

# 12. Shortfall Risk | 下行风险

## Definition

Shortfall risk is the probability that portfolio return falls below a minimum acceptable level.

下行风险是投资组合收益低于最低可接受收益率的概率。

Formula:

$$
P(R_p<R_L)
$$

Where:

| Symbol | Meaning |
|---|---|
| $R_p$ | Portfolio return 投资组合收益 |
| $R_L$ | Minimum acceptable return 最低目标收益 |

---

# 13. Safety-First Ratio | 安全第一比率

Roy's safety-first criterion selects the portfolio with the highest safety-first ratio.

Roy安全第一原则选择安全第一比率最高的组合。

Formula:

$$
SF Ratio
=
\frac{E(R_p)-R_L}{\sigma_p}
$$

Where:

| Symbol | Meaning |
|---|---|
| $E(R_p)$ | Expected portfolio return |
| $R_L$ | Minimum acceptable return |
| $\sigma_p$ | Portfolio standard deviation |

---

## Decision Rule | 判断规则

Choose:

$$
\text{Highest Safety-First Ratio}
$$

Higher ratio means:

- Higher expected return
- Lower downside probability

---

# 14. Relationship with Sharpe Ratio | 与夏普比率关系

When:

$$
R_L=R_f
$$

Safety-first ratio becomes:

$$
SF Ratio
=
\frac{E(R_p)-R_f}{\sigma_p}
$$

which equals:

$$
Sharpe\ Ratio
$$

---

# 15. Normal Distribution Application | 正态分布应用

If portfolio returns follow a normal distribution:

$$
P(R_p<R_L)=N(-SF Ratio)
$$

Higher safety-first ratio:

↓

Lower probability of shortfall
