# Time Value of Money in Finance | 金融中的货币时间价值

## 1. 核心记忆

本章主要把 time value of money 应用到金融资产定价。

核心问题：

- Bond price 如何由未来现金流折现得到；
- Stock price 如何由未来 dividends 折现得到；
- 已知 price 时，如何反推出 implied return；
- 已知 stock price 和 required return 时，如何反推出 implied growth；
- Cash flow additivity 如何支持 no-arbitrage pricing。

核心关键词：

- Present Value
- Future Value
- Bond Pricing
- Yield to Maturity
- Dividend Discount Model
- Constant Growth Model
- Implied Return
- Implied Growth
- Cash Flow Additivity
- No Arbitrage
- Forward Rate
- Forward Exchange Rate
- Option Replication

---

## 2. Present Value and Future Value

Future value:

$$
FV_t = PV(1+r)^t
$$

Present value:

$$
PV = \frac{FV_t}{(1+r)^t}
$$

连续复利：

$$
FV_t = PV e^{rt}
$$

$$
PV = FV e^{-rt}
$$

记忆：

> Future cash flow 越晚收到，present value 越低。  
> Discount rate 越高，present value 越低。

---

## 3. Discount Bond / Zero-Coupon Bond

Discount bond 没有中间 coupon，只在 maturity 收回 face value。

价格公式：

$$
PV = {FV}{(1+r)^t}
$$

特点：

- no coupon payments；
- issued at discount；
- return comes from price increasing toward par value；
- also called zero-coupon bond or pure discount bond。

记忆：

> Zero-coupon bond 的价格就是未来 par value 的现值。

---

## 4. Coupon Bond

Coupon bond 的价格等于所有 coupon 和 par value 的现值之和。

$$
PV = \frac{PMT_1}{(1+r)^1}
+ \frac{PMT_2}{(1+r)^2}
+ \cdots
+ \frac{PMT_N + FV}{(1+r)^N}
$$

记忆：

> Bond price = PV of coupons + PV of principal。

如果 coupon rate = YTM：

> Bond trades at par。

如果 coupon rate > YTM：

> Bond trades at premium。

如果 coupon rate < YTM：

> Bond trades at discount。

---

## 5. Perpetual Bond

Perpetual bond 没有 maturity，永久支付固定 coupon。

$$
PV = \frac{PMT}{r}
$$

记忆：

> Perpetual bond = fixed coupon perpetuity。

---

## 6. Annuity / Level Payment Instrument

Annuity 是固定期限内的 level payments。

常见例子：

- mortgage；
- amortizing loan；
- fixed payment loan。

特点：

> 每期 payment 包含 interest 和 principal repayment。

记忆：

> Early payments contain more interest.  
> Later payments contain more principal.

---

## 7. Equity Valuation

Stock valuation 的核心：

> Stock price should equal the present value of expected future dividends.

Equity 没有固定 maturity，所以通常视为 perpetuity。

常见 dividend assumptions：

- no growth；
- constant growth；
- changing growth。

---

## 8. No-Growth Dividend Model

如果 dividend 永远固定：

$$
P_0 = \frac{D}{r}
$$

适合：

- preferred stock；
- stable no-growth company；
- perpetual constant dividend。

记忆：

> No-growth stock is valued like a perpetuity。

---

## 9. Constant Growth Dividend Model

如果 dividend 以固定速度增长：

$$
D_1 = D_0(1+g)
$$

$$
P_0 = \frac{D_1}{r-g}
$$

条件：

$$
r > g
$$

记忆：

> Gordon Growth Model: next dividend divided by required return minus growth。

考试陷阱：

> 用的是 next dividend, D1，不是 D0。

---

## 10. Changing Growth / Two-Stage Dividend Model

Two-stage model 适合：

- early high growth；
- later stable growth；
- company matures over time。

基本逻辑：

1. Forecast dividends during high-growth period；
2. Calculate terminal value at the start of stable growth；
3. Discount all cash flows back to today。

Terminal value:

$$
P_n = \frac{D_{n+1}}{r-g_L}
$$

Current value:

$$
P_0 =
\sum_{t=1}^{n}\frac{D_t}{(1+r)^t}
+
\frac{P_n}{(1+r)^n}
$$

记忆：

> Two-stage DDM = PV of short-term dividends + PV of terminal value。

---

## 11. Implied Return for Bonds

如果 bond price 和 future cash flows 已知，可以反推出 implied return，也就是 YTM。

Discount bond:

$$
r = \left(\frac{FV}{PV}\right)^{1/t} - 1
$$

Coupon bond:

> YTM is the discount rate that makes PV of all promised cash flows equal to current bond price。

记忆：

> Bond YTM = implied return if all promised cash flows are received and reinvested at the same YTM。

---

## 12. Implied Return for Stocks

在 constant growth model 下：

$$
P_0 = \frac{D_1}{r-g}
$$

可以变形为：

$$
r = \frac{D_1}{P_0} + g
$$

记忆：

> Required return = dividend yield + growth rate。

---

## 13. Implied Growth for Stocks

同样由 constant growth model 变形：

$$
g = r - \frac{D_1}{P_0}
$$

记忆：

> Implied growth = required return - dividend yield。

如果 implied growth 过高：

> stock may be overvalued。

如果 implied growth 低于合理预期：

> stock may be undervalued。

---

## 14. Price-to-Earnings Ratio

P/E ratio:

$$
P/E = \frac{Price}{Earnings\ per\ Share}
$$

Forward P/E:

$$
Forward\ P/E = \frac{P_0}{E_1}
$$

在 constant growth framework 下：

$$
Forward\ P/E = \frac{Dividend\ Payout\ Ratio}{r-g}
$$

记忆：

> Forward P/E increases when payout ratio increases or growth increases。  
> Forward P/E decreases when required return increases。

---

## 15. Cash Flow Additivity

Cash flow additivity principle:

> Dollar amounts indexed at the same point in time are additive。

意思是：

> 同一时间点的现金流可以直接相加。

例子：

如果两个资产在所有时间点产生完全相同的 cash flows，它们今天的价格必须相同。

否则会产生 arbitrage。

---

## 16. No-Arbitrage Principle

No arbitrage means:

> Investors should not be able to earn risk-free profit with zero initial investment。

如果两个 strategy 的未来 cash flows 完全相同：

> 它们今天的 cost 必须相同。

记忆：

> Same future cash flows must have same present value。

---

## 17. Implied Forward Interest Rate

如果：

- one-year rate = \(r_1\)
- two-year rate = \(r_2\)
- one-year forward rate starting in one year = \(F_{1,1}\)

无套利条件：

$$
(1+r_2)^2 = (1+r_1)(1+F_{1,1})
$$

所以：

$$
F_{1,1} = \frac{(1+r_2)^2}{1+r_1} - 1
$$

记忆：

> Forward rate 是让 two-year investment 和 rolling one-year investments 无套利的 breakeven rate。

---

## 18. Forward Exchange Rate

Forward exchange rate 也来自 no-arbitrage。

逻辑：

1. 投资本国 risk-free asset；
2. 换成外币，投资外国 risk-free asset，再用 forward rate 换回来；
3. 两个 strategy 必须给出相同结果。

记忆：

> Currency forward rate is determined by interest rate differential。

利率高的货币通常：

> trades at a forward discount。

利率低的货币通常：

> trades at a forward premium。

---

## 19. Option Pricing and Replication

Option pricing 也可以用 cash flow additivity 和 no-arbitrage。

核心逻辑：

> 用 underlying asset 和 risk-free borrowing/lending 复制 option payoff。

如果 replicating portfolio 和 option 未来 payoff 一样：

> option price must equal cost of replicating portfolio。

记忆：

> Option value comes from the cost of replicating its future payoff。

---

## 20. Hedge Ratio

Hedge ratio 是复制 option payoff 时需要持有的 underlying asset 比例。

Call option hedge ratio:

$$
h = \frac{C_u - C_d}{S_u - S_d}
$$

其中：

- \(C_u\) = option value if stock goes up；
- \(C_d\) = option value if stock goes down；
- \(S_u\) = stock price if up；
- \(S_d\) = stock price if down。

记忆：

> Hedge ratio measures how much underlying asset is needed to replicate the option。

---

## 21. Exam Traps | 高频陷阱

- Bond price is PV of promised cash flows.
- Zero-coupon bond only has one future cash flow.
- Coupon bond includes coupons and principal.
- If coupon rate equals YTM, bond trades at par.
- Perpetual bond uses PMT / r.
- Stock value is PV of expected future dividends.
- Constant growth model uses D1, not D0.
- Gordon Growth Model requires r > g.
- Required return = dividend yield + growth.
- Implied growth = required return - dividend yield.
- Forward P/E is positively related to payout ratio and growth.
- Forward P/E is negatively related to required return.
- Cash flows at the same time can be added.
- Same cash flows must have same price.
- No-arbitrage pricing prevents risk-free profit.
- Forward rates are breakeven reinvestment rates.
- Option prices can be derived using replication.

## 22. Quick Review | 考前速记

- PV 把未来现金流折现到今天。
- Discount rate 越高，PV 越低。
- Zero-coupon bond price = PV of par value。
- Coupon bond price = PV of coupons + PV of par。
- Perpetual bond price = PMT / r。
- Stock price = PV of expected future dividends。
- No-growth stock = D / r。
- Constant-growth stock = D1 / (r - g)。
- Two-stage DDM = PV of high-growth dividends + PV of terminal value。
- Bond YTM 是由 price 和 promised cash flows 反推的 implied return。
- Stock required return = dividend yield + growth。
- Stock implied growth = required return - dividend yield。
- Forward P/E = payout ratio / (r - g)。
- Cash flow additivity = same-time cash flows can be added。
- No arbitrage = same future cash flows must have same current price。
- Forward rate = breakeven reinvestment rate。
- Option price can be found by replicating portfolio。
