# 1. Fintech and Quantitative Investment Analysis | Fintech 与量化投资分析

Fintech 是金融服务和产品在设计与交付方面的技术驱动创新. 该术语也可以指开发这些技术的公司或 fintech 行业.

# 2. Big Data | 大数据

## Definition | 定义

Big Data is the vast amount of information generated by traditional sources and non-traditional sources. Traditional sources include markets, companies, and governments. Non-traditional sources include electronic devices, social media, sensor networks, and company exhaust.

Big Data 是由传统来源和非传统来源产生的海量信息. 传统来源包括市场, 公司和政府. 非传统来源包括电子设备, 社交媒体, 传感器网络和 company exhaust.

## The Four Vs | 四个 V

| V | Meaning | 中文解释 |
|---|---|---|
| Volume | Extremely large quantities of records and data points | 数据量极大 |
| Velocity | High speed and frequency of recording and transmission | 数据记录和传输的速度与频率很高 |
| Variety | Many sources and formats | 数据来源和格式多样 |
| Veracity | Credibility and reliability of the data | 数据的可信度和可靠性 |

The first three Vs describe Big Data generally. Veracity becomes especially important when the data are used for inference or prediction.

前三个 V 用于描述 Big Data 的一般特征. 当数据用于推断或预测时, veracity 尤其重要.

## Data Structures | 数据结构

| Type | Characteristics | Examples | 中文记忆点 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Structured | Organized in tables with consistent fields | SQL tables, market records, transactions | 可用行和列组织, 字段类型一致 |
| Semistructured | Has features of both structured and unstructured data | HTML code | 同时具有结构化和非结构化特征 |
| Unstructured | Cannot readily be represented in tables | Text, email, audio, images, video, sensor data | 难以直接用表格表示, 通常需要专门处理 |

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Big Data includes both traditional and alternative data.
- Big Data 同时包括传统数据和另类数据.
- Alternative data are not necessarily unstructured. Business-process data often are structured.
- Alternative data 不一定是非结构化数据. Business-process data 通常是结构化数据.
- More data do not necessarily mean better data. Quantity cannot replace quality and suitability.
- 数据更多并不代表数据更好. 数据量不能替代质量和适用性.

# 3. Traditional Data and Alternative Data | 传统数据与另类数据

## Comparison | 对比

| Category | Sources and Examples | 中文解释 |
|---|---|---|

| Alternative data | Social media, electronic communications, web traffic, sensors, satellites, geolocation, and company exhaust | 社交媒体, 电子通信, 网站流量, 传感器, 卫星, 地理位置和 company exhaust |

Alternative data may provide more timely or additional insight into consumer behavior, company performance, agriculture, shipping, oil activity, market trends, and sentiment.

Alternative data 可能为消费者行为, 公司表现, 农业, 航运, 石油活动, 市场趋势和情绪提供更及时或额外的信息.

## Three Main Sources of Alternative Data | 另类数据的三类主要来源

| Source | Typical Structure | Examples | Investment Insight |
|---|---|---|---|
| Individuals | Often unstructured | Social media, news, reviews, web searches, clicks, email, photos, and audio | Preferences, behavior, and sentiment |
| Business processes | Often structured | Transactions, credit cards, supply chains, bank records, and point-of-sale data | Leading or real-time indicators of company activity |
| Sensors | Can be unstructured and extremely high volume | Satellites, geolocation, smartphones, cameras, RFID, and IoT devices | Traffic, shipping, manufacturing, agriculture, and physical activity |

| 来源 | 典型结构 | 示例 | 投资信息 |
|---|---|---|---|

| 业务流程 | 通常是结构化数据 | 交易, 信用卡, 供应链, 银行记录和销售点数据 | 公司活动的领先或实时指标 |

## Company Exhaust | 企业经营衍生数据

Company exhaust is information generated in the normal course of business. Examples include supply-chain information, banking records, and retail point-of-sale scanner data.

Company exhaust 是企业正常经营过程中产生的信息. 示例包括供应链信息, 银行记录和零售销售点扫描数据.

## Internet of Things | 物联网

The Internet of Things is a network of physical objects embedded with electronics, sensors, software, and network connections that allow the objects to interact and share information.

Internet of Things 是由内置电子元件, 传感器, 软件和网络连接的实体组成的网络. 这些实体可以互动并共享信息.

## Investment Uses | 投资用途

- Identify factors that may affect security prices.
- 识别可能影响证券价格的因素.
- Improve asset selection and trade execution.
- 改善资产选择和交易执行.
- Detect trends, sentiment, and changes in business activity.
- 识别趋势, 情绪和企业活动变化.
- Provide inputs for data-driven investment models.
- 为数据驱动的投资模型提供输入.

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Individuals, business processes, and sensors are sources of alternative data. They are not the three Vs.
- Individuals, business processes 和 sensors 是另类数据来源, 不是三个 V.
- Business-process data may be leading or real-time indicators. Traditional quarterly or annual company metrics are generally lagging indicators.
- Business-process data 可能是领先或实时指标. 传统季度或年度企业指标通常是滞后指标.
- Sensor data may have much greater volume than individual-generated or business-process data.
- Sensor data 的规模可能远高于个人生成数据或业务流程数据.

# 4. Big Data Risks and Challenges | Big Data 风险与挑战

## Data Challenges | 数据挑战

| Question | Risk | 中文解释 |
|---|---|---|
| Is the source credible and reliable? | Low veracity | 来源可能不可信或不可靠 |
| Is the sample representative? | Selection bias | 样本可能存在选择偏差 |
| Are observations complete and accurate? | Missing data, errors, and outliers | 可能存在缺失值, 错误和异常值 |
| Is volume sufficient? | Weak training or inference | 数据量可能不足以支持训练或推断 |
| Is the dataset suited to the question? | Irrelevant or misleading analysis | 数据可能不适合所研究的问题 |
| Can the data be processed? | Technical complexity | 非结构化数据可能难以处理 |

Data normally must be sourced, cleansed, and organized before analysis. Traditional methods may be inadequate for very large, complex, or unstructured datasets.

数据通常必须先经过获取, 清理和组织, 然后才能分析. 传统方法可能无法处理规模极大, 结构复杂或非结构化的数据集.

## Scraping and Ethical Risk | Scraping 与道德风险

Scraping is an automated, large-scale method of retrieving unstructured website data and converting the data into a more structured format.

Scraping 是自动化且大规模地获取网站非结构化数据, 并将其转换为更结构化格式的方法.

Web scraping may capture regulated personal information or information published without informed consent. Legal rules and best practices may differ or conflict across jurisdictions.

Web scraping 可能收集受监管的个人信息, 或收集未经知情同意而发布的信息. 不同司法管辖区的法律规则和最佳实践可能不同或相互冲突.

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

Technical accessibility does not remove legal or ethical obligations.

技术上可以获取数据, 并不代表可以忽视法律或道德义务.

# 5. Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning | 人工智能与机器学习

## Core Definitions | 核心定义

| Concept | Definition | 中文定义 |
|---|---|---|
| Artificial intelligence | Computer systems capable of tasks that traditionally required human intelligence | 能够执行传统上需要人类智能任务的计算机系统 |
| Expert system | An early AI system that simulates expert reasoning, often through explicit `if-then` rules | 早期 AI 系统, 通常通过明确的 `if-then` 规则模拟专家推理 |
| Machine learning | Techniques that learn from examples to identify structure or generate predictions without assuming an underlying probability distribution | 从样本中学习并识别结构或产生预测的技术, 不要求预设底层概率分布 |
| Neural network | A program design inspired by how the human brain learns and processes information | 受人脑学习和信息处理方式启发的程序结构 |
| Deep learning | Multistage, nonlinear processing using neural networks, often with many hidden layers | 使用神经网络进行多阶段非线性处理, 通常包含多个隐藏层 |

The basic ML idea is `find the pattern, apply the pattern`.

ML 的基本思想是 `find the pattern, apply the pattern`.

## Relationship Map | 关系图

- AI is the broad field.
- AI 是更广泛的领域.
- ML is a data-driven approach within AI.
- ML 是 AI 领域中的数据驱动方法.
- Neural networks are an ML design inspired by the brain.
- Neural networks 是受人脑启发的 ML 程序结构.
- Deep learning uses multilayer neural networks and may use supervised or unsupervised learning.
- Deep learning 使用多层神经网络, 并且可以采用 supervised learning 或 unsupervised learning.

## Why ML Use Expanded | ML 应用扩大的原因

Broader use of ML has been supported by greater data availability, improved algorithms, faster computing and software processing, and lower storage costs.

ML 应用扩大得益于数据可用性提高, 算法改进, 计算和软件处理速度提升, 以及存储成本下降.

## Strengths | 优势

- Identify complex, nonlinear relationships.
- 识别复杂的非线性关系.
- Detect patterns, trends, and classifications in large datasets.
- 在大型数据集中识别模式, 趋势和分类.
- Analyze images, speech, text, and other unstructured data.
- 分析图像, 语音, 文本和其他非结构化数据.
- Scale analysis beyond human processing capacity.
- 将分析扩展到超过人类处理能力的规模.

## Limitations | 局限

- ML usually requires sufficiently large training and validation datasets.
- ML 通常需要足够大的训练集和验证集.
- Biased, inaccurate, or spurious data produce unreliable models.
- 有偏差, 不准确或虚假的数据会产生不可靠模型.
- Human judgment remains necessary for understanding data and selecting techniques.
- 理解数据和选择分析方法仍然需要人类判断.
- Models may be opaque or difficult to explain.
- 模型可能不透明或难以解释.

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- AI is broader than ML.
- AI 的范围大于 ML.
- Expert systems follow programmed rules. ML learns patterns from data.
- Expert systems 遵循预先编写的规则. ML 从数据中学习模式.
- ML does not require an assumed probability distribution, but it still requires suitable data and human judgment.
- ML 不要求预设概率分布, 但仍然需要合适的数据和人类判断.

# 6. Machine Learning Workflow | 机器学习流程

## Dataset Preparation and Use | 数据准备与使用

| Step | Dataset or Action | Purpose | 中文解释 |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 1 | Clean the data | Identify and address errors, bias, and spurious observations | 识别并处理错误, 偏差和虚假观测值 |
| 2 | Training dataset | Learn historical relationships between inputs and outputs | 学习输入与输出之间的历史关系 |
| 3 | Validation dataset | Validate relationships and tune the model | 验证关系并调整模型 |
| 4 | Test dataset | Assess prediction on new data | 检验模型对新数据的预测能力 |
| 5 | Application | Predict outcomes using other datasets | 使用其他数据集预测结果 |

The test dataset evaluates out-of-sample performance. It must not be used to train or tune the model.

Test dataset 用于评估样本外表现. 它不应被用于训练或调整模型.

## Inputs and Outputs | 输入与输出

When inputs and labeled outputs are supplied, the model learns the relationship between them. When outputs are not supplied, the model seeks to describe the underlying structure of the inputs.

当输入和带标签输出同时提供时, 模型学习二者之间的关系. 当没有提供输出时, 模型寻找输入数据的底层结构.

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

Training learns, validation tunes, and testing evaluates new-data prediction.

Training 用于学习, validation 用于调参, testing 用于评估新数据预测能力.

# 8. Overfitting and Underfitting | 过拟合与欠拟合

## Comparison | 对比

| Issue | What the Model Does | Typical Cause | Result |
|---|---|---|---|
| Overfitting | Treats noise as true parameters | Model is too complex or overtrained | Strong training fit but weak prediction on different data |
| Underfitting | Treats true parameters as noise | Model is too simple | Fails to discover relationships even in training data |

| 问题 | 模型行为 | 常见原因 | 结果 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Overfitting | 将噪声视为真实参数 | 模型过于复杂或训练过度 | 训练拟合较强, 但在不同数据上的预测较差 |
| Underfitting | 将真实参数视为噪声 | 模型过于简单 | 连训练数据中的关系也无法充分识别 |

## Memory Cue | 记忆提示

- Overfitting: noise becomes signal.
- Overfitting: 噪声被当作信号.
- Underfitting: signal becomes noise.
- Underfitting: 信号被当作噪声.

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

A model should be judged by its performance on new data, not only by its fit to the training dataset.

判断模型时应关注其在新数据上的表现, 而不能只看训练集拟合程度.

Data Science 是结合计算机科学, 包括 ML, 统计学和其他学科的跨学科领域, 其目标是从数据中提取信息和洞见.

Alternative data often include unstructured information and may require specialized processing.

Alternative data 经常包含非结构化信息, 因而可能需要专门处理.

## Investment Management Applications | 投资管理应用

The curriculum identifies text analytics and NLP, risk analysis, and algorithmic trading as three broad applications. This lesson develops text analytics and NLP in detail but only names the other two.

教材将 text analytics 和 NLP, risk analysis 以及 algorithmic trading 列为三类主要应用. 本节详细说明 text analytics 和 NLP, 但仅列举另外两类.

## Big Data Processing Methods | Big Data 处理方法

| Method | Main Question | Function | 中文解释 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Capture | How are data collected? | Collect and transform data into a usable format | 收集数据并转换为可分析格式 |
| Curation | Are data accurate? | Clean data, detect errors, and address missing data when appropriate | 清理数据, 检测错误, 并在适当时处理缺失值 |
| Storage | How are data retained? | Record, archive, access, and design the database | 记录, 归档, 访问数据并设计数据库 |

方法清单: `Capture, Curation, Storage, Search, Transfer`.

教材将这些内容列为处理方法, 但没有规定强制性的时间顺序.

## Latency | 延迟

Low-latency systems process high data volumes with minimal delay and are essential for automated trading based on real-time prices or events. High-latency systems are suitable when real-time data and calculations are unnecessary.

Low-latency systems 以极小延迟处理大量数据, 对基于实时价格或事件的自动交易至关重要. 当不需要实时数据和计算时, 可以使用 high-latency systems.

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Capture means collecting and converting data. Curation means cleaning and validating data.
- Capture 指收集和转换数据. Curation 指清理和验证数据.
- Storage concerns retention and access. Transfer concerns movement to an analytical tool.
- Storage 关注保存和访问. Transfer 关注将数据移动到分析工具.
- Low latency is essential for real-time automated trading, not for every analytical application.
- Low latency 对实时自动交易至关重要, 但并非所有分析应用都需要低延迟.

# 11. Text Analytics and Natural Language Processing | 文本分析与自然语言处理

## Text Analytics | 文本分析

Text analytics uses computer programs to analyze large, unstructured text or voice datasets and derive meaning from them.

Text analytics 使用计算机程序分析大型非结构化文本或语音数据, 并从中提取含义.

Sources include company filings, written reports, earnings calls, social media, email, internet postings, and surveys.

数据来源包括公司申报文件, 书面报告, 业绩电话会议, 社交媒体, 电子邮件, 网络帖子和调查.

Methods include automated information retrieval, lexical analysis based on word frequency, pattern recognition using keywords and phrases, and predictive analysis using indicators such as consumer sentiment.

方法包括自动信息检索, 基于词频的词汇分析, 使用关键词和短语的模式识别, 以及使用消费者情绪等指标进行预测分析.

## Natural Language Processing | 自然语言处理

NLP lies at the intersection of computer science, AI, and linguistics. It develops programs that analyze and interpret human language.

NLP 位于计算机科学, AI 和语言学的交叉领域. 它开发能够分析和解释人类语言的程序.

NLP is an application within the broader field of text analytics.

NLP 是更广泛的 text analytics 领域中的一种应用.

NLP tasks include translation, speech recognition, text mining, sentiment analysis, and topic analysis.

NLP 任务包括翻译, 语音识别, 文本挖掘, 情绪分析和主题分析.

## Investment Applications | 投资应用

| Application | Use | 中文解释 |

# CFA Memory Points | CFA 记忆重点

## 1. Big Data Characteristics | Big Data 特征

`Volume + Velocity + Variety`, with `Veracity` added when credibility and reliability matter for inference or prediction.

`Volume + Velocity + Variety`, 当推断或预测需要关注可信度和可靠性时加入 `Veracity`.

## 2. Alternative Data Sources | 另类数据来源

`Individuals + Business processes + Sensors`.

`Individuals + Business processes + Sensors`.

## 3. ML Workflow | ML 流程

`Clean -> Train -> Validate and tune -> Test -> Apply`.

`清理 -> 训练 -> 验证和调参 -> 测试 -> 应用`.

## 4. ML Classification | ML 分类

Supervised learning uses labeled data. Unsupervised learning uses unlabeled data. Deep learning uses multilayer neural networks and may use either approach.

Supervised learning 使用带标签数据. Unsupervised learning 使用无标签数据. Deep learning 使用多层神经网络, 并且可以采用前两种方法中的任何一种.

## 5. Model-Fit Trap | 模型拟合陷阱

Overfitting treats noise as signal. Underfitting treats signal as noise.

Overfitting 将噪声视为信号. Underfitting 将信号视为噪声.

## 6. Data Processing | 数据处理

`Capture, Curation, Storage, Search, Transfer`.

`Capture, Curation, Storage, Search, Transfer`.

## 7. Text Analysis Hierarchy | 文本分析层级

Text analytics is broader than NLP.

Text analytics 的范围大于 NLP.

## 8. Database Shortcut | 数据库速记

SQL-based databases run on a server. SQLite is embedded and serverless. NoSQL handles non-tabular data.

SQL-based databases 在服务器上运行. SQLite 嵌入程序且不需要服务器. NoSQL 处理非表格数据.

## 9. Common Mistakes | 常见错误

- Do not equate Big Data with alternative data.
- 不要将 Big Data 等同于 alternative data.
- Do not equate all alternative data with unstructured data.
- 不要将所有 alternative data 都视为非结构化数据.
- Do not confuse validation with testing.
- 不要混淆 validation 和 testing.
- Do not judge a model only by training fit.
- 不要只根据训练集拟合程度判断模型.
- Do not treat technical access as legal or ethical permission.
- 不要将技术上可以获取数据等同于获得法律或道德许可.

## 10. Exam Shortcut | 考试技巧

For classification questions, first identify whether the item describes a data characteristic, a data source, an analytical method, or a processing step.

处理分类题时, 先判断题目描述的是数据特征, 数据来源, 分析方法还是处理步骤.
