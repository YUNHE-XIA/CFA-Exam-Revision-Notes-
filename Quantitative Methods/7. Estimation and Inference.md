# 1. Population, Sample, Parameter, and Statistic | 总体、样本、参数与统计量

## Definition | 定义

A population contains all members of a specified group; a sample is a subset of that population. A parameter describes a population and is conventionally represented by a Greek letter. A statistic is calculated from a sample and is used to estimate a population parameter.

总体包含特定群体的全部成员；样本是总体的一个子集。参数描述总体，通常用希腊字母表示；统计量根据样本计算，用于估计总体参数。

| Concept | Example | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| Population parameter | Population mean $\mu$ | 总体参数，例如总体均值 |
| Sample statistic | Sample mean $\bar{X}$ | 样本统计量，例如样本均值 |
| Sampling plan | Rules used to select a sample | 选择样本的一套规则 |

## Why Sample? | 为什么抽样

Sampling saves time and money when examining the entire population is impossible or uneconomic. The trade-off is sampling error because only part of the population is observed.

当普查总体不可行或不经济时，抽样可以节约时间和成本；代价是只观察总体的一部分会产生抽样误差。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- **Parameter = population; statistic = sample.**  
  **参数对应总体；统计量对应样本。**
- A statistic varies from sample to sample and is therefore a random variable.  
  统计量会随样本变化，因此本身是随机变量。

# 2. Sampling Error and Sampling Distribution | 抽样误差与抽样分布

## Definition | 定义

Sampling error is the difference between an observed sample statistic and the population parameter it estimates, arising because only a subset of the population is used.

抽样误差是样本统计量与其所估计总体参数之间的差异，其产生原因是只使用了总体的一个子集。

## Formula | 公式

For the sample mean:

对于样本均值：

$$
\mathrm{Sampling\ error}=\bar{X}-\mu
$$

## Sampling Distribution | 抽样分布

The sampling distribution of a statistic is the distribution of all distinct values the statistic can take across random samples of the same size from the same population.

统计量的抽样分布，是从同一总体随机抽取相同规模的所有可能样本时，该统计量所有可能取值构成的分布。

## Interpretation | 解释

Larger representative samples generally reduce sampling error. However, increasing sample size cannot repair a sample drawn from the wrong population or a non-representative selection process.

更大且具有代表性的样本通常可以降低抽样误差；但若样本来自错误总体或选择过程缺乏代表性，增加样本量无法纠正问题。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Sampling error is caused by sampling variation; selection bias arises from an unrepresentative sampling process.  
  抽样误差来自样本波动；选择偏差来自缺乏代表性的抽样过程。
- A larger sample is not automatically better if observations come from different underlying distributions.  
  若观测值来自不同的基础分布，更大的样本不一定更好。

# 3. Probability vs. Non-Probability Sampling | 概率抽样与非概率抽样

## Definition | 定义

Probability sampling uses a random selection mechanism and can produce representative samples. Non-probability sampling depends on accessibility or researcher judgment and carries greater risk of a non-representative sample.

概率抽样采用随机选择机制，能够产生具有代表性的样本。非概率抽样取决于数据可得性或研究者判断，样本缺乏代表性的风险更高。

## Comparison | 对比

| Category | Methods | Main Advantage | Main Risk |
|---|---|---|---|
| Probability | Simple random, systematic, stratified random, cluster | Greater reliability and statistical validity | Cost and implementation effort |
| Non-probability | Convenience, judgmental | Fast, inexpensive, or uses expertise | Selection bias and poor representativeness |

| 类别 | 方法 | 主要优点 | 主要风险 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 概率抽样 | 简单随机、系统、分层随机、整群抽样 | 可靠性与统计有效性较高 | 成本与执行难度 |
| 非概率抽样 | 便利抽样、判断抽样 | 快速、低成本或可利用专业判断 | 选择偏差与代表性不足 |

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

Probability sampling generally provides greater accuracy and reliability, all else equal. Non-probability sampling may be practical, but a large non-probability sample can still be biased.

其他条件相同，概率抽样通常更准确、更可靠。非概率抽样可能更实用，但即使样本很大，也仍可能存在偏差。

# 4. Simple Random and Systematic Sampling | 简单随机抽样与系统抽样

## Simple Random Sampling | 简单随机抽样

Each population element has an equal probability of selection. This method is most useful when the population is broadly homogeneous.

总体中的每个元素被选中的概率相同。该方法最适用于总体较为同质的情况。

## Systematic Sampling | 系统抽样

Select every kth population member until the desired sample size is reached. The resulting sample should be approximately random.

每隔 $k$ 个总体成员选取一个，直至达到目标样本量；所得样本应近似随机。

## Interpretation | 解释

Simple random sampling is statistically straightforward but may fail to represent important subgroups in a heterogeneous population. Systematic sampling is useful when coding or identifying every population member is difficult.

简单随机抽样在统计上直接，但面对异质总体时可能遗漏重要子群。系统抽样适用于难以编码或识别全部总体成员的情况。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- “Every element has equal probability” $\Rightarrow$ simple random sampling.  
  “每个元素具有相同被选概率” $\Rightarrow$ 简单随机抽样。
- “Every $k$th item” $\Rightarrow$ systematic sampling.  
  “每隔 $k$ 个选一个” $\Rightarrow$ 系统抽样。

# 5. Stratified Random Sampling | 分层随机抽样

## Definition | 定义

Divide the population into strata using relevant classification criteria, then randomly sample from **every stratum**, usually in proportion to each stratum's population weight. Pool the selected observations to form the full sample.

按照相关分类标准将总体划分为不同层，然后从**每一层**随机抽样，样本量通常与该层在总体中的比重成比例，最后合并为完整样本。

## Interpretation | 解释

Stratification guarantees representation of important population subdivisions. When strata capture meaningful population differences, parameter estimates can have lower variance and greater precision than estimates from simple random sampling.

分层可确保重要总体子群被纳入样本。当分层反映总体的重要差异时，参数估计通常比简单随机抽样具有更低方差和更高精度。

## Investment Application | 投资应用

In bond indexing, bonds may be stratified by issuer type, maturity, coupon, duration, sector, credit quality, cash flow distribution, and call exposure. Securities are sampled from each cell to approximate index risk exposures at lower cost than full replication.

在债券指数化中，可按发行人类型、期限、票息、久期、行业、信用质量、现金流分布及赎回风险进行分层，再从每个单元抽取证券，以低于完全复制的成本近似指数风险敞口。

## Worked Example | 计算示例

Given | 已知：

- 3 issuer groups.  
  3 个发行人类别。
- 10 maturity intervals.  
  10 个期限区间。
- 2 coupon categories.  
  2 个票息类别。

Calculation | 计算：

$$
\mathrm{Number\ of\ strata}=3\times10\times2=60
$$

Answer | 答案：

- The plan has **60 strata**.  
  该方案共有 **60 层**。
- At least one issue per stratum implies a minimum of **60 issues**.  
  若每层至少选择一只债券，最低需要 **60 只债券**。
- Selecting the most liquid security within each cell is judgmental, so the final sample is **not random**.  
  若在每层按流动性筛选证券，则包含主观判断，最终样本**不是随机样本**。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

**All strata are included; only some elements within each stratum are selected.**

**所有层都被纳入；每层仅选取部分元素。**

# 6. Cluster Sampling | 整群抽样

## Definition | 定义

Divide the population into clusters, each designed as a mini-representation of the population. Randomly select only certain clusters; the cluster is the sampling unit.

将总体划分为若干整群，每个群尽量作为总体的微型代表；仅随机选择部分整群，整群本身是抽样单位。

## One-Stage vs. Two-Stage | 单阶段与两阶段

| Method | Procedure | 中文解释 |
|---|---|---|
| One-stage cluster | Include every member of each selected cluster | 纳入被选整群中的全部成员 |
| Two-stage cluster | Randomly subsample members within each selected cluster | 在被选整群内部再次随机抽样 |

## Interpretation | 解释

Cluster sampling is highly time- and cost-efficient for large, geographically dispersed populations. Given equal sample size, it usually has lower accuracy than other probability methods because selected clusters may not represent the entire population well.

整群抽样适合规模庞大、地域分散的总体，时间和成本效率很高。在样本量相同的情况下，其准确性通常低于其他概率抽样，因为被选整群可能无法充分代表总体。

## Stratified vs. Cluster | 分层与整群的区别

| Stratified Sampling | Cluster Sampling |
|---|---|
| Sample from every stratum | Select only some clusters |
| Individual elements are sampling units | Whole clusters are initial sampling units |
| Strata represent distinct population subdivisions | Each cluster should resemble the whole population |
| Usually improves precision | Usually prioritizes cost and operational efficiency |

| 分层抽样 | 整群抽样 |
|---|---|
| 从每一层抽样 | 只选择部分整群 |
| 个体元素是抽样单位 | 整群是初始抽样单位 |
| 各层代表不同总体子群 | 每个整群应近似整个总体 |
| 通常提高精度 | 通常侧重成本与执行效率 |

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- **Stratified: all groups, some members.**  
  **分层：所有组、每组选部分成员。**
- **Cluster: some groups, then all or some members.**  
  **整群：选部分组，再选组内全部或部分成员。**

# 7. Convenience and Judgmental Sampling | 便利抽样与判断抽样

## Convenience Sampling | 便利抽样

Select observations because they are readily accessible. It is fast and inexpensive and can be useful for pilot research, but the sample may not represent the population.

根据观测值是否容易获得进行选择。该方法快速、成本低，适合初步研究，但样本可能无法代表总体。

## Judgmental Sampling | 判断抽样

The researcher handpicks observations using knowledge and professional judgment. It can target relevant observations efficiently, but researcher bias may skew the results.

研究者利用知识和专业判断主动挑选观测值。该方法可以高效锁定重要对象，但研究者偏差可能使结果失真。

## Applications | 应用

| Clue | Sampling Method | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Available internal database | Convenience | Use data already accessible to the analyst |
| Expert selects high-risk transactions | Judgmental | Experienced auditor targets relevant accounts |

| 识别线索 | 抽样方法 | 示例 |
|---|---|---|
| 使用现有内部数据库 | 便利抽样 | 使用分析师已经能够取得的数据 |
| 专家挑选高风险交易 | 判断抽样 | 资深审计师锁定重要账户 |

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Convenience is based on **ease of access**.  
  便利抽样依据的是**可得性**。
- Judgmental sampling is based on **expert choice**.  
  判断抽样依据的是**专家判断**。
- Neither method permits the same statistical confidence as a representative probability sample.  
  两者都无法提供与代表性概率样本相同的统计可信度。

# 8. Sample Size and Population Consistency | 样本量与总体一致性

## Interpretation | 解释

Sampling error generally declines as sample size increases, but the incremental benefit may diminish. More importantly, all observations should represent the same underlying population or distribution.

随着样本量增加，抽样误差通常下降，但边际收益可能递减。更重要的是，所有观测值应代表同一基础总体或分布。

## Example: Pooling Different Strategies | 示例：合并不同策略

| Period | Mean Excess Return | Standard Deviation | Sharpe Ratio |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Year 1: low risk | 1.0% | 4.62% | 0.22 |
| Year 2: high risk | 4.0% | 18.48% | 0.22 |
| Pooled two years | 2.5% | 12.57% | 0.199 |

Each individual year outperforms a benchmark Sharpe ratio of 0.21, but the pooled sample appears to underperform. The manager changed strategy, so the two years came from distributions with different means and variances. The pooled sample represents neither population properly.

每个年度的 Sharpe ratio 都高于基准的 0.21，但合并样本后却显示为落后。原因是管理人改变了策略，两年的收益来自均值和方差不同的分布；合并后的样本无法正确代表任何一个总体。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

**Homogeneity and correct model assumptions matter more than sample size alone.**

**同质性与正确的模型假设比单纯扩大样本量更重要。**

# 9. Central Limit Theorem | 中心极限定理

## Definition | 定义

For independent random samples of size $n$ from a population with mean $\mu$ and finite variance $\sigma^2$, the sampling distribution of the sample mean becomes approximately normal as $n$ becomes large, regardless of the population's distribution.

从均值为 $\mu$、有限方差为 $\sigma^2$ 的总体中抽取独立随机样本，当样本量 $n$ 足够大时，无论总体分布为何，样本均值的抽样分布都近似服从正态分布。

## Formula | 公式

$$
\bar{X}\approx N\left(\mu,\frac{\sigma^2}{n}\right)
$$

$$
E(\bar{X})=\mu
$$

$$
\mathrm{Var}(\bar{X})=\frac{\sigma^2}{n}
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $\bar{X}$ | Sample mean | 样本均值 |
| $\mu$ | Population mean | 总体均值 |
| $\sigma^2$ | Population variance | 总体方差 |
| $n$ | Sample size | 样本量 |

## Interpretation | 解释

As $n$ increases, the sampling distribution becomes more nearly normal and more concentrated around $\mu$. The CLT makes confidence intervals and hypothesis tests for population means possible even when the underlying population is not normal.

随着 $n$ 增加，样本均值的抽样分布更接近正态分布，并更集中于 $\mu$。即使基础总体不是正态分布，中心极限定理也使总体均值的置信区间与假设检验成为可能。

## Sample-Size Guideline | 样本量经验规则

When $n\geq30$, the sample mean is often treated as approximately normal. A substantially larger sample may be required when the underlying population is highly non-normal.

当 $n\geq30$ 时，通常可将样本均值视为近似正态；若基础总体严重偏离正态，则可能需要远大于 30 的样本。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- The **sample mean**, not the underlying population, becomes approximately normal.  
  近似正态的是**样本均值的抽样分布**，不是基础总体本身。
- Finite population variance and a sufficiently large independent random sample are critical.  
  有限总体方差以及足够大的独立随机样本是关键条件。
- The CLT does not prove that portfolio risk approaches zero when asset returns are dependent.  
  当资产收益相互依赖时，中心极限定理不能证明投资组合风险趋近于零。

# 10. Standard Error of the Sample Mean | 样本均值的标准误

## Definition | 定义

Standard error is the standard deviation of a statistic's sampling distribution. It measures the sampling-related imprecision of a parameter estimate.

标准误是统计量抽样分布的标准差，衡量参数估计因抽样而产生的不精确程度。

## Formula | 公式

When population standard deviation $\sigma$ is known:

总体标准差 $\sigma$ 已知时：

$$
\sigma_{\bar{X}}=\frac{\sigma}{\sqrt{n}}
$$

When $\sigma$ is unknown, estimate it with sample standard deviation $s$:

总体标准差 $\sigma$ 未知时，用样本标准差 $s$ 估计：

$$
s_{\bar{X}}=\frac{s}{\sqrt{n}}
$$

$$
s^2=\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n}(X_i-\bar{X})^2}{n-1}
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $\sigma_{\bar{X}}$ | True standard error when $\sigma$ is known | $\sigma$ 已知时的标准误 |
| $s_{\bar{X}}$ | Estimated standard error | 估计标准误 |
| $s^2$ | Sample variance | 样本方差 |

## Standard Deviation vs. Standard Error | 标准差与标准误

| Measure | What It Describes | Effect of Larger $n$ |
|---|---|---|
| Standard deviation | Dispersion of individual observations | Does not mechanically fall with $n$ |
| Standard error | Precision of a sample statistic | Falls at the rate $1/\sqrt{n}$ |

| 指标 | 描述对象 | 增大 $n$ 的影响 |
|---|---|---|
| 标准差 | 个体观测值的离散程度 | 不会因 $n$ 增加而机械下降 |
| 标准误 | 样本统计量的估计精度 | 按 $1/\sqrt{n}$ 的速度下降 |

## Worked Example: Required Sample Size | 计算示例：所需样本量

Given population standard deviation $\sigma=6\%$:

已知总体标准差 $\sigma=6\%$：

$$
n=\left(\frac{\sigma}{\sigma_{\bar{X}}}\right)^2
$$

For a target standard error of $1\%$:

目标标准误为 $1\%$ 时：

$$
n=\left(\frac{6\%}{1\%}\right)^2=36
$$

For a target standard error of $0.25\%$:

目标标准误为 $0.25\%$ 时：

$$
n=\left(\frac{6\%}{0.25\%}\right)^2=576
$$

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Cutting standard error in half requires four times the sample size.  
  标准误减半需要将样本量扩大至四倍。
- In practice, $\sigma$ is usually unknown, so $s/\sqrt{n}$ is commonly used.  
  实务中通常不知道 $\sigma$，因此常用 $s/\sqrt{n}$。
- Standard deviation describes data; standard error supports inference.  
  标准差描述数据；标准误用于统计推断。

# 11. Resampling and Bootstrap | 重抽样与 Bootstrap

## Definition | 定义

Resampling repeatedly draws samples from the original observed sample to infer population parameters. Bootstrap is a model-free or non-parametric resampling method that draws repeated samples **with replacement**, each normally the same size as the original sample.

重抽样从原始观测样本中反复抽取样本，以推断总体参数。Bootstrap 是一种无模型或非参数重抽样方法，进行**有放回抽样**，每个重抽样样本通常与原样本等大。

## Procedure | 步骤

1. Start with an observed sample of size $n$.  
   从容量为 $n$ 的观测样本开始。
2. Draw a bootstrap resample of size $n$ with replacement.  
   有放回地抽取一个容量为 $n$ 的 bootstrap 样本。
3. Calculate the statistic of interest.  
   计算目标统计量。
4. Repeat for $B$ resamples.  
   重复 $B$ 次。
5. Use the empirical distribution of the $B$ statistics to estimate standard error or construct a confidence interval.  
   使用 $B$ 个统计量的经验分布估计标准误或构建置信区间。

## Bootstrap Standard Error | Bootstrap 标准误

$$
\bar{X}_{boot}=\frac{1}{B}\sum_{b=1}^{B}\bar{X}_b
$$

$$
s_{boot}=\sqrt{\frac{\sum_{b=1}^{B}(\bar{X}_b-\bar{X}_{boot})^2}{B-1}}
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $B$ | Number of bootstrap resamples | Bootstrap 重抽样次数 |
| $\bar{X}_b$ | Mean of bootstrap resample $b$ | 第 $b$ 个重抽样样本的均值 |
| $\bar{X}_{boot}$ | Mean across bootstrap sample means | 全部重抽样均值的平均值 |
| $s_{boot}$ | Bootstrap estimate of standard error | Bootstrap 标准误估计值 |

## Worked Example | 计算示例

Given | 已知：

- $B=1{,}000$.  
  重抽样次数为 1,000。
- Sum of squared deviations of resample means: $1.94143$.  
  重抽样均值的离差平方和为 1.94143。

Calculation | 计算：

$$
s_{boot}=\sqrt{\frac{1.94143}{1{,}000-1}}
$$

Answer | 答案：

$$
s_{boot}=0.0441
$$

## Applications and Advantages | 应用与优点

- Estimate standard errors and confidence intervals without an analytical formula.  
  无需解析公式即可估计标准误和置信区间。
- Apply to complicated statistics, including the median.  
  可用于复杂统计量，包括中位数。
- Support historical simulation in asset allocation and strategy evaluation against a benchmark.  
  可用于资产配置的历史模拟及投资策略相对基准的表现评估。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- **With replacement** is essential; one observation can appear multiple times while another may be absent.  
  **有放回抽样**是关键；同一观测值可能出现多次，其他观测值可能未出现。
- The formula $s/\sqrt{n}$ is for the standard error of the sample mean, not automatically for the sample median. Bootstrap can estimate the median's standard error.  
  $s/\sqrt{n}$ 是样本均值标准误公式，不能直接套用于样本中位数；Bootstrap 可估计中位数标准误。
- Bootstrap results usually differ across runs because resampling is random.  
  由于抽样具有随机性，不同运行的 Bootstrap 结果通常不同。

# 12. Jackknife Resampling | Jackknife 重抽样

## Definition | 定义

Jackknife repeatedly forms samples by leaving out one observation at a time from the original sample, without replacement. For an original sample of size $n$, it usually produces $n$ leave-one-out samples, each of size $n-1$.

Jackknife 每次从原始样本中剔除一个观测值且不放回。原样本容量为 $n$ 时，通常生成 $n$ 个“逐一删除”样本，每个样本容量为 $n-1$。

## Uses | 用途

- Reduce estimator bias.  
  降低估计量偏差。
- Estimate standard errors.  
  估计标准误。
- Construct confidence intervals.  
  构建置信区间。

## Bootstrap vs. Jackknife | Bootstrap 与 Jackknife 对比

| Feature | Bootstrap | Jackknife |
|---|---|---|
| Resampling rule | Draw with replacement | Leave out one observation at a time |
| Resample size | Usually $n$ | $n-1$ |
| Number of repetitions | Chosen by analyst, often large | Usually $n$ |
| Randomness | Random; results may vary by run | Systematic; results are similar each run |
| Common strengths | Flexible for complex sampling distributions | Often used for bias reduction |

| 特征 | Bootstrap | Jackknife |
|---|---|---|
| 重抽样规则 | 有放回抽样 | 每次剔除一个观测值 |
| 重抽样样本量 | 通常为 $n$ | $n-1$ |
| 重复次数 | 由分析师决定，通常较多 | 通常为 $n$ 次 |
| 随机性 | 随机；不同运行结果可能不同 | 系统性；每次运行结果相似 |
| 常见优势 | 灵活估计复杂抽样分布 | 常用于降低偏差 |

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- **Bootstrap = draw with replacement.**  
  **Bootstrap = 有放回抽样。**
- **Jackknife = leave one out, no replacement.**  
  **Jackknife = 逐一删除、不放回。**

# CFA Memory Points | CFA 记忆重点

## 1. Key Formula | 核心公式

$$
\bar{X}\approx N\left(\mu,\frac{\sigma^2}{n}\right)
$$

$$
\sigma_{\bar{X}}=\frac{\sigma}{\sqrt{n}}
$$

The sample mean becomes approximately normal, and its standard error falls at the rate $1/\sqrt{n}$.

样本均值近似服从正态分布，其标准误按 $1/\sqrt{n}$ 的速度下降。

## 2. Key Relationship | 核心关系

- **Stratified:** all strata, some members from each.  
  **分层抽样：** 所有层，每层选部分成员。
- **Cluster:** some clusters, then all or some members.  
  **整群抽样：** 选部分整群，再选组内全部或部分成员。
- **Convenience:** accessibility.  
  **便利抽样：** 可得性。
- **Judgmental:** expertise.  
  **判断抽样：** 专业判断。

## 3. Common Mistake | 常见错误

Do not confuse standard deviation with standard error, and do not assume that the underlying population must be normal for the CLT to apply.

不要混淆标准差与标准误，也不要误认为应用中心极限定理时基础总体必须服从正态分布。

## 4. Resampling Cue | 重抽样记忆

- **Bootstrap:** with replacement, usually size $n$, random repetitions.  
  **Bootstrap：** 有放回、通常样本量为 $n$、随机重复。
- **Jackknife:** leave one out, size $n-1$, usually $n$ repetitions.  
  **Jackknife：** 每次剔除一个、样本量为 $n-1$、通常重复 $n$ 次。

## 5. Exam Shortcut | 考试技巧

To reduce standard error by a factor of $k$, multiply sample size by $k^2$.

若要将标准误降低为原来的 $1/k$，样本量需扩大为原来的 $k^2$ 倍。
