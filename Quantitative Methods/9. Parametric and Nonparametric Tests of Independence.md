# Module 9: Parametric and Nonparametric Tests of Independence | 参数与非参数独立性检验

# Learning Outcomes | 学习目标

- Explain parametric and nonparametric tests of whether the population correlation coefficient equals zero and make a decision at a given significance level.  
  解释总体相关系数是否等于零的参数与非参数检验，并在给定显著性水平下作出决策。
- Explain chi-square tests of independence using contingency table data.  
  解释使用列联表数据进行的卡方独立性检验。

# 1. Correlation and Hypotheses | 相关性与假设

## Definition | 定义

Correlation measures the strength and direction of a linear relationship between two random variables. A significance test asks whether an observed sample correlation could reasonably occur by chance when the population correlation is zero.

相关系数衡量两个随机变量线性关系的强度与方向。显著性检验判断：当总体相关系数为零时，观测到的样本相关系数是否可能仅由随机因素产生。

## Hypothesis Forms | 假设形式

Two-tailed | 双尾：

$$
H_0:\rho=0
$$

$$
H_a:\rho\neq0
$$

Right-tailed | 右尾：

$$
H_0:\rho\leq0
$$

$$
H_a:\rho>0
$$

Left-tailed | 左尾：

$$
H_0:\rho\geq0
$$

$$
H_a:\rho<0
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $\rho$ | Population correlation coefficient | 总体相关系数 |
| $r$ | Sample correlation coefficient | 样本相关系数 |

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- “A relationship exists” $\Rightarrow$ $H_a:\rho\neq0$.  
  “存在关系” $\Rightarrow$ $H_a:\rho\neq0$。
- “Positive relationship” $\Rightarrow$ $H_a:\rho>0$.  
  “存在正相关” $\Rightarrow$ $H_a:\rho>0$。
- Statistical significance does not imply a strong or economically important relationship.  
  统计显著并不代表关系很强或具有重要经济意义。
- Correlation does not establish causation.  
  相关关系不能证明因果关系。

# 2. Pearson Correlation | Pearson 相关系数

## Definition | 定义

Pearson correlation, also called bivariate correlation, is a parametric measure of linear association. It uses the original numerical values, so the magnitude of each observation's deviation from its mean matters.

Pearson 相关系数也称二元相关系数，是衡量线性关系的参数指标。它使用原始数值，因此每个观测值偏离均值的幅度会影响结果。

## Formula | 公式

$$
r_{XY}=\frac{s_{XY}}{s_Xs_Y}
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $r_{XY}$ | Sample Pearson correlation between $X$ and $Y$ | $X$ 与 $Y$ 的样本 Pearson 相关系数 |
| $s_{XY}$ | Sample covariance of $X$ and $Y$ | $X$ 与 $Y$ 的样本协方差 |
| $s_X,s_Y$ | Sample standard deviations | 两个变量的样本标准差 |

## Interpretation | 解释

The covariance determines the sign of correlation. Standardizing by $s_Xs_Y$ makes correlation unit-free and bounded between $-1$ and $+1$.

协方差决定相关系数的符号。除以 $s_Xs_Y$ 后，相关系数成为无量纲指标，取值范围为 $-1$ 至 $+1$。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

Pearson correlation is appropriate for numerical variables when its parametric distributional assumptions are reasonable. It is sensitive to extreme values because it uses the original observations.

当变量为数值型且参数分布假设合理时，适合使用 Pearson 相关。由于使用原始观测值，该指标对极端值较敏感。

# 3. Parametric Test of Zero Pearson Correlation | Pearson 零相关的参数检验

## Definition | 定义

When the two variables satisfy the relevant normality assumptions, the sample Pearson correlation can be transformed into a $t$-statistic to test whether population correlation equals zero.

当两个变量满足相关正态性假设时，可将样本 Pearson 相关系数转换为 $t$ 统计量，以检验总体相关系数是否为零。

## Formula | 公式

$$
t=\frac{r\sqrt{n-2}}{\sqrt{1-r^2}}
$$

$$
df=n-2
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $r$ | Sample Pearson correlation | 样本 Pearson 相关系数 |
| $n$ | Number of paired observations | 成对观测值数量 |
| $df$ | Degrees of freedom | 自由度 |

## Decision Rule | 决策规则

- Two-tailed: reject $H_0$ if $|t|$ exceeds the positive critical value.  
  双尾检验：若 $|t|$ 大于正临界值，则拒绝 $H_0$。
- Right-tailed: reject $H_0$ if $t$ exceeds the right-tail critical value.  
  右尾检验：若 $t$ 大于右尾临界值，则拒绝 $H_0$。
- Left-tailed: reject $H_0$ if $t$ is below the left-tail critical value.  
  左尾检验：若 $t$ 小于左尾临界值，则拒绝 $H_0$。

## Sample Size and Power | 样本量与检验功效

For the same nonzero $r$, a larger $n$ increases $|t|$ and degrees of freedom, making rejection more likely. Thus, larger samples increase power, but very large samples can make economically trivial correlations statistically significant.

对于相同的非零 $r$，更大的 $n$ 会提高 $|t|$ 和自由度，使拒绝 $H_0$ 更容易。因此，大样本可提高检验功效，但极大样本也可能使经济意义很弱的相关关系达到统计显著。

## Worked Example 1: Same Correlation, Different Sample Size | 计算示例 1：相同相关系数、不同样本量

Given $r=0.35$ and a 5% two-tailed test:

已知 $r=0.35$，采用 5% 双尾检验：

| $n$ | Calculated $t$ | Critical Values | Decision |
|---:|---:|---:|---|
| 12 | 1.182 | $\pm2.228$ | Fail to reject $H_0$ |
| 32 | 2.046 | $\pm2.042$ | Reject $H_0$ |

The sample correlation is identical, but only the larger sample produces statistical significance.

样本相关系数相同，但只有较大样本得到统计显著结果。

## Worked Example 2: Investment Returns | 计算示例 2：投资收益

Given $n=33$, $r=0.43051$, and a 1% right-tailed test:

已知 $n=33$、$r=0.43051$，采用 1% 右尾检验：

$$
H_0:\rho\leq0
$$

$$
H_a:\rho>0
$$

$$
t=\frac{0.43051\sqrt{33-2}}{\sqrt{1-0.43051^2}}=2.656
$$

With $df=31$, the statistic exceeds the 1% right-tail critical value, so reject $H_0$. Evidence supports a positive population correlation.

自由度为 31，统计量超过 1% 右尾临界值，因此拒绝 $H_0$；证据支持总体相关系数为正。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Use paired observation count $n$, not the total number of individual $X$ and $Y$ values.  
  $n$ 是成对观测数，而不是 $X$ 与 $Y$ 单独观测数之和。
- Degrees of freedom are $n-2$.  
  自由度为 $n-2$。
- A negative $r$ produces a negative $t$; the sign is preserved.  
  负的 $r$ 会产生负的 $t$，符号保持一致。

# 4. Spearman Rank Correlation | Spearman 秩相关系数

## Definition | 定义

Spearman rank correlation is a nonparametric measure calculated from the ranks of two variables rather than their original values. It is appropriate when normality assumptions are doubtful, observations contain influential outliers, or the data are ordinal or ranked.

Spearman 秩相关系数是一种非参数指标，根据两个变量的秩而非原始数值计算。当正态性假设存疑、存在重要异常值，或数据为顺序或排名数据时适用。

## Ranking Procedure | 排名步骤

1. Rank $X$ from largest to smallest; rank $Y$ separately in the same way.  
   分别对 $X$ 与 $Y$ 从大到小排序。
2. For ties, assign each tied observation the average of the occupied ranks.  
   若存在并列值，为每个并列观测分配其所占秩的平均值。
3. For each pair, calculate the rank difference $d_i$ and square it.  
   对每一对观测计算秩差 $d_i$ 及其平方。

## Formula | 公式

When there are no ties, the shortcut formula is:

不存在并列秩时，可使用以下简化公式：

$$
r_s=1-\frac{6\sum_{i=1}^{n}d_i^2}{n(n^2-1)}
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $r_s$ | Sample Spearman rank correlation | 样本 Spearman 秩相关系数 |
| $d_i$ | Difference between the two ranks for pair $i$ | 第 $i$ 对观测的两个秩之差 |
| $n$ | Number of paired observations | 成对观测值数量 |

Spearman correlation is equivalent to calculating Pearson correlation on the ranks. When ties exist, use average ranks and calculate correlation from those ranks.

Spearman 相关本质上等于对秩数据计算 Pearson 相关。若存在并列值，应使用平均秩，再根据这些秩计算相关系数。

## Testing Spearman Correlation | Spearman 相关检验

- For small samples, use specialized Spearman critical-value tables.  
  小样本应使用 Spearman 专用临界值表。
- For large samples, defined in the curriculum as $n>30$, use the same $t$ transformation with $n-2$ degrees of freedom.  
  对大样本（课程定义为 $n>30$），使用相同的 $t$ 转换，自由度为 $n-2$。

$$
t=\frac{r_s\sqrt{n-2}}{\sqrt{1-r_s^2}}
$$

## Worked Example | 计算示例

Given $n=35$ and $\sum d_i^2=2{,}202$:

已知 $n=35$，且 $\sum d_i^2=2{,}202$：

$$
r_s=1-\frac{6(2{,}202)}{35(35^2-1)}=0.6916
$$

$$
t=\frac{0.6916\sqrt{35-2}}{\sqrt{1-0.6916^2}}=5.500
$$

With $df=33$ and 5% two-tailed critical values $\pm2.0345$, reject the null of zero rank correlation.

自由度为 33，5% 双尾临界值为 $\pm2.0345$。因此拒绝秩相关系数为零的原假设。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- **Pearson uses values; Spearman uses ranks.**  
  **Pearson 使用原始数值；Spearman 使用秩。**
- Spearman is nonparametric and reduces the influence of extreme magnitudes, but it does not make poor data quality irrelevant.  
  Spearman 属于非参数方法，可降低极端数值幅度的影响，但并不能消除数据质量问题。
- Do not confuse $r_s$ with Pearson's $r$.  
  不要混淆 Spearman 的 $r_s$ 与 Pearson 的 $r$。

# 5. Pearson vs. Spearman | Pearson 与 Spearman 对比

| Feature | Pearson Correlation | Spearman Rank Correlation |
|---|---|---|
| Type | Parametric | Nonparametric |
| Input | Original numerical values | Ranks |
| Relationship measured | Linear association | Rank association |
| Distributional reliance | Requires stronger assumptions | Minimal assumptions |
| Outlier sensitivity | Higher | Lower |
| Large-sample zero-correlation test | $t$, $df=n-2$ | $t$, $df=n-2$ when $n>30$ |

| 特征 | Pearson 相关 | Spearman 秩相关 |
|---|---|---|
| 类型 | 参数方法 | 非参数方法 |
| 输入数据 | 原始数值 | 秩 |
| 衡量关系 | 线性关系 | 秩关系 |
| 分布依赖 | 假设较强 | 假设较少 |
| 对异常值敏感度 | 较高 | 较低 |
| 大样本零相关检验 | $t$，$df=n-2$ | $n>30$ 时用 $t$，$df=n-2$ |

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

Choose the method based on the data and assumptions, not on which result is more convenient. If parametric assumptions are satisfied, Pearson generally uses more information; when assumptions fail or data are ranked, Spearman is more appropriate.

应根据数据与假设选择方法，而不是根据哪个结果更方便。若参数假设成立，Pearson 通常利用的信息更多；若假设不成立或数据为排名数据，则 Spearman 更合适。

# 6. Categorical Data and Contingency Tables | 分类数据与列联表

## Definition | 定义

A contingency table, frequency table, or two-way table reports observed frequencies classified by two categorical variables. Correlation methods for continuous numerical data are not appropriate for testing whether these classifications are independent.

列联表、频数表或双向表按照两个分类变量列示观测频数。用于连续数值数据的相关分析方法不适合检验这些分类是否独立。

## Hypotheses | 假设

$$
H_0:\mathrm{The\ two\ classifications\ are\ independent}
$$

$$
H_a:\mathrm{The\ two\ classifications\ are\ dependent}
$$

In words, $H_0$ means no relationship; $H_a$ means a relationship exists.

文字表述中，$H_0$ 表示不存在关系；$H_a$ 表示存在关系。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

Independence tests evaluate **categorical classifications**, not linear correlation between continuous variables.

独立性检验评估的是**分类变量之间的关系**，而不是连续变量之间的线性相关。

# 7. Chi-Square Test of Independence | 卡方独立性检验

## Definition | 定义

The chi-square test of independence is a nonparametric test that compares observed cell frequencies with the frequencies expected if the two categorical variables were independent.

卡方独立性检验是一种非参数检验，将各单元格的观测频数与两个分类变量独立时的期望频数进行比较。

## Expected Frequency | 期望频数

$$
E_{ij}=\frac{(\mathrm{Row\ total}_i)(\mathrm{Column\ total}_j)}{N}
$$

## Test Statistic | 检验统计量

$$
\chi^2=\sum_{i=1}^{r}\sum_{j=1}^{c}\frac{(O_{ij}-E_{ij})^2}{E_{ij}}
$$

$$
df=(r-1)(c-1)
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $O_{ij}$ | Observed frequency in row $i$, column $j$ | 第 $i$ 行、第 $j$ 列的观测频数 |
| $E_{ij}$ | Expected frequency under independence | 独立假设下的期望频数 |
| $r$ | Number of row categories | 行类别数量 |
| $c$ | Number of column categories | 列类别数量 |
| $N$ | Total number of observations | 观测总数 |

## Decision Rule | 决策规则

Each cell contribution is nonnegative because deviations are squared. Larger differences between observed and expected frequencies produce a larger $\chi^2$. Therefore, the test has only a **right-tail rejection region**.

由于偏差被平方，每个单元格的贡献均为非负。观测频数与期望频数差异越大，$\chi^2$ 越大。因此，该检验只有**右尾拒绝域**。

Reject $H_0$ when the calculated $\chi^2$ exceeds the right-tail critical value.

当计算得到的 $\chi^2$ 大于右尾临界值时，拒绝 $H_0$。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Expected frequency is based on marginal totals, not an equal split across cells.  
  期望频数根据行列边际合计计算，并非简单地在单元格之间平均分配。
- Degrees of freedom use category counts, not sample size.  
  自由度使用类别数量计算，而不是样本量。
- The test indicates whether a relationship exists; it does not establish causation.  
  该检验只能说明是否存在关系，不能证明因果关系。

# 8. Worked Example: ETF Size and Investment Type | 计算示例：ETF 规模与投资类型

## Given | 已知

| Investment Type | Small | Medium | Large | Total |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| Value | 50 | 110 | 343 | 503 |
| Growth | 42 | 122 | 202 | 366 |
| Blend | 56 | 149 | 520 | 725 |
| Total | 148 | 381 | 1,065 | 1,594 |

## Calculation | 计算

For the small-cap value cell:

以小盘价值型单元格为例：

$$
E_{11}=\frac{(503)(148)}{1{,}594}=46.703
$$

Repeating for all nine cells gives:

对全部九个单元格重复计算：

| Investment Type | Small | Medium | Large |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Value | 46.703 | 120.228 | 336.070 |
| Growth | 33.982 | 87.482 | 244.536 |
| Blend | 67.315 | 173.290 | 484.395 |

$$
\chi^2=32.0803
$$

$$
df=(3-1)(3-1)=4
$$

## Answer | 答案

At $\alpha=5\%$, the critical value is $9.4877$. Because $32.0803>9.4877$, reject independence. ETF size and investment type are statistically related.

在 $\alpha=5\%$ 时，临界值为 $9.4877$。由于 $32.0803>9.4877$，拒绝独立性原假设；ETF 规模与投资类型在统计上存在关系。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

If every observed frequency equaled its expected frequency, $\chi^2$ would equal zero. Any departure from expectation increases the statistic.

若每个观测频数都等于其期望频数，则 $\chi^2=0$。偏离期望频数会增大检验统计量。

# 9. Pearson Residual and Mosaic Plot | Pearson 残差与马赛克图

## Formula | 公式

The standardized residual for a cell is:

单元格的标准化残差为：

$$
R_{ij}=\frac{O_{ij}-E_{ij}}{\sqrt{E_{ij}}}
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $R_{ij}$ | Standardized or Pearson residual | 标准化残差或 Pearson 残差 |

## Interpretation | 解释

- Positive residual: more observations than expected under independence.  
  正残差：实际观测数高于独立假设下的期望数。
- Negative residual: fewer observations than expected.  
  负残差：实际观测数低于期望数。
- Larger absolute residual: greater contribution to the chi-square statistic.  
  残差绝对值越大，对卡方统计量的贡献越大。

In the ETF example, medium-size growth ETFs have $R=3.69$, so they are more numerous than expected. Large-size growth ETFs have $R=-2.72$, so they are less numerous than expected.

在 ETF 示例中，中盘成长型 ETF 的 $R=3.69$，数量高于预期；大盘成长型 ETF 的 $R=-2.72$，数量低于预期。

A mosaic plot visualizes observed-versus-expected patterns and category proportions. It helps identify which cells drive a significant test but does not replace the formal chi-square calculation.

马赛克图可视化观测与期望的差异及各类别比例，有助于识别哪些单元格推动检验显著，但不能替代正式的卡方计算。

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

The chi-square statistic establishes overall dependence; residuals help locate the cells responsible for that result.

卡方统计量判断整体是否存在依赖关系；残差帮助定位主要贡献单元格。

# 10. Complete Test Workflow | 完整检验流程

## Correlation Test | 相关检验

1. State $H_0$ and $H_a$ for population correlation.  
   陈述总体相关系数的 $H_0$ 与 $H_a$。
2. Choose Pearson for suitable numerical data or Spearman for ranks/non-normal data.  
   对合适的数值数据选择 Pearson；对排名或非正态数据选择 Spearman。
3. Calculate $r$ or $r_s$, transform to $t$ when appropriate, and use $df=n-2$.  
   计算 $r$ 或 $r_s$，适用时转换为 $t$，并使用 $df=n-2$。
4. Compare with the correct one- or two-tailed critical value.  
   与正确的单尾或双尾临界值比较。

## Contingency-Table Test | 列联表检验

1. State $H_0$: classifications are independent.  
   陈述 $H_0$：两个分类相互独立。
2. Compute each expected frequency from row total, column total, and $N$.  
   根据行合计、列合计和 $N$ 计算每个期望频数。
3. Sum all cell contributions to obtain $\chi^2$.  
   汇总所有单元格贡献，得到 $\chi^2$。
4. Use $df=(r-1)(c-1)$ and a right-tail critical value.  
   使用 $df=(r-1)(c-1)$ 和右尾临界值。
5. Rejecting $H_0$ means the classifications are related, not that one causes the other.  
   拒绝 $H_0$ 表示两个分类存在关系，但不代表一方导致另一方。

# CFA Memory Points | CFA 记忆重点

## 1. Pearson Correlation Test | Pearson 相关检验

$$
t=\frac{r\sqrt{n-2}}{\sqrt{1-r^2}}
$$

$$
df=n-2
$$

Larger $|r|$ or larger $n$ produces larger $|t|$, all else equal.

其他条件相同，$|r|$ 越大或 $n$ 越大，$|t|$ 越大。

## 2. Spearman Rank Correlation | Spearman 秩相关

$$
r_s=1-\frac{6\sum d_i^2}{n(n^2-1)}
$$

**Pearson = values; Spearman = ranks.**

**Pearson = 原始数值；Spearman = 秩。**

## 3. Chi-Square Independence Test | 卡方独立性检验

$$
E_{ij}=\frac{(\mathrm{Row\ total}_i)(\mathrm{Column\ total}_j)}{N}
$$

$$
\chi^2=\sum_i\sum_j\frac{(O_{ij}-E_{ij})^2}{E_{ij}}
$$

$$
df=(r-1)(c-1)
$$

The rejection region is always in the right tail.

拒绝域始终位于右尾。

## 4. Common Mistakes | 常见错误

- Do not use Pearson correlation for categorical classifications.  
  不要对分类变量使用 Pearson 相关。
- Do not use sample size to calculate contingency-table degrees of freedom.  
  不要使用样本量计算列联表自由度。
- Do not interpret failure to reject zero correlation as proof of independence.  
  无法拒绝零相关，并不能证明变量相互独立。
- Do not infer causation from correlation or dependence.  
  不要根据相关或依赖关系推断因果关系。

## 5. Exam Shortcut | 考试技巧

- Numerical values + normality reasonable $\Rightarrow$ Pearson $t$-test.  
  数值数据 + 正态性合理 $\Rightarrow$ Pearson $t$ 检验。
- Ranks, outliers, or non-normal data $\Rightarrow$ Spearman.  
  排名、异常值或非正态数据 $\Rightarrow$ Spearman。
- Two categorical variables $\Rightarrow$ contingency-table $\chi^2$ test.  
  两个分类变量 $\Rightarrow$ 列联表 $\chi^2$ 检验。
