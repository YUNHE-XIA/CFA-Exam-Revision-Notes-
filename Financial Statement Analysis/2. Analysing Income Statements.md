# Analyzing Income Statements | 利润表分析

## 1. 核心记忆

本章主要解决五个问题：

- Revenue 如何确认；
- Expense 如何确认；
- Non-recurring items 如何分析；
- Basic EPS 和 Diluted EPS 如何计算；
- 如何用 common-size income statement 和 ratios 分析盈利能力。

核心关键词：

- Revenue Recognition
- Expense Recognition
- Capitalization vs Expensing
- Non-Recurring Items
- Discontinued Operations
- Accounting Policy Changes
- Basic EPS
- Diluted EPS
- Common-Size Income Statement
- Gross Profit Margin
- Operating Profit Margin
- Net Profit Margin

---

## 2. Income Statement 的作用

Income Statement 用来分析公司的：

- revenue growth；
- profitability；
- operating performance；
- earnings quality；
- EPS；
- valuation inputs。

记忆：

> Equity analysts 看 growth、profitability、EPS。  
> Credit analysts 看盈利稳定性和偿债能力。

---

## 3. Revenue Recognition | 收入确认

Revenue recognition 的核心原则：

> Revenue is recognized when it is earned, not necessarily when cash is received.

现金收到时间不等于收入确认时间。

常见情况：

| 情况 | 会计处理 |
|---|---|
| 先交付产品，后收现金 | Recognize revenue + accounts receivable |
| 先收现金，后交付产品 | Record unearned revenue / deferred revenue |
| 分期提供服务 | Revenue recognized over time |

---

## 4. Revenue Recognition 的五步法

Revenue recognition standards under IFRS and US GAAP are largely converged.

五步法：

1. Identify the contract with a customer
2. Identify separate performance obligations
3. Determine the transaction price
4. Allocate the transaction price to performance obligations
5. Recognize revenue when performance obligations are satisfied

记忆：

> Contract → Obligation → Price → Allocation → Recognition

---

## 5. Revenue Recognition: Principal vs Agent

这是考试高频。

| Role | Revenue Recognized |
|---|---|
| Principal | Gross amount |
| Agent | Net commission / fee only |

例子：

如果平台卖货价格是 100，成本是 70，其他费用是 10：

### Principal

- Revenue = 100
- COGS = 70
- Gross profit = 30
- Net profit = 20
- Net margin = 20%

### Agent

- Revenue = 30
- COGS = 0
- Gross profit = 30
- Net profit = 20
- Net margin = 67%

记忆：

> Agent revenue 更低，但 margin 更高。  
> Principal revenue 更高，但 margin 更低。

分析师要特别注意电商平台、OTA、外卖平台、marketplace 的 principal-agent mix。

---

## 6. Revenue Recognition: Franchise / Licensing

Franchise business 通常有几类收入：

| Revenue Type | Recognition |
|---|---|
| Company-owned store revenue | Retail sales recognized directly |
| Franchise royalties | Recognize based on franchisee sales percentage |
| Upfront franchise fees | Initially deferred, then recognized over franchise term |
| Supply chain revenue | Recognized upon delivery or shipment |

记忆：

> Franchise company 不确认加盟商门店总销售额，只确认 royalty / fee。

---

## 7. Revenue Recognition: Software / SaaS

软件收入确认取决于客户是否取得软件控制权。

| Model | Revenue Recognition |
|---|---|
| Perpetual / term software license | Usually recognized at point in time |
| Software support and updates | Recognized over contract term |
| Cloud / SaaS subscription | Recognized over time |

记忆：

> License 可能 upfront recognize。  
> SaaS 通常 over time recognize。

---

## 8. Revenue Recognition: Long-Term Contracts

Long-term contracts 可以 over time recognize revenue，如果满足以下条件之一：

- 客户同时接收并消耗服务；
- 公司创造或增强客户控制的资产；
- 公司创造的资产没有 alternative use，且有 enforceable right to payment。

进度衡量方法：

| Method | Example |
|---|---|
| Output method | units completed, milestones |
| Input method | costs incurred / total estimated costs |

记忆：

> Long-term contract revenue 重点看 control 是否持续转移给客户。

---

## 9. Revenue Recognition 的分析影响

分析师要关注：

- revenue 是否 aggressive；
- revenue 是否 conservative；
- revenue 是否可持续；
- revenue mix 是否变化；
- principal-agent mix 是否变化；
- contract asset / contract liability 是否异常；
- deferred revenue 是否增长；
- receivables 是否增长快于 revenue。

记忆：

> Revenue recognition affects growth, margins, profitability, and comparability.

---

## 10. Expense Recognition | 费用确认

Expense recognition 的核心原则：

> Expenses are recognized when economic benefits are consumed.

三种常见模式：

| Model | 中文记忆 | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Matching principle | 收入和相关费用匹配 | COGS |
| Expensing as incurred | 发生时费用化 | Admin costs, repairs |
| Capitalization then depreciation / amortization | 先资本化，再折旧或摊销 | PP&E, software development |

---

## 11. Matching Principle

Matching principle 指：

> Expenses should be recognized in the same period as the related revenue.

典型例子：

- Inventory sold → recognize COGS；
- Sales commission → matched with sales；
- Product cost → first inventory, then COGS。

记忆：

> COGS follows revenue.

---

## 12. Period Costs

Period costs 无法直接和某一项收入匹配，因此通常当期费用化。

常见 period costs：

- administrative salaries；
- management costs；
- IT costs；
- research and development；
- repairs and maintenance。

记忆：

> Period costs are expensed as incurred.

---

## 13. Capitalization vs Expensing

这是本章最重要考点之一。

| Treatment | Current Net Income | Future Net Income | Assets | CFO |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Capitalize | Higher | Lower due to depreciation / amortization | Higher | Higher |
| Expense immediately | Lower | Higher | Lower | Lower |

记忆：

> Capitalizing makes current performance look better.  
> Expensing makes current performance look worse but future trend better.

---

## 14. Capitalizing 的影响

如果公司资本化一项支出：

- current expense lower；
- current net income higher；
- assets higher；
- equity higher；
- depreciation / amortization recognized later；
- CFO usually higher；
- CFI usually lower。

记忆：

> Capitalization shifts expense from today to future periods.

---

## 15. Expensing 的影响

如果公司直接费用化一项支出：

- current expense higher；
- current net income lower；
- assets lower；
- equity lower；
- future depreciation / amortization lower；
- future profit may look better。

记忆：

> Expensing hurts current income but improves future income trend.

---

## 16. Capitalized Interest

当公司建造或取得需要较长时间才能投入使用的资产时，相关 interest cost 通常需要资本化。

Capitalized interest 可能进入：

| Asset Type | Treatment |
|---|---|
| Asset for own use | Included in long-lived asset |
| Asset for sale | Included in inventory |

分析影响：

- capitalized interest appears in investing cash flows；
- expensed interest usually reduces operating cash flow under US GAAP；
- interest coverage ratios should include both expensed and capitalized interest。

记忆：

> For true interest coverage, use total interest = expensed interest + capitalized interest.

---

## 17. Internal Development Costs

Development costs 的处理会影响利润和现金流。

一般逻辑：

| Stage | Treatment |
|---|---|
| Research / conceptual stage | Expense as incurred |
| Development after feasibility established | May be capitalized |

分析师要关注：

- feasibility 判断是否主观；
- capitalized development costs 是否异常增加；
- amortization 是否低于新增 capitalized costs；
- 是否通过资本化提高 earnings。

记忆：

> More capitalization usually means higher current earnings and higher CFO.

---

## 18. Expense Recognition 的分析影响

分析师要关注 estimates：

- bad debt expense；
- warranty expense；
- useful life；
- salvage value；
- depreciation method；
- amortization period；
- impairment assumptions。

记忆：

> Later expense recognition = less conservative。  
> Earlier expense recognition = more conservative。

---

## 19. Non-Recurring Items

分析未来盈利时，需要区分：

- recurring items；
- non-recurring items；
- unusual items；
- infrequent items；
- discontinued operations；
- accounting changes。

记忆：

> Forecasting future earnings requires separating continuing and non-continuing items.

---

## 20. Unusual or Infrequent Items

Unusual or infrequent items may include:

- restructuring charges；
- asset disposal gains / losses；
- impairment charges；
- litigation costs；
- acquisition integration costs；
- major crisis-related costs。

分析原则：

> Do not automatically ignore all unusual items.

原因：

- 有些 unusual items 可能再次发生；
- 有些反映业务真实风险；
- 有些说明 management strategy changes。

---

## 21. Discontinued Operations

Discontinued operations 是公司已经出售或计划出售，并且未来不再参与的 component operation。

报告方式：

- reported separately；
- net of tax；
- shown below income from continuing operations；
- EPS also separately disclosed。

分析记忆：

> Forecast future earnings based on continuing operations, not discontinued operations.

---

## 22. Changes in Accounting Policy vs Estimate

| Type | Treatment |
|---|---|
| Change in accounting policy | Usually retrospective application |
| Change in accounting estimate | Prospective application |

### Retrospective application

意思是：

> Restate prior financial statements as if the new policy had always been used.

### Prospective application

意思是：

> Apply change only to current and future periods.

记忆：

> Policy change looks backward.  
> Estimate change looks forward.

---

## 23. Basic EPS

Basic EPS:

$$
Basic\ EPS = \frac{Net\ Income - Preferred\ Dividends}{Weighted\ Average\ Common\ Shares\ Outstanding}
$$

记忆：

> Basic EPS uses actual shares outstanding.

注意：

- preferred dividends must be subtracted；
- use weighted average shares；
- share repurchases reduce weighted shares；
- new share issuance increases weighted shares；
- stock splits and stock dividends are applied retroactively。

---

## 24. Simple vs Complex Capital Structure

| Capital Structure | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Simple capital structure | No potentially dilutive securities |
| Complex capital structure | Has potentially dilutive securities |

Potentially dilutive securities include:

- convertible preferred stock；
- convertible bonds；
- stock options；
- warrants。

记忆：

> Simple structure: Basic EPS = Diluted EPS.  
> Complex structure: Diluted EPS may be lower.

---

## 25. Diluted EPS

Diluted EPS shows EPS if all dilutive securities were converted or exercised.

Rule:

$$
Diluted\ EPS \leq Basic\ EPS
$$

If a security increases EPS, it is antidilutive and excluded.

记忆：

> Antidilutive securities are not included in diluted EPS.

---

## 26. If-Converted Method

Used for:

- convertible preferred stock；
- convertible debt。

### Convertible Preferred Stock

Assume conversion at beginning of period.

Effects:

- add shares from conversion to denominator；
- add back preferred dividends to numerator。

Formula logic:

$$
Diluted\ EPS =
\frac{Net\ Income}{Weighted\ Average\ Shares + New\ Shares\ from\ Conversion}
$$

---

## 27. Convertible Debt

Assume debt converted at beginning of period.

Effects:

- add shares from conversion to denominator；
- add back after-tax interest expense to numerator。

Formula logic:

$$
Diluted\ EPS =
\frac{Net\ Income + Interest(1-Tax\ Rate)}
{Weighted\ Average\ Shares + New\ Shares\ from\ Conversion}
$$

记忆：

> Convertible debt conversion removes interest expense, so add back after-tax interest.

---

## 28. Treasury Stock Method

Used for:

- stock options；
- warrants。

Assumption:

1. Options are exercised；
2. Company receives cash；
3. Company uses cash to repurchase shares at average market price；
4. Only net new shares are added to denominator。

Incremental shares:

$$
Incremental\ Shares =
Shares\ Issued - Shares\ Repurchased
$$

Where:

$$
Shares\ Repurchased =
\frac{Exercise\ Price \times Options}{Average\ Market\ Price}
$$

记忆：

> Treasury stock method affects denominator only, not net income.

---

## 29. Antidilutive Securities

Antidilutive means including the security would increase EPS.

Rule:

> Antidilutive securities are excluded from diluted EPS.

记忆：

> Diluted EPS can never be higher than Basic EPS.

---

## 30. Common-Size Income Statement

Common-size income statement expresses each line item as a percentage of revenue.

Example:

$$
Common\ Size\ Item = \frac{Income\ Statement\ Item}{Revenue}
$$

用途：

- compare across companies；
- compare across time；
- remove size effect；
- analyze margin structure；
- identify business strategy differences。

记忆：

> Common-size analysis standardizes the income statement.

---

## 31. Income Statement Ratios

### Gross Profit Margin

$$
Gross\ Profit\ Margin = \frac{Gross\ Profit}{Revenue}
$$

Measures:

> How much gross profit is generated from each dollar of sales.

---

### Operating Profit Margin

$$
Operating\ Profit\ Margin = \frac{Operating\ Income}{Revenue}
$$

Measures:

> Profitability from core operations before financing and tax effects.

---

### Pretax Margin

$$
Pretax\ Margin = \frac{Earnings\ Before\ Tax}{Revenue}
$$

Measures:

> Profitability before tax impact.

---

### Net Profit Margin

$$
Net\ Profit\ Margin = \frac{Net\ Income}{Revenue}
$$

Also called:

- Profit Margin；
- Return on Sales。

Measures:

> How much net income is left from each dollar of revenue.

---

## 32. Margin Analysis

Higher margin usually indicates stronger profitability, but analysts must understand why.

High gross margin may reflect:

- strong brand；
- differentiated product；
- pricing power；
- superior technology；
- patent protection。

Low gross margin may reflect:

- commodity-like product；
- price competition；
- cost pressure；
- low-cost volume strategy。

记忆：

> Margin differences often reflect business model and strategy differences.

---

## 33. Exam Traps | 高频陷阱

- Revenue is recognized when earned, not when cash is collected.
- Cash received before delivery creates deferred revenue.
- Agent reports net commission, not gross transaction value.
- Capitalizing expenses increases current profit and assets.
- Expensing immediately reduces current profit but may improve future trend.
- Capitalized interest should be included when analyzing interest coverage.
- Non-recurring items should not always be ignored.
- Discontinued operations should be separated from continuing operations.
- Accounting policy changes are usually retrospective.
- Accounting estimate changes are prospective.
- Basic EPS subtracts preferred dividends.
- Diluted EPS is always less than or equal to Basic EPS.
- Antidilutive securities are excluded.
- Convertible debt requires adding back after-tax interest.
- Options and warrants use treasury stock method.
- Common-size income statement uses revenue as 100%.

---

## 34. Quick Review | 考前速记

- Income statement 分析 growth、profitability、risk 和 valuation inputs。
- Revenue recognition 五步法：contract、obligation、price、allocation、recognition。
- Principal 记 gross revenue，Agent 只记 commission。
- SaaS 通常 over time recognize，license 可能 upfront recognize。
- Expense recognition 三种：matching、expense as incurred、capitalize then depreciate/amortize。
- Capitalize 提高当期利润和资产，expense 降低当期利润。
- Non-recurring items 要判断未来是否会重复。
- Discontinued operations 不应直接用于预测未来 continuing earnings。
- Policy change retrospective，estimate change prospective。
- Basic EPS = income available to common shareholders / weighted average shares。
- Diluted EPS 反映潜在稀释，且不能高于 Basic EPS。
- If-converted method 用于 convertible preferred 和 convertible debt。
- Treasury stock method 用于 options 和 warrants。
- Common-size income statement 把每一项除以 revenue。
- Gross margin 看产品盈利能力。
- Operating margin 看核心经营盈利能力。
- Net margin 看最终盈利能力。
