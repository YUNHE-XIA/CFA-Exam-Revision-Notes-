# 1. Geopolitics and Actors | 地缘政治与行为主体

## Definition | 定义

Geopolitics is the study of how geography affects politics and international relations. It matters to investors because geopolitical relationships affect economic growth, business performance, market volatility, transaction costs, and required returns.

地缘政治研究地理因素如何影响政治与国际关系. 它与投资密切相关, 因为地缘政治关系会影响经济增长, 企业表现, 市场波动, 交易成本和必要收益率.

## State and Non-State Actors | 国家行为主体与非国家行为主体

| Actor | Definition | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| State actor | Controls or exercises authority over national security and country resources | National governments, political organizations, country leaders |
| 国家行为主体 | 控制国家安全和国家资源, 或对其行使权力 | 国家政府, 政治组织, 国家领导人 |
| Non-state actor | Participates in political, economic, or financial affairs without directly controlling national security or resources | NGOs, multinational companies, charities, influential individuals |
| 非国家行为主体 | 参与政治, 经济或金融事务, 但不直接控制国家安全或资源 | NGO, 跨国公司, 慈善机构, 有影响力的个人 |

Relationships form an interconnected network. An actor's behavior toward one ally or adversary can alter its relationships with other actors.

行为主体之间形成相互关联的网络. 一个行为主体对某个盟友或对手的行为, 可能改变其与其他行为主体的关系.

# 2. Cooperation vs Non-Cooperation | 合作与不合作

## Political Cooperation | 政治合作

Cooperation is the process by which countries work together toward shared military, economic, financial, or cultural goals.

合作是各国为实现共同的军事, 经济, 金融或文化目标而共同采取行动的过程.

Political cooperation is the degree to which countries agree on rules and standards governing their interactions.

政治合作是各国就管理相互关系的规则和标准达成一致的程度.

| Cooperative Country | Non-Cooperative Country |
|---|---|
| Reciprocal and standardized rules | Inconsistent or arbitrary rules |
| Harmonized tariffs | Retaliatory tariffs or restrictions |
| Trade, migration, and regulatory agreements | Restricted flows of goods, services, people, and capital |
| Free flow of information and technology | Limited information and technology exchange |
| 合作型国家: 互惠且标准化的规则, 统一关税, 签订协议, 允许信息流动 | 不合作型国家: 规则不一致或随意, 采取报复或限制措施, 限制跨境流动与技术交流 |

## National Interest | 国家利益

National interest is a country's hierarchy of goals and ambitions. Survival-related needs usually rank above non-essential objectives.

国家利益是一个国家目标和抱负的层级结构. 与生存相关的需求通常高于非必要目标.

Key influences include:

主要影响因素包括:

- National security and military needs.
  国家安全与军事需求.
- Access to food, water, energy, and other resources.
  获取食物, 水, 能源和其他资源.
- Economic growth, social stability, and competitiveness.
  经济增长, 社会稳定和竞争力.
- Political leadership and the length of the political cycle.
  政治领导层和政治周期长度.
- Cultural ties and historical relationships.
  文化联系和历史关系.

When objectives conflict, the goal higher in the hierarchy generally dominates. A country may abandon beneficial trade cooperation if national survival or military independence is at stake.

当不同目标发生冲突时, 层级更高的目标通常占主导. 如果国家生存或军事独立受到威胁, 一个国家可能放弃有利的贸易合作.

**Exam point:** A country's position is not fixed. Leadership changes, shocks, and shifting priorities can move it toward cooperation or non-cooperation.

**考试重点:** 一个国家的位置并非固定. 领导层变化, 冲击和优先事项改变, 都可能使其转向合作或不合作.

# 3. Drivers of Cooperation | 合作的驱动因素

## 3.1 Geophysical Resource Endowment | 地球物理资源禀赋

Geophysical resource endowment includes livable geography, climate, food, water, energy, and other resources required for sustainable growth.

地球物理资源禀赋包括适宜居住的地理条件, 气候, 食物, 水, 能源, 以及可持续增长所需的其他资源.

- Resource scarcity can make cooperation essential.
  资源稀缺可能使合作成为必要.
- Resource abundance can provide political leverage.
  资源丰富可以带来政治影响力.
- Unequal distribution of resource benefits can create domestic instability.
  资源收益分配不均可能造成国内不稳定.
- Strategic geography, such as trade routes or transit corridors, can create bargaining power.
  贸易路线或运输走廊等战略地理位置可以形成谈判能力.

## 3.2 Standardization | 标准化

Standardization creates shared protocols for producing, selling, transporting, or using products and services.

标准化为产品与服务的生产, 销售, 运输或使用建立共同规则.

| Type | Purpose | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Regulatory cooperation | Harmonize governance and risk standards | Basel banking standards |
| 监管合作 | 统一治理与风险标准 | Basel 银行监管标准 |
| Process standardization | Reduce transaction costs and delays | SWIFT financial messaging |
| 流程标准化 | 降低交易成本与延迟 | SWIFT 金融报文系统 |
| Operational synchronization | Make physical processes compatible | Standard shipping containers |
| 操作同步 | 使实际操作流程兼容 | 标准化集装箱 |

Standardization facilitates trade and capital flows, supports economies of scale, and raises potential growth and living standards.

标准化促进贸易与资本流动, 支持规模经济, 并提高潜在增长与生活水平.

## 3.3 Soft Power | 软实力

Soft power influences another country's decisions without force or coercion. It can be developed through cultural programs, advertising, travel grants, education, and exchanges.

软实力在不使用武力或胁迫的情况下影响其他国家的决策. 它可以通过文化项目, 广告, 旅行资助, 教育和交流活动形成.

## 3.4 Institutions | 制度

Institutions are established organizations or practices. They can be formal, such as legal systems and universities, or informal, such as customs and social practices.

制度是已经建立的组织或社会惯例. 它可以是法律体系和大学等正式结构, 也可以是习俗和社会惯例等非正式结构.

Strong institutions support government accountability, rule of law, property rights, policy continuity, and durable international cooperation.

强大制度支持政府问责, 法治, 产权, 政策连续性和持久的国际合作.

# 4. Globalization and Nationalism | 全球化与民族主义

## Definition | 定义

Globalization is interaction and integration among people, companies, and governments worldwide. It is marked by cross-border flows of goods, services, capital, currencies, information, jobs, and culture.

全球化是世界各地个人, 企业和政府之间的互动与融合. 其特征是商品, 服务, 资本, 货币, 信息, 就业和文化的跨境流动.

Nationalism promotes domestic economic interests to the exclusion or detriment of other countries. It is associated with limited trade, capital flows, foreign investment, and currency exchange.

民族主义以排除或损害其他国家利益的方式促进本国经济利益. 它通常伴随有限的贸易, 资本流动, 外国投资和货币兑换.

## Geopolitics vs Globalization | 地缘政治与全球化

| Dimension | Political Cooperation | Globalization |
|---|---|---|
| Primary actors | Mainly state actors | Mainly non-state actors |
| 主要行为主体 | 主要是国家行为主体 | 主要是非国家行为主体 |
| Main focus | Rules, treaties, security, tariffs, migration, and regulation | Trade, production, capital, currency, information, and culture |
| 主要关注 | 规则, 条约, 安全, 关税, 移民和监管 | 贸易, 生产, 资本, 货币, 信息和文化 |
| Relationship | Can facilitate or obstruct globalization | Can proceed through private activity even without full political cooperation |
| 相互关系 | 可以促进或阻碍全球化 | 即使缺乏充分政治合作, 私人活动仍可能推动全球化 |

Political cooperation and globalization are correlated but distinct.

政治合作与全球化相关, 但两者并不相同.

## Motivations for Globalization | 全球化动机

### 1. Higher Profits | 更高利润

- Increase sales by accessing new customers.
  通过接触新客户增加销售.
- Reduce labor, tax, production, and supply chain costs.
  降低劳动力, 税收, 生产和供应链成本.
- Achieve economies of scale in capital-intensive industries.
  在资本密集型行业实现规模经济.

### 2. Access to Resources and Markets | 获取资源与市场

- Obtain talent, raw materials, technology, and essential inputs.
  获取人才, 原材料, 技术和必要投入.
- Access foreign markets and investment opportunities.
  获取外国市场和投资机会.

Portfolio investment flows are shorter-term investments in foreign stocks or bonds.

证券投资流是对外国股票或债券进行的较短期投资.

Foreign direct investment, or FDI, is a long-term investment in the productive capacity of a foreign country.

外国直接投资, 即 FDI, 是对外国生产能力进行的长期投资.

### 3. Intrinsic Gains | 内在收益

Intrinsic gains are benefits beyond direct profit, including education, cultural understanding, personal growth, innovation, and productivity improvements.

内在收益是直接利润以外的收益, 包括教育, 文化理解, 个人成长, 创新和生产率提高.

## Additional Benefits | 其他收益

- Greater consumer choice and product quality.
  更多消费者选择和更高产品质量.
- Increased competition and efficiency.
  更高竞争与效率.
- Greater labor mobility and knowledge transfer.
  更高劳动力流动性与知识转移.
- Potentially stronger cross-border trust and lower conflict incentives.
  可能增强跨境信任并降低冲突动机.

## Costs and Risks | 成本与风险

| Cost | Investment Relevance |
|---|---|
| Unequal gains | Some workers, sectors, or countries lose even if aggregate output rises |
| 收益不均 | 即使总产出增加, 某些劳动者, 行业或国家仍可能受损 |
| Lower ESG standards | Production may move to jurisdictions with weaker environmental, social, or governance rules |
| 更低 ESG 标准 | 生产可能转移到环境, 社会或治理规则更弱的地区 |
| Political backlash | Inequality and job losses can fuel nationalism and reduced cooperation |
| 政治反弹 | 不平等与就业损失可能推动民族主义和减少合作 |
| Interdependence | Concentrated supply chains create exposure to foreign disruptions |
| 相互依赖 | 集中的供应链使企业暴露于外国冲击 |
| Transaction and adjustment costs | New restrictions can disrupt established production and distribution systems |
| 交易与调整成本 | 新限制可能扰乱既有生产和分销体系 |

## Responses to Supply Chain Risk | 供应链风险应对

- Reshoring essential production to the home country.
  将必要生产活动回迁本国.
- Reglobalizing through geographically diversified or duplicated supply chains.
  通过地域多元化或重复建设供应链进行再全球化.
- Doubling down in key markets when scale, productivity, infrastructure, and local demand justify continued investment.
  当规模, 生产率, 基础设施和当地需求支持继续投资时, 加大关键市场投入.
- Producing in-country for local customers while maintaining external supply chains.
  在当地为当地客户生产, 同时保留外部供应链.

# 5. International Organizations | 国际组织

## Comparison | 对比

| Organization | Primary Objective | Main Functions | Investment Relevance |
|---|---|---|---|
| IMF | Stability of the international monetary system | Surveillance, policy advice, temporary conditional foreign currency lending for balance of payments problems | Reduces country and systemic risk and may limit contagion |
| IMF | 维护国际货币体系稳定 | 监督, 政策建议, 为国际收支问题提供临时且有条件的外汇贷款 | 降低国家与系统性风险, 并可能限制危机传染 |
| World Bank | Reduce poverty and support environmentally sound growth in developing countries | Development finance, grants, technical assistance, institutional and infrastructure support | Builds foundations for domestic markets and financial systems |
| World Bank | 减少贫困并支持发展中国家的环境友好型增长 | 提供发展融资, 赠款, 技术援助, 制度和基础设施支持 | 建立国内市场与金融体系的基础 |
| WTO | Provide the legal and institutional foundation for global trade | Administer agreements, host negotiations, settle disputes, monitor policies, and provide technical assistance | Creates predictable rules supporting multinational business and investment |
| WTO | 为全球贸易提供法律与制度基础 | 管理协议, 主持谈判, 解决争端, 监督政策并提供技术援助 | 建立可预测规则, 支持跨国经营与投资 |

## 5.1 International Monetary Fund | 国际货币基金组织

The IMF:

IMF:

- Provides a forum for international monetary cooperation.
  提供国际货币合作平台.
- Promotes exchange rate stability and an open international payments system.
  促进汇率稳定与开放的国际支付体系.
- Monitors global, regional, and national economies.
  监督全球, 区域和国家经济.
- Assesses macroeconomic and financial vulnerabilities.
  评估宏观经济与金融脆弱性.
- Provides temporary foreign currency loans with policy conditions and safeguards.
  在附带政策条件和保障措施的情况下提供临时外汇贷款.

**Exam point:** IMF lending addresses balance of payments and external financing problems, and normally includes policy conditionality and ongoing monitoring.

**考试重点:** IMF 贷款用于处理国际收支和外部融资问题, 通常附带政策条件并接受持续监督.

## 5.2 World Bank | 世界银行

The World Bank supports developing countries by:

世界银行通过以下方式支持发展中国家:

- Strengthening governments and public administration.
  加强政府与公共管理.
- Supporting legal systems, property rights, and contract enforcement.
  支持法律体系, 产权和合同执行.
- Developing financial systems and infrastructure.
  发展金融体系与基础设施.
- Combating corruption.
  打击腐败.
- Providing research, advice, finance, grants, and technical expertise.
  提供研究, 建议, 融资, 赠款和技术专长.

The International Bank for Reconstruction and Development, or IBRD, lends mainly to developing countries and finances itself substantially through highly rated bonds.

国际复兴开发银行, 即 IBRD, 主要向发展中国家贷款, 并在很大程度上通过发行高评级债券融资.

The International Development Association, or IDA, provides low or no-interest loans and grants to the poorest countries.

国际开发协会, 即 IDA, 向最贫困国家提供低息或无息贷款与赠款.

## 5.3 World Trade Organization | 世界贸易组织

The WTO:

WTO:

- Administers multilateral trade agreements.
  管理多边贸易协议.
- Provides a platform for trade negotiations.
  提供贸易谈判平台.
- Settles disputes among members.
  解决成员之间的争端.
- Reviews and promotes transparent trade policies.
  审查并促进透明的贸易政策.
- Provides training and technical cooperation to developing countries.
  向发展中国家提供培训与技术合作.

The WTO was established in 1995 and replaced GATT as the main global trade organization. GATT had governed multilateral trade from 1948 to 1995 and remains the WTO's principal treaty for trade in goods.

WTO 成立于 1995 年, 并取代 GATT 成为主要全球贸易组织. GATT 在 1948 年至 1995 年间管理多边贸易, 目前仍是 WTO 关于商品贸易的主要条约.

**Memory rule:** IMF = monetary stability. World Bank = development and poverty reduction. WTO = global trade rules.

**记忆规则:** IMF = 货币稳定. World Bank = 发展与减贫. WTO = 全球贸易规则.

# 6. Country Behavior Archetypes | 国家行为原型

Countries can be mapped along 2 axes:

国家可以沿 2 条轴进行分类:

1. Political cooperation vs non-cooperation.
   政治合作 vs 不合作.
2. Globalization vs nationalism.
   全球化 vs 民族主义.

| Archetype | Cooperation | Globalization | Core Characteristics |
|---|---|---|---|
| Autarky | Low | Low | Political self-sufficiency, little external trade or finance, state control of strategic industries |
| 自给自足 | 低 | 低 | 寻求政治自给, 很少开展对外贸易或融资, 国家控制战略行业 |
| Hegemony | Low or selective | High | Regional or global leader uses political or economic influence to control resources or set rules |
| 霸权主义 | 低或选择性 | 高 | 区域或全球领导者利用政治或经济影响力控制资源或制定规则 |
| Multilateralism | High | High | Mutually beneficial trade, extensive rules harmonization, and globally integrated private firms |
| 多边主义 | 高 | 高 | 互利贸易, 广泛规则统一, 私营企业深度融入全球体系 |
| Bilateralism | High | Lower or selective | One-at-a-time agreements between 2 countries, often without a broader multilateral framework |
| 双边主义 | 高 | 较低或选择性 | 2 个国家逐一签订协议, 通常不依赖更广泛的多边框架 |

Regionalism lies between bilateralism and multilateralism. A group of countries cooperates internally and may discriminate against outsiders.

区域主义位于双边主义与多边主义之间. 一组国家在内部合作, 同时可能区别对待外部国家.

## Risk Trade-Offs | 风险权衡

- Greater cooperation can reduce incentives for partners to attack one another because the economic and political costs are higher.
  更高合作可以提高冲突的经济与政治成本, 从而降低合作伙伴相互攻击的动机.
- Greater interdependence also creates more channels through which disruptions can spread.
  更高相互依赖也会创造更多冲击传播渠道.
- Autarky reduces some external exposures but sacrifices trade, capital, technology, competition, and growth opportunities.
  自给自足减少部分外部风险敞口, 但牺牲贸易, 资本, 技术, 竞争和增长机会.
- A hegemon may stabilize a system by enforcing standards, but shifts in its power can increase conflict risk.
  霸权国家可以通过执行标准稳定体系, 但其权力变化可能提高冲突风险.

**Exam point:** A country's movement within the framework can matter more than its current quadrant. A multilateral actor moving toward withdrawal may create more risk than a stable hegemon moving toward cooperation.

**考试重点:** 一个国家在框架中的移动可能比其当前象限更重要. 正在退出合作的多边主义国家, 可能比稳定转向合作的霸权国家产生更大风险.

# 7. Geopolitical Risk | 地缘政治风险

Geopolitical risk is the risk that tensions or actions between state and non-state actors disrupt the normal and peaceful course of international relations.

地缘政治风险是国家与非国家行为主体之间的紧张关系或行动扰乱正常和平国际关系的风险.

Risk tends to rise when the geographic or political foundations of relationships shift.

当地缘或政治关系基础发生变化时, 风险通常上升.

## Three Types of Geopolitical Risk | 3 类地缘政治风险

| Type | Timing | Description | Examples |
|---|---|---|---|
| Event risk | Date known in advance | Risk surrounding scheduled events or milestones | Elections, referendums, legislation, political anniversaries |
| 事件风险 | 日期提前已知 | 围绕预定事件或里程碑产生的风险 | 选举, 公投, 立法, 政治纪念日 |
| Exogenous risk | Sudden and unexpected | Unanticipated shock affecting cooperation or globalization | Invasion, uprising, natural disaster |
| 外生风险 | 突然且意外 | 影响合作或全球化的非预期冲击 | 入侵, 起义, 自然灾害 |
| Thematic risk | Known and evolving | Persistent risk that develops over time | Climate change, cyber threats, migration, populism, terrorism |
| 主题风险 | 已知且逐步演变 | 随时间发展的持续风险 | 气候变化, 网络威胁, 移民, 民粹主义, 恐怖主义 |

Black swan risk is a rare, difficult-to-predict event with a major impact. It is generally associated with sudden high-velocity risk.

黑天鹅风险是罕见, 难以预测且影响重大的事件. 它通常与突然发生的高速度风险相关.

**Exam trap:** Event risk is known in advance, but its outcome, probability, velocity, and impact are not necessarily predictable.

**考试陷阱:** 事件风险的日期提前已知, 但其结果, 概率, 速度和影响不一定可预测.

# 8. Tools of Geopolitics | 地缘政治工具

Tools that increase cross-border flows generally support cooperation. Tools that restrict goods, services, capital, labor, information, or currency flows generally escalate non-cooperation.

增加跨境流动的工具通常支持合作. 限制商品, 服务, 资本, 劳动力, 信息或货币流动的工具通常加剧不合作.

## 8.1 National Security Tools | 国家安全工具

National security tools influence or coerce actors through effects on territory, people, resources, or borders.

国家安全工具通过影响领土, 人口, 资源或边界来影响或迫使行为主体采取行动.

Examples include:

包括:

- Armed conflict.
  武装冲突.
- Threat of force.
  武力威胁.
- Espionage.
  间谍活动.
- Military alliances and collective security arrangements.
  军事联盟与集体安全安排.

Armed conflict can destroy infrastructure and capital stock, reduce current and potential output, cause migration, disrupt trade, and affect neighboring countries.

武装冲突可以破坏基础设施和资本存量, 降低当前与潜在产出, 导致人口迁移, 扰乱贸易, 并影响邻国.

National security tools can be cooperative. Military alliances may deter conflict and support shared security goals.

国家安全工具也可以具有合作性. 军事联盟可以威慑冲突并支持共同安全目标.

## 8.2 Economic Tools | 经济工具

Cooperative economic tools include:

合作型经济工具包括:

- Multilateral and regional trade agreements.
  多边和区域贸易协议.
- Harmonized tariff and regulatory rules.
  统一关税和监管规则.
- Common markets and common currencies.
  共同市场与共同货币.

Non-cooperative economic tools include:

不合作型经济工具包括:

- Nationalization of private industries.
  将私营行业国有化.
- Voluntary export restraints.
  自愿出口限制.
- Domestic content requirements.
  国内成分要求.
- Trade restrictions and embargoes.
  贸易限制与禁运.

## 8.3 Financial Tools | 金融工具

Cooperative financial tools include free currency exchange, cross-border payments, foreign investment, and international lending.

合作型金融工具包括自由货币兑换, 跨境支付, 外国投资和国际贷款.

Non-cooperative financial tools include capital controls, restrictions on local currency access, limits on foreign investment, asset freezes, and financial sanctions.

不合作型金融工具包括资本管制, 限制获取本币, 限制外国投资, 冻结资产和金融制裁.

Cooperative tools can still create vulnerability. For example, dependence on a dominant funding currency can expose countries to liquidity shortages when that currency's monetary policy tightens.

合作型工具仍可能产生脆弱性. 例如, 对主导融资货币的依赖, 可能使其他国家在该货币收紧政策时面临流动性短缺.

## 8.4 Multifaceted Tools | 多层面工具

Political, security, economic, and financial tools often operate together.

政治, 安全, 经济和金融工具通常共同发挥作用.

Cabotage is the right of a foreign firm to transport passengers or goods within another country. Allowing cabotage requires coordination on security, regulation, and economic policy.

沿岸运输权是外国企业在另一国家境内运输乘客或货物的权利. 允许沿岸运输需要在安全, 监管和经济政策方面进行协调.

**Exam matching:**

**考试配对:**

- Espionage -> National security tool.
  间谍活动 -> 国家安全工具.
- Nationalization -> Economic tool.
  国有化 -> 经济工具.
- Free cross-border currency exchange -> Financial tool.
  自由跨境货币兑换 -> 金融工具.

# 9. Geopolitical Risk Assessment | 地缘政治风险评估

Investors assess 3 dimensions together:

投资者需要同时评估 3 个维度:

1. Likelihood.
   发生可能性.
2. Velocity.
   影响速度.
3. Size and nature of impact.
   影响规模与性质.

## 9.1 Likelihood | 发生可能性

Likelihood is the probability that a risk occurs.

发生可能性是风险发生的概率.

Factors affecting likelihood include:

影响发生可能性的因素包括:

- Political stability and institutional strength.
  政治稳定性与制度强度.
- Economic needs and resource dependence.
  经济需求与资源依赖.
- Leaders' motivations and national priorities.
  领导者动机与国家优先事项.
- Current cooperation and globalization.
  当前合作与全球化程度.
- Movement within the country behavior framework.
  国家行为框架中的移动方向.

Greater cooperation may reduce the likelihood of direct conflict, but greater interconnectedness increases exposure to risks originating elsewhere.

更高合作可能降低直接冲突发生的可能性, 但更高相互关联会增加对其他地区风险的敞口.

## 9.2 Velocity | 影响速度

Velocity is the pace at which risk affects a portfolio.

影响速度是风险影响投资组合的速度.

| Velocity | Typical Manifestation | Likely Investor Response |
|---|---|---|
| High or short-term | Sudden volatility, repricing, and flight to quality | Tactical hedge, liquidity management, or buying opportunity |
| 高速度或短期 | 突然波动, 重新定价和避险交易 | 战术对冲, 流动性管理或把握买入机会 |
| Medium-term | Higher costs, impaired processes, and sector-specific valuation effects | Adjust sector or security exposures |
| 中期 | 成本上升, 流程受损和特定行业估值影响 | 调整行业或证券敞口 |
| Low or long-term | Persistent ESG, growth, and structural effects | Adjust strategic asset allocation |
| 低速度或长期 | 持续 ESG, 增长与结构性影响 | 调整战略资产配置 |

One risk can have multiple velocities. An event may cause immediate market volatility and later produce persistent economic or regulatory effects.

同一风险可以具有多种速度. 一个事件可能立即造成市场波动, 并在之后产生持续的经济或监管影响.

## 9.3 Impact | 影响

Impact should be assessed by both size and breadth.

影响应当同时从规模与广度评估.

- Discrete impact: Concentrated in one company or sector.
  离散影响: 集中于单个公司或行业.
- Broad impact: Affects a sector, country, region, or global economy.
  广泛影响: 影响一个行业, 国家, 区域或全球经济.
- Downturns can magnify the effect of geopolitical shocks.
  经济下行可能放大地缘政治冲击.

A high-likelihood but low-impact risk may not merit extensive resources. A low-likelihood but high-impact risk may justify scenario planning even if continuous monitoring is unnecessary.

高可能性但低影响的风险可能不值得投入大量资源. 低可能性但高影响的风险可能值得制定情景计划, 即使无需持续监控.

# 10. Scenario Analysis and Signposts | 情景分析与路标

## Scenario Analysis | 情景分析

Scenario analysis evaluates portfolio outcomes across different possible states of the world.

情景分析评估不同可能世界状态下的投资组合结果.

A useful structure includes:

有效结构包括:

- Base case.
  基准情景.
- Upside case.
  上行情景.
- Downside case.
  下行情景.
- Likelihood, velocity, and impact for each case.
  每个情景的发生可能性, 速度和影响.
- Expected market recovery path and second-order effects.
  预期市场恢复路径和二阶影响.

Qualitative scenarios describe possible developments. Quantitative scenarios measure sensitivity to interest rates, asset prices, exchange rates, or historical extreme events.

定性情景描述可能的发展路径. 定量情景衡量对利率, 资产价格, 汇率或历史极端事件的敏感度.

Scenario analysis is preferable to a single point forecast because geopolitical risks rarely develop linearly.

由于地缘政治风险很少线性发展, 情景分析优于单点预测.

**Behavioral risk:** Groupthink suppresses dissent and creativity, weakening scenario design.

**行为风险:** 群体思维会压制异议与创造力, 从而削弱情景设计.

## Signposts | 路标

A signpost is an indicator, market level, data point, policy change, or event that signals whether a risk is becoming more or less likely.

路标是显示风险正在变得更可能或更不可能的指标, 市场水平, 数据点, 政策变化或事件.

| Signal | Interpretation | Response |
|---|---|---|
| Green | Low likelihood, velocity, or impact | No immediate action |
| 绿色 | 低可能性, 低速度或低影响 | 无需立即行动 |
| Amber | Medium or rising risk | Increase monitoring and preparation |
| 黄色 | 中等或上升风险 | 加强监控与准备 |
| Red | High or rapidly rising risk | Execute or prepare an action plan |
| 红色 | 高风险或快速上升风险 | 执行或准备行动计划 |

Good signposts are tied to the assumptions in a scenario. Investors should emphasize policy changes over political rhetoric because implemented policy produces more durable economic and market effects.

有效路标应与情景中的核心假设相关. 投资者应当更加重视政策变化, 而不是政治言论, 因为已实施政策会产生更持久的经济与市场影响.

Potential warning combinations include:

潜在警告组合包括:

- High inflation plus deteriorating employment -> Political unrest risk.
  高通胀加就业恶化 -> 政治动荡风险.
- Pegged currency plus rapidly falling export revenue -> Exchange rate regime risk.
  固定汇率加出口收入快速下降 -> 汇率制度风险.

# 11. Investment Impact | 投资影响

## Macroeconomic and Market Channels | 宏观经济与市场渠道

Geopolitical risk can affect:

地缘政治风险可以影响:

- Economic and potential growth.
  经济增长与潜在增长.
- Inflation and interest rates.
  通胀与利率.
- Exchange rates and capital flows.
  汇率与资本流动.
- Commodity prices and supply chains.
  大宗商品价格与供应链.
- Market volatility and liquidity.
  市场波动与流动性.
- Business revenue, costs, and investment.
  企业收入, 成本和投资.

High-velocity risk often causes prompt changes in commodities, currencies, equities, and bond yields, together with flight-to-quality behavior.

高速度风险通常导致大宗商品, 货币, 股票和债券收益率迅速变化, 并伴随避险行为.

Low-velocity risk can reduce revenue, raise costs, weaken growth, and impair valuations over a longer period.

低速度风险可能在较长时期内减少收入, 提高成本, 削弱增长并降低估值.

## Valuation and Required Return | 估值与必要收益率

Persistent geopolitical exposure can increase the risk premium and discount rate required by investors.

持续地缘政治风险敞口可能提高投资者要求的风险溢价和折现率.

Higher required return -> Lower present value, all else equal.

必要收益率提高 -> 其他条件不变时, 现值下降.

Countries, sectors, or firms exposed to frequent disruption may therefore trade at a valuation discount.

因此, 经常受到冲击的国家, 行业或企业可能以估值折价交易.

## Portfolio Actions | 投资组合行动

### Asset Allocation | 资产配置

- Adjust geographic, sector, currency, and asset class exposure.
  调整地域, 行业, 货币和资产类别敞口.
- Incorporate geopolitical assumptions into capital market expectations.
  将地缘政治假设纳入资本市场预期.
- Allocate more cautiously to areas with persistent military or institutional risk.
  对持续存在军事或制度风险的地区采取更谨慎配置.

### Security Selection and Risk Models | 证券选择与风险模型

- Include geopolitical exposure in multifactor analysis.
  在多因子分析中加入地缘政治风险敞口.
- Assess supply chain concentration and production alternatives.
  评估供应链集中度与替代生产能力.
- Adjust valuation inputs, confidence intervals, or scenario weights.
  调整估值输入, 置信区间或情景权重.

Diversified production can create more points of exposure, increasing likelihood, while reducing the impact of disruption because alternative locations remain available.

多元化生产会增加风险接触点并提高发生可能性, 但由于存在替代地点, 可以降低冲击影响.

### Hedging and Tactical Response | 对冲与战术应对

- Use hedges when a known event could create material volatility.
  当已知事件可能造成重大波动时使用对冲.
- Maintain liquidity for rapid portfolio adjustment.
  保持流动性以便迅速调整投资组合.
- Treat dislocations as buying opportunities only when consistent with risk tolerance and time horizon.
  只有在符合风险承受能力和投资期限时, 才将市场错位视为买入机会.

The same event can require different actions. A long-horizon investor may exploit a temporary sell-off, while an investor approaching retirement may need to protect terminal wealth.

同一事件可能需要不同的行动. 长期投资者可能利用暂时性抛售机会, 而接近退休的投资者可能需要保护终值财富.

**Core rule:** The appropriate response depends on investor objectives, risk tolerance, liquidity needs, and time horizon.

**核心规则:** 适当反应取决于投资者目标, 风险承受能力, 流动性需求和投资期限.

# CFA Memory Points | CFA 记忆重点

## 1. Two Axes | 2 条轴

Political cooperation vs non-cooperation, and globalization vs nationalism.

政治合作 vs 不合作, 以及全球化 vs 民族主义.

## 2. Four Archetypes | 4 种原型

- Autarky = Low cooperation + Low globalization.
  自给自足 = 低合作 + 低全球化.
- Hegemony = Selective cooperation + High globalization and influence.
  霸权主义 = 选择性合作 + 高全球化与高影响力.
- Multilateralism = High cooperation + High globalization.
  多边主义 = 高合作 + 高全球化.
- Bilateralism = Cooperation through one-at-a-time 2-country agreements.
  双边主义 = 通过逐一签订的 2 国协议开展合作.

## 3. Three Organizations | 3 个国际组织

- IMF = International monetary stability and conditional balance of payments lending.
  IMF = 国际货币稳定与附带条件的国际收支贷款.
- World Bank = Development, poverty reduction, infrastructure, and institutions.
  World Bank = 发展, 减贫, 基础设施和制度建设.
- WTO = Global trade rules, negotiation, monitoring, and dispute settlement.
  WTO = 全球贸易规则, 谈判, 监督与争端解决.

## 4. Three Tools | 3 类工具

National security, economic, and financial tools.

国家安全, 经济和金融工具.

## 5. Three Risks | 3 类风险

- Event risk = Known date.
  事件风险 = 日期已知.
- Exogenous risk = Sudden and unexpected.
  外生风险 = 突然且意外.
- Thematic risk = Known and evolving.
  主题风险 = 已知且逐步演变.

## 6. Three Assessment Dimensions | 3 个评估维度

Likelihood + Velocity + Size and nature of impact.

发生可能性 + 影响速度 + 影响规模与性质.

## 7. Velocity Shortcut | 速度判断技巧

- High velocity -> Market volatility and flight to quality -> Tactical response.
  高速度 -> 市场波动与避险交易 -> 战术反应.
- Medium velocity -> Sector and company effects -> Adjust security or sector exposure.
  中期速度 -> 行业与公司影响 -> 调整证券或行业敞口.
- Low velocity -> Structural growth and ESG effects -> Strategic asset allocation.
  低速度 -> 结构性增长与 ESG 影响 -> 战略资产配置.

## 8. Core Investment Relationship | 核心投资关系

Higher geopolitical risk -> Higher required return and discount rate -> Lower valuation, all else equal.

更高地缘政治风险 -> 更高必要收益率和折现率 -> 其他条件不变时估值更低.

## 9. Common Mistakes | 常见错误

- Do not equate political cooperation with globalization. They are related but distinct.
  不要将政治合作等同于全球化. 两者相关但不同.
- Do not assume multilateralism eliminates risk. Interdependence creates transmission channels.
  不要假设多边主义消除风险. 相互依赖会创造风险传导渠道.
- Do not assess likelihood alone. Combine likelihood, velocity, and impact.
  不要只评估发生可能性. 应同时结合可能性, 速度和影响.
- Do not confuse politics with policy. Durable market effects usually come from implemented policy.
  不要混淆政治言论与政策. 持久市场影响通常来自已实施政策.
