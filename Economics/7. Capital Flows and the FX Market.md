# 1. Foreign Exchange Market | 外汇市场

## Definition and Functions | 定义与功能

The foreign exchange, or FX, market is the global market in which currencies are traded against one another. It is the world's largest financial market and operates 24 hours per business day through electronic networks across time zones.

外汇市场, 即 FX market, 是不同货币相互交易的全球市场. 它是全球最大的金融市场, 通过跨时区电子网络在每个工作日连续运行 24 小时.

The FX market:

外汇市场:

- Facilitates international trade in goods and services.
  促进商品与服务的国际贸易.
- Facilitates cross-border capital flows.
  促进跨境资本流动.
- Converts foreign-currency cash flows into domestic-currency values.
  将外币现金流转换为本币价值.
- Allows participants to hedge currency risk.
  允许参与者对冲货币风险.
- Provides opportunities for investment and speculation.
  提供投资与投机机会.
- Connects pricing across global asset markets.
  连接全球资产市场的定价.

Even a domestic portfolio is exposed indirectly through multinational corporate earnings, foreign competition, imported inputs, and foreign demand for domestic assets.

即使是国内投资组合, 也会通过跨国企业盈利, 外国竞争, 进口投入品和外国投资者对国内资产的需求间接受到汇率影响.

# 2. Currency Codes and Exchange Rate Quotes | 货币代码与汇率报价

## Currency vs Exchange Rate | 货币与汇率

An individual currency can be held by itself. An exchange rate always involves 2 currencies and is the price of one currency in terms of another.

单一货币可以被独立持有. 汇率始终涉及 2 种货币, 表示一种货币相对于另一种货币的价格.

Currencies use standardized 3-letter codes, such as USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, CHF, CAD, AUD, and CNY.

货币使用标准化 3 字母代码, 例如 USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, CHF, CAD, AUD 和 CNY.

## A/B Convention | A/B 报价规则

In this module:

在本模块中:

$$
A/B=\frac{\mathrm{units\ of\ currency}\ A}{1\ \mathrm{unit\ of\ currency}\ B}
$$

| Position | Currency | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| $A$ | Price currency or quote currency | Number of units quoted |
| $A$ | 标价货币或报价货币 | 报价中显示数量的货币 |
| $B$ | Base currency | Always 1 unit |
| $B$ | 基础货币 | 始终为 1 单位 |

For example:

例如:

$$
USD/EUR=1.1700
$$

This means EUR 1 costs USD 1.1700. EUR is the base currency and USD is the price currency.

这表示 EUR 1 的价格为 USD 1.1700. EUR 是基础货币, USD 是标价货币.

## Interpretation of a Quote Change | 报价变化解释

If $A/B$ rises:

如果 $A/B$ 上升:

- Base currency $B$ appreciates against price currency $A$.
  基础货币 $B$ 相对于标价货币 $A$ 升值.
- Price currency $A$ depreciates against base currency $B$.
  标价货币 $A$ 相对于基础货币 $B$ 贬值.
- More units of $A$ are needed to buy 1 unit of $B$.
  购买 1 单位 $B$ 需要更多 $A$.

If $A/B$ falls, the directions reverse.

如果 $A/B$ 下降, 方向相反.

**Memory rule:** Quote rises -> Base rises.

**记忆规则:** 报价上升 -> 基础货币升值.

# 3. Direct and Indirect Quotes | 直接报价与间接报价

For an investor in a specified domestic country:

对于位于指定本国的投资者:

- Direct quote: Domestic currency is the price currency.
  直接报价: 本币为标价货币.
- Indirect quote: Domestic currency is the base currency.
  间接报价: 本币为基础货币.

For a Mexico-based investor:

对于墨西哥投资者:

$$
MXN/USD=18.8590
$$

This is a direct quote because MXN, the domestic currency, is the price currency.

这是直接报价, 因为本币 MXN 是标价货币.

The reciprocal quote is:

倒数报价为:

$$
USD/MXN=\frac{1}{18.8590}=0.0530
$$

This is an indirect quote for the Mexico-based investor.

对墨西哥投资者而言, 这是间接报价.

**Exam point:** Direct or indirect depends on the location of the investor. Professional FX markets usually follow market conventions instead of labeling quotes direct or indirect.

**考试重点:** 直接或间接报价取决于投资者所在地. 专业外汇市场通常遵循市场报价惯例, 而不使用直接或间接标签.

# 4. Bid and Offer Quotes | 买价与卖价

A dealer provides a 2-sided quote for the base currency:

交易商针对基础货币提供双向报价:

- Bid: Dealer buys the base currency.
  Bid: 交易商买入基础货币.
- Offer or ask: Dealer sells the base currency.
  Offer 或 ask: 交易商卖出基础货币.

For:

对于:

$$
CHF/EUR=1.1583-1.1585
$$

- The dealer buys EUR 1 and pays CHF 1.1583.
  交易商买入 EUR 1 并支付 CHF 1.1583.
- The dealer sells EUR 1 and receives CHF 1.1585.
  交易商卖出 EUR 1 并收取 CHF 1.1585.

The bid is lower than the offer because the dealer buys the base currency low and sells it high.

买价低于卖价, 因为交易商低价买入基础货币并高价卖出.

## Inverting a 2-Sided Quote | 双向报价取倒数

If:

如果:

$$
A/B=b-o
$$

where $b$ is the bid and $o$ is the offer, then:

其中 $b$ 是买价, $o$ 是卖价, 则:

$$
B/A=\frac{1}{o}-\frac{1}{b}
$$

The sides reverse when the quote is inverted.

报价取倒数时, 两侧顺序反转.

**Exam trap:** Never invert bid and offer independently without switching their positions.

**考试陷阱:** 对买价和卖价取倒数时, 必须同时交换两者位置.

# 5. FX Market Participants | 外汇市场参与者

## Sell Side | 卖方

The sell side mainly consists of large FX dealing banks that quote prices and provide FX products to clients.

卖方主要由大型外汇交易银行组成, 它们向客户报价并提供外汇产品.

Tier-1 global banks benefit from:

第一层全球银行受益于:

- Large investments in trading technology.
  大规模交易技术投资.
- Broad global client networks.
  广泛全球客户网络.
- Ability to match buyers and sellers internally.
  在内部匹配买方与卖方的能力.
- Deep liquidity and competitive pricing.
  深度流动性与有竞争力的定价.

Smaller regional banks may outsource FX services to larger banks.

较小区域银行可能将外汇服务外包给大型银行.

## Buy Side | 买方

| Participant | Main Activities |
|---|---|
| Corporate accounts | Trade settlement, foreign borrowing, foreign investment, and cross-border M&A |
| 企业账户 | 贸易结算, 外币借款, 外国投资和跨境并购 |
| Real money accounts | Institutional investing with limited leverage, including mutual funds, pensions, insurers, ETFs, and endowments |
| 实际资金账户 | 杠杆受限的机构投资, 包括共同基金, 养老金, 保险公司, ETF 和捐赠基金 |
| Leveraged accounts | Active risk-taking for profit, including hedge funds, proprietary traders, CTAs, and algorithmic traders |
| 杠杆账户 | 为盈利主动承担风险, 包括对冲基金, 自营交易者, CTA 和算法交易者 |
| Retail accounts | Individuals and small investors |
| 零售账户 | 个人与小型投资者 |
| Governments, central banks, and sovereign wealth funds | Policy intervention, reserve management, and public investment |
| 政府, 中央银行和主权财富基金 | 政策干预, 储备管理和公共投资 |

Most participants combine hedging and speculative motives. Public-sector participants may also pursue policy objectives.

大多数参与者同时具有对冲与投机动机. 公共部门参与者还可能追求政策目标.

**Exam point:** Corporations and trade flows are important, but financial institutions and investment flows account for a much larger share of FX turnover.

**考试重点:** 企业与贸易流动很重要, 但金融机构与投资流动在外汇交易量中的占比更大.

# 6. FX Products | 外汇产品

| Product | Settlement and Use |
|---|---|
| Spot | Exchange currencies at the current spot rate with near-term settlement |
| 即期交易 | 按当前即期汇率交换货币并在近期结算 |
| Outright forward | Agree today on an exchange rate for settlement at a future date |
| 远期交易 | 今天约定未来日期结算的汇率 |
| FX swap | Combine a spot FX transaction with an offsetting forward FX transaction |
| 外汇掉期 | 将一笔即期外汇交易与一笔方向相反的远期外汇交易组合 |
| FX option | Provides a right, but not an obligation, to exchange currencies at specified terms |
| 外汇期权 | 提供按规定条件兑换货币的权利, 但不承担义务 |

FX swaps are widely used for hedging and obtaining foreign currency funding.

外汇掉期广泛用于对冲与获取外币融资.

**Exam trap:** An FX swap combines spot and forward transactions. It is not the same as a multi-period currency swap.

**考试陷阱:** 外汇掉期组合即期与远期交易. 它不同于多期货币互换.

# 7. Nominal vs Real Exchange Rates | 名义汇率与实际汇率

## Nominal Exchange Rate | 名义汇率

The nominal spot exchange rate is the market price of one currency in terms of another. It is directly quoted and traded in the FX market.

名义即期汇率是一种货币以另一种货币表示的市场价格. 它在外汇市场中被直接报价与交易.

## Real Exchange Rate | 实际汇率

The real exchange rate adjusts the nominal exchange rate for relative price levels. It is an analytical index, not a directly traded market price.

实际汇率根据相对价格水平调整名义汇率. 它是分析指数, 不是可以直接交易的市场价格.

Let:

设:

| Symbol | Definition |
|---|---|
| $S_{d/f}$ | Nominal spot rate, domestic currency per 1 unit of foreign currency |
| $S_{d/f}$ | 名义即期汇率, 每 1 单位外币对应的本币数量 |
| $P_f$ | Foreign price level in foreign currency |
| $P_f$ | 以外币表示的外国价格水平 |
| $P_d$ | Domestic price level in domestic currency |
| $P_d$ | 以本币表示的国内价格水平 |
| $R_{d/f}$ | Real exchange rate |
| $R_{d/f}$ | 实际汇率 |

$$
R_{d/f}=S_{d/f}\frac{P_f}{P_d}
$$

## Directional Interpretation | 方向解释

For a quote expressed as domestic currency per unit of foreign currency:

对于以每单位外币对应本币数量表示的报价:

- Higher $S_{d/f}$ -> Higher $R_{d/f}$.
  更高 $S_{d/f}$ -> 更高 $R_{d/f}$.
- Higher $P_f$ -> Higher $R_{d/f}$.
  更高 $P_f$ -> 更高 $R_{d/f}$.
- Higher $P_d$ -> Lower $R_{d/f}$.
  更高 $P_d$ -> 更低 $R_{d/f}$.

A higher $R_{d/f}$ means foreign goods are more expensive in real domestic terms and domestic purchasing power over foreign goods is lower.

更高的 $R_{d/f}$ 表示外国商品以国内实际价值衡量更昂贵, 国内收入对外国商品的购买力更低.

A lower $R_{d/f}$ means foreign goods are cheaper in real domestic terms and domestic purchasing power is higher.

更低的 $R_{d/f}$ 表示外国商品以国内实际价值衡量更便宜, 国内购买力更高.

**Memory rule:** Real exchange rate = Real price paid for foreign goods. Higher real price -> Lower purchasing power.

**记忆规则:** 实际汇率 = 购买外国商品支付的实际价格. 实际价格越高 -> 购买力越低.

## Real Exchange Rate Change | 实际汇率变化

For multiplicative percentage changes:

对于乘法形式的百分比变化:

$$
1+g_R=\frac{(1+g_S)(1+\pi_f)}{1+\pi_d}
$$

where $g_R$ is the change in the real exchange rate, $g_S$ is the change in the nominal exchange rate, $\pi_f$ is foreign inflation, and $\pi_d$ is domestic inflation.

其中 $g_R$ 是实际汇率变化, $g_S$ 是名义汇率变化, $\pi_f$ 是外国通胀率, $\pi_d$ 是国内通胀率.

## Example | 例题

Given:

已知:

- Nominal domestic-currency-per-foreign-currency rate rises by 10%.
  每单位外币对应本币的名义汇率上升 10%.
- Foreign price level rises by 5%.
  外国价格水平上升 5%.
- Domestic price level rises by 2%.
  国内价格水平上升 2%.

Calculation:

计算:

$$
1+g_R=\frac{1.10\times1.05}{1.02}=1.13235
$$

$$
g_R=13.24\%
$$

The real exchange rate rises by approximately 13.24%, so domestic purchasing power over foreign goods falls by approximately 13.24%.

实际汇率上升约 13.24%, 因此国内收入对外国商品的购买力下降约 13.24%.

# 8. Purchasing Power Parity | 购买力平价

Purchasing power parity, or PPP, states that nominal exchange rates should adjust so identical goods or baskets have the same price across countries.

购买力平价, 即 PPP, 表示名义汇率应当调整, 使相同商品或商品篮子在不同国家具有相同价格.

Persistent deviations occur because:

持续偏离的原因包括:

- Goods and services are not identical.
  商品与服务并不相同.
- Consumption baskets differ.
  消费篮子不同.
- Many goods and services are non-tradable.
  许多商品与服务不可贸易.
- Shipping costs, taxes, and trade barriers exist.
  存在运输成本, 税收与贸易壁垒.
- Capital flows are major drivers of nominal exchange rates.
  资本流动是名义汇率的重要驱动因素.

PPP describes a long-run equilibrium concept but is a weak short- to medium-term exchange rate forecasting tool.

PPP 描述长期均衡概念, 但对短期至中期汇率的预测能力较弱.

Real exchange rates help assess purchasing power and competitiveness, but they also have a weak record in predicting future nominal rates.

实际汇率有助于评估购买力与竞争力, 但在预测未来名义汇率方面表现也较弱.

# 9. Percentage Currency Changes | 货币百分比变化

Suppose the exchange rate is $A/B$, where $A$ is the price currency and $B$ is the base currency.

假设汇率为 $A/B$, 其中 $A$ 是标价货币, $B$ 是基础货币.

Let $S_0$ be the initial quote and $S_1$ be the ending quote.

设 $S_0$ 为期初报价, $S_1$ 为期末报价.

## Change in Base Currency B | 基础货币 B 的变化

$$
g_B=\frac{S_1}{S_0}-1
$$

- $g_B>0$ -> $B$ appreciates against $A$.
  $g_B>0$ -> $B$ 相对于 $A$ 升值.
- $g_B<0$ -> $B$ depreciates against $A$.
  $g_B<0$ -> $B$ 相对于 $A$ 贬值.

## Change in Price Currency A | 标价货币 A 的变化

Use the inverted quote:

使用倒数报价:

$$
g_A=\frac{S_0}{S_1}-1
$$

- $g_A>0$ -> $A$ appreciates against $B$.
  $g_A>0$ -> $A$ 相对于 $B$ 升值.
- $g_A<0$ -> $A$ depreciates against $B$.
  $g_A<0$ -> $A$ 相对于 $B$ 贬值.

## Example | 例题

USD/EUR increases from 1.1500 to 1.2000.

USD/EUR 从 1.1500 上升至 1.2000.

EUR is the base currency:

EUR 是基础货币:

$$
g_{EUR}=\frac{1.2000}{1.1500}-1=4.35\%
$$

EUR appreciates by 4.35% against USD.

EUR 相对于 USD 升值 4.35%.

USD is the price currency:

USD 是标价货币:

$$
g_{USD}=\frac{1.1500}{1.2000}-1=-4.17\%
$$

USD depreciates by 4.17% against EUR.

USD 相对于 EUR 贬值 4.17%.

**Exam trap:** One currency's appreciation percentage is not equal in magnitude to the other currency's depreciation percentage because the calculations use reciprocal denominators.

**考试陷阱:** 一种货币的升值百分比不等于另一种货币贬值百分比的绝对值, 因为两者使用倒数报价和不同分母.

# 10. The Impossible Trinity | 不可能三角

An ideal currency regime would provide:

理想汇率制度希望同时实现:

1. Credibly fixed exchange rates.
   可信的固定汇率.
2. Full currency convertibility and free capital movement.
   完全货币可兑换与资本自由流动.
3. Fully independent monetary policy.
   完全独立的货币政策.

A country cannot achieve all 3 simultaneously.

一个国家无法同时实现全部 3 项.

## Fixed Rate with Free Capital Mobility | 固定汇率与资本自由流动

If domestic interest rates fall below foreign rates, capital flows out. To defend the currency, the central bank sells foreign reserves and buys domestic currency. The domestic money supply contracts and interest rates rise back toward foreign rates.

如果国内利率低于外国利率, 资本外流. 为维护本币, 中央银行出售外汇储备并买入本币. 国内货币供应量收缩, 利率回升至接近外国利率.

Independent monetary easing is therefore neutralized.

因此, 独立货币宽松被抵消.

## Floating Rate with Free Capital Mobility | 浮动汇率与资本自由流动

A domestic rate cut can cause currency depreciation. Depreciation supports exports and reduces imports, reinforcing expansionary monetary policy.

国内降息可能导致本币贬值. 贬值支持出口并减少进口, 从而强化扩张性货币政策.

**Core trade-off:** More exchange rate stability and convertibility -> Less monetary independence.

**核心权衡:** 更高汇率稳定性与可兑换性 -> 更低货币政策独立性.

# 11. Exchange Rate Regimes | 汇率制度

Exchange rate regimes range from fully fixed arrangements to independently floating currencies. Actual regimes form a spectrum and may change over time.

汇率制度从完全固定安排到独立浮动货币不等. 实际制度形成一个连续谱, 并可能随时间变化.

## Regime Comparison | 制度对比

| Regime | Exchange Rate Flexibility | Monetary Independence | Key Feature |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Dollarization | Minimal | None | Uses another country's currency as legal tender |
| 美元化 | 极低 | 无 | 使用另一国家货币作为法定货币 |
| Monetary union | Minimal within union | No national policy | Members share a currency and jointly determine monetary policy |
| 货币联盟 | 联盟内部极低 | 无独立国家政策 | 成员使用共同货币并共同决定货币政策 |
| Currency board | Very low | Very limited | Domestic monetary base fully backed by foreign reserves at a legislated rate |
| 货币发行局 | 很低 | 非常有限 | 国内货币基础按法定汇率由外汇储备完全支持 |
| Fixed parity | Low | Limited | Peg with narrow intervention band and discretionary reserves |
| 固定平价 | 低 | 有限 | 采用狭窄干预区间, 储备水平由政策决定 |
| Target zone | Low to moderate | More than fixed parity | Wider fixed band around parity |
| 目标区间 | 低至中等 | 高于固定平价 | 平价周围设置更宽固定区间 |
| Crawling peg | Gradual movement | Limited | Parity adjusted in small periodic steps |
| 爬行盯住 | 渐进变化 | 有限 | 平价按小幅度定期调整 |
| Crawling band | Gradually increasing flexibility | Increasing | Central parity or band changes over time |
| 爬行区间 | 灵活性逐渐增加 | 逐渐增加 | 中央平价或区间随时间改变 |
| Managed float | Substantial | Substantial | Market rate with discretionary intervention and no explicit target |
| 管理浮动 | 较高 | 较高 | 市场定价, 但存在无明确目标的相机干预 |
| Independent float | Highest | Highest | Market-determined rate with relatively infrequent intervention |
| 独立浮动 | 最高 | 最高 | 汇率由市场决定, 干预相对较少 |

# 12. Fixed Arrangements | 固定汇率安排

## 12.1 Dollarization | 美元化

A country adopts another country's currency as its medium of exchange and unit of account.

一个国家采用另一国家货币作为交换媒介与记账单位.

Advantages:

优点:

- Imports the currency credibility of the issuing country.
  引入货币发行国的货币可信度.
- Prevents domestic monetization of government debt.
  防止国内通过货币化为政府债务融资.
- May support trade and capital flows by increasing stability.
  可以通过提高稳定性支持贸易与资本流动.

Costs:

成本:

- Gives up independent monetary policy.
  放弃独立货币政策.
- Does not import the issuing country's sovereign creditworthiness or deposit insurance.
  不会引入货币发行国的主权信用或存款保险.
- Gives seigniorage to the foreign currency issuer.
  将铸币税收益交给外国货币发行者.

## 12.2 Monetary Union | 货币联盟

Members share legal tender and jointly determine monetary policy through a common central bank.

成员使用共同法定货币, 并通过共同中央银行联合决定货币政策.

A monetary union can confer currency credibility but does not automatically confer fiscal discipline or creditworthiness.

货币联盟可以带来货币可信度, 但不会自动带来财政纪律或信用水平.

## 12.3 Currency Board | 货币发行局

A currency board legally commits to exchange domestic currency for a foreign anchor at a fixed rate. The domestic monetary base must be 100% backed by foreign reserves.

货币发行局依法承诺按固定汇率将本币兑换为外国锚定货币. 国内货币基础必须由外汇储备提供 100% 支持.

Consequences:

结果:

- Monetary base expands and contracts with trade and capital flows.
  货币基础随贸易与资本流动扩张或收缩.
- Conventional lender-of-last-resort capacity is restricted.
  常规最后贷款人能力受到限制.
- Domestic monetary policy is highly constrained.
  国内货币政策受到高度约束.
- Currency credibility is generally higher than under a simple fixed parity.
  货币可信度通常高于简单固定平价.
- The authority can earn seigniorage on reserve assets.
  货币当局可以从储备资产获得铸币税收益.

## 12.4 Fixed Parity | 固定平价

A fixed parity pegs the currency to a single foreign currency or a basket, typically within a narrow band.

固定平价将本币盯住单一外国货币或货币篮子, 通常允许在狭窄区间内波动.

Unlike a currency board:

与货币发行局不同:

- There is no binding legislative commitment to maintain the parity.
  不存在维持平价的法定强制承诺.
- Foreign reserve backing is discretionary rather than 100% of the monetary base.
  外汇储备支持由政策决定, 而不是货币基础的 100%.
- The central bank can perform traditional functions, including lender of last resort.
  中央银行可以履行包括最后贷款人在内的传统职能.

Credibility depends on sufficient reserves and willingness to defend the parity. A speculative attack can become self-fulfilling if markets expect reserves to be exhausted.

可信度取决于充足储备与维护平价的意愿. 如果市场预期储备将耗尽, 投机攻击可能自我实现.

---

# 13. Intermediate and Floating Regimes | 中间与浮动制度

## 13.1 Target Zone | 目标区间

A target zone maintains a fixed central parity with wider intervention bands than a conventional fixed parity. Wider bands give the monetary authority greater policy discretion.

目标区间在中央平价周围设置比传统固定平价更宽的干预区间. 更宽区间给予货币当局更大政策裁量权.

## 13.2 Passive vs Active Crawling Peg | 被动与主动爬行盯住

- Passive crawl: Exchange rate adjusts frequently to keep pace with actual inflation.
  被动爬行: 汇率频繁调整以跟随实际通胀.
- Active crawl: Future small exchange rate changes are announced in advance to influence inflation expectations.
  主动爬行: 提前宣布未来小幅汇率变化, 以影响通胀预期.

## 13.3 Crawling Band | 爬行区间

A crawling band gradually changes or widens around a central parity. It can provide a gradual exit from a fixed regime when a country wants more flexibility but lacks the credibility or infrastructure for a free float.

爬行区间围绕中央平价逐渐变化或扩大. 当一个国家希望提高灵活性, 但缺乏实行自由浮动所需的可信度或基础设施时, 它可以提供逐步退出固定制度的路径.

## 13.4 Managed Float | 管理浮动

The exchange rate is primarily market determined, but authorities intervene to pursue trade balance, price stability, employment, or other objectives. The target is usually not explicit.

汇率主要由市场决定, 但货币当局会为贸易平衡, 价格稳定, 就业或其他目标进行干预. 目标通常不公开.

Managed float is sometimes called dirty floating.

管理浮动有时被称为 dirty floating.

## 13.5 Independent Float | 独立浮动

The market determines the exchange rate, allowing substantial monetary policy independence. Authorities may still intervene occasionally.

市场决定汇率, 从而提供较高货币政策独立性. 货币当局仍可能偶尔干预.

**Exam point:** Regime labels are not rigid. Even independently floating currencies may be subject to occasional intervention or temporary regime shifts.

**考试重点:** 制度标签并非绝对. 即使独立浮动货币也可能受到偶尔干预或临时制度切换.

# 14. Historical Perspective | 历史视角

## Classical Gold Standard | 古典金本位制

Currencies were fixed to gold. Trade surpluses brought gold inflows, expanded the money supply, raised prices, and eventually reduced exports. Trade deficits produced the reverse adjustment.

货币与黄金固定挂钩. 贸易盈余带来黄金流入, 扩张货币供应, 提高价格, 并最终减少出口. 贸易赤字产生相反调整.

This price-specie-flow mechanism was self-adjusting but prevented independent monetary policy.

这种价格-铸币流动机制可以自动调整, 但限制独立货币政策.

## Bretton Woods | Bretton Woods 体系

Bretton Woods established fixed parities with periodic realignments after World War II. Persistent imbalance led to official appreciation or depreciation.

Bretton Woods 在第二次世界大战后建立固定平价与定期调整制度. 持续失衡会导致官方升值或贬值.

## Post-1973 Flexible Rates | 1973 年后的浮动汇率

Most industrial countries moved toward flexible rates. Exchange rates proved more volatile than expected because FX is a liquid, forward-looking asset market driven heavily by investment and speculation.

多数工业国家转向浮动汇率. 汇率波动超过预期, 因为外汇市场是高度流动且前瞻的资产市场, 并受到投资与投机的重要驱动.

# 15. Exchange Rates, Trade, and Capital Flows | 汇率, 贸易与资本流动

## Balance Identity | 平衡关系

A country's trade balance must be matched by an equal and opposite capital account balance.

一个国家的贸易余额必须由金额相等且方向相反的资本账户余额对应.

- Trade deficit -> Capital account surplus.
  贸易赤字 -> 资本账户盈余.
- Trade surplus -> Capital account deficit.
  贸易盈余 -> 资本账户赤字.

Let:

设:

| Symbol | Definition |
|---|---|
| $X$ | Exports |
| $X$ | 出口 |
| $M$ | Imports |
| $M$ | 进口 |
| $S$ | Private saving |
| $S$ | 私人储蓄 |
| $I$ | Investment in productive capacity |
| $I$ | 生产能力投资 |
| $T$ | Taxes net of transfers |
| $T$ | 扣除转移支付后的税收 |
| $G$ | Government expenditure |
| $G$ | 政府支出 |

$$
X-M=(S-I)+(T-G)
$$

## Interpretation | 解释

A trade surplus means national saving exceeds domestic investment. The country uses excess saving to acquire financial claims on foreigners.

贸易盈余表示国民储蓄超过国内投资. 该国使用超额储蓄获取对外国的金融债权.

A trade deficit means national saving is insufficient to fund domestic investment. The country must borrow from foreigners or sell assets to them.

贸易赤字表示国民储蓄不足以为国内投资融资. 该国必须向外国借款或向外国出售资产.

**Exam trap:** A trade-deficit country receives net capital inflows. It does not use excess saving to buy foreign assets.

**考试陷阱:** 贸易赤字国家获得净资本流入. 它不会使用超额储蓄购买外国资产.

## Short-to-Medium Term | 短期至中期

Capital flow expectations and financial market adjustments dominate. Investors rapidly sell currencies expected to depreciate and buy currencies expected to appreciate.

资本流动预期与金融市场调整占主导. 投资者会迅速卖出预期贬值货币并买入预期升值货币.

In a floating regime, exchange rates can adjust rapidly to limit prospective capital flows.

在浮动制度下, 汇率可以迅速调整以限制潜在资本流动.

In a fixed regime, the central bank offsets private capital flows with reserve transactions. Interest rates and other asset prices adjust until the peg changes or is abandoned.

在固定制度下, 中央银行通过储备交易抵消私人资本流动. 利率与其他资产价格进行调整, 直到平价改变或被放弃.

## Long Term | 长期

Trade flows, expenditure, saving, and goods prices become more important as real economic decisions adjust.

随着实际经济决策进行调整, 贸易流动, 支出, 储蓄和商品价格变得更加重要.

**Core relationship:** Capital flows are the primary short-to-medium-term exchange rate driver. Trade and real-sector adjustment matter more over the long term.

**核心关系:** 资本流动是短期至中期汇率的主要驱动因素. 贸易与实际部门调整在长期更重要.

# 16. Exchange Rate Effects on Trade | 汇率对贸易的影响

For a domestic currency:

对于本币:

## Depreciation | 贬值

- Domestic goods become cheaper for foreign buyers.
  国内商品对外国买家而言更便宜.
- Foreign goods become more expensive for domestic buyers.
  外国商品对国内买家而言更昂贵.
- Exports tend to rise and imports tend to fall.
  出口倾向增加, 进口倾向减少.
- Trade balance tends to improve over time.
  贸易余额倾向随时间改善.

## Appreciation | 升值

- Domestic goods become more expensive for foreign buyers.
  国内商品对外国买家而言更昂贵.
- Foreign goods become cheaper for domestic buyers.
  外国商品对国内买家而言更便宜.
- Exports tend to fall and imports tend to rise.
  出口倾向减少, 进口倾向增加.
- Trade balance tends to weaken over time.
  贸易余额倾向随时间恶化.

Actual outcomes depend on demand responsiveness, production capacity, contracts, and adjustment lags.

实际结果取决于需求反应程度, 生产能力, 合同和调整时滞.

# 17. Capital Restrictions | 资本限制

Capital restrictions are policies designed to limit or redirect capital inflows or outflows.

资本限制是用于限制或改变资本流入与流出方向的政策.

## Benefits of Free Capital Movement | 资本自由流动的收益

- Capital moves toward its highest expected return.
  资本流向预期收益率最高的用途.
- Countries can invest beyond the limits of domestic saving.
  国家可以进行超过国内储蓄能力的投资.
- Foreign direct investment can raise productive capacity and growth.
  外国直接投资可以提高生产能力与增长.
- Foreign firms can transfer technology, skills, management practices, and supplier networks.
  外国企业可以转移技术, 技能, 管理实践和供应商网络.
- Foreign competition can improve domestic efficiency.
  外国竞争可以提高国内效率.

Costs can include domestic firm exit, dependence on foreign financing, and exposure to sudden capital flight.

成本可能包括国内企业退出, 对外国融资的依赖, 以及突然资本外逃风险.

---

# 18. Objectives of Capital Restrictions | 资本限制目标

Governments may impose restrictions to:

政府可能为以下目标实施限制:

- Prevent or slow capital flight during a macroeconomic crisis.
  在宏观经济危机期间防止或减缓资本外逃.
- Reduce destabilizing short-term portfolio inflows.
  减少造成不稳定的短期证券投资流入.
- Limit currency appreciation that harms domestic competitiveness.
  限制损害国内竞争力的本币升值.
- Maintain a fixed exchange rate or external balance.
  维持固定汇率或外部平衡.
- Regain monetary policy independence.
  恢复货币政策独立性.
- Protect strategic industries, such as defense and telecommunications.
  保护国防与电信等战略行业.
- Pursue employment or regional development objectives.
  追求就业或区域发展目标.
- Keep capital domestically available for taxation and government financing.
  将资本留在国内以便征税与政府融资.
- Maintain lower domestic interest rates and borrowing costs.
  维持较低国内利率与借款成本.
- Direct credit toward preferred domestic uses.
  将信贷引导至优先国内用途.

Capital controls and fixed exchange rate targets can be complementary because controls reduce flows that would otherwise undermine the peg and domestic monetary policy.

资本管制与固定汇率目标可以相互补充, 因为管制会减少可能破坏汇率平价与国内货币政策的资本流动.

---

# 19. Forms of Capital Restrictions | 资本限制形式

## Inflow Restrictions | 流入限制

- Limits on foreign ownership or investment in specified industries.
  限制外国所有权或对特定行业的投资.
- Taxes on foreign investment returns or transactions.
  对外国投资收益或交易征税.
- Mandatory unremunerated reserve requirements.
  强制无息准备金要求.
- Quantity ceilings or approval requirements for foreign borrowing.
  对外国借款实施数量上限或审批要求.

## Outflow Restrictions | 流出限制

- Limits on residents purchasing foreign assets.
  限制居民购买外国资产.
- Restrictions on repatriating capital, interest, profits, royalties, or license fees.
  限制汇出资本, 利息, 利润, 特许权使用费或许可费.
- Deadlines requiring foreign investment income to be repatriated.
  要求外国投资收入在规定期限内汇回.
- Prohibitions or taxes on specified cross-border asset transactions.
  禁止特定跨境资产交易或对其征税.

## Implementation Methods | 实施方式

| Method | Example |
|---|---|
| Price control | Transaction tax or tax on international investment returns |
| 价格控制 | 交易税或国际投资收益税 |
| Quantity control | Ceiling on foreign borrowing or investment |
| 数量控制 | 外国借款或投资上限 |
| Administrative control | Government approval required for specified transactions |
| 行政控制 | 特定交易需要政府审批 |
| Prohibition | Outright ban on selected asset transactions |
| 禁止措施 | 直接禁止特定资产交易 |

# 20. Costs and Effectiveness of Capital Restrictions | 资本限制的成本与有效性

## Costs | 成本

- Significant administration, monitoring, and enforcement expense.
  显著行政, 监督和执行成本.
- Participants develop avoidance methods, requiring broader rules.
  市场参与者会寻找规避方式, 从而要求扩大规则范围.
- Controls can delay necessary macroeconomic adjustment.
  管制可能延迟必要宏观经济调整.
- Controls can impede private-sector adaptation.
  管制可能阻碍私人部门适应.
- Negative investor perceptions can raise the cost of foreign funding.
  负面投资者认知可能提高外国融资成本.
- Restrictions can reduce long-term capital inflows and market accessibility.
  限制可能减少长期资本流入和市场可进入性.

## Effectiveness | 有效性

Inflow controls generally need comprehensive coverage and forceful implementation to prevent circumvention.

流入管制通常需要全面覆盖与强力实施, 才能防止规避.

Outflow controls during crises have mixed results. They may provide only temporary relief, but in some cases they create time for financial and corporate restructuring.

危机期间流出管制的效果不一. 它们可能只提供暂时缓解, 但在某些情况下可以为金融与企业重组争取时间.

It is difficult to isolate control effects from other policies, such as prudential regulation, monetary tightening, or increased exchange rate flexibility.

管制效果难以与审慎监管, 货币紧缩或提高汇率灵活性等其他政策的影响区分.

# 21. Capital Control Case Logic | 资本管制案例逻辑

During a crisis:

危机期间:

Capital outflow -> Currency depreciation pressure -> Higher interest rates may be needed to defend the currency -> Domestic economy weakens.

资本外流 -> 本币贬值压力 -> 维护本币可能需要更高利率 -> 国内经济减弱.

Outflow controls can interrupt this sequence:

流出管制可以中断这一过程:

Outflow controls -> Reduced speculative outflow -> More exchange rate stability -> Greater room to cut rates -> Time for restructuring.

流出管制 -> 减少投机性资本外流 -> 更高汇率稳定性 -> 更大降息空间 -> 获得重组时间.

Potential long-term cost:

潜在长期成本:

Lower investor confidence -> Reduced index accessibility and foreign capital -> Higher long-term funding cost.

更低投资者信心 -> 更低指数可进入性与外国资本流入 -> 更高长期融资成本.

# 22. Exam Decision Framework | 考试判断框架

## Step 1: Parse the Quote | 第 1 步: 解析报价

For $A/B$, label $A$ as price currency and $B$ as base currency.

对于 $A/B$, 将 $A$ 标记为标价货币, 将 $B$ 标记为基础货币.

## Step 2: Interpret Direction | 第 2 步: 解释方向

- Quote rises -> Base appreciates.
  报价上升 -> 基础货币升值.
- Quote falls -> Base depreciates.
  报价下降 -> 基础货币贬值.

## Step 3: Select the Correct Percentage Formula | 第 3 步: 选择正确百分比公式

- Asked about base currency -> Ending quote divided by beginning quote.
  询问基础货币 -> 期末报价除以期初报价.
- Asked about price currency -> Beginning quote divided by ending quote.
  询问标价货币 -> 期初报价除以期末报价.

## Step 4: For Real Exchange Rates, Identify Domestic and Foreign | 第 4 步: 对实际汇率识别本国与外国

Use domestic currency per foreign currency, multiply by foreign prices, and divide by domestic prices.

使用每单位外币对应的本币数量, 乘以外国价格, 再除以国内价格.

## Step 5: Identify the Regime Constraint | 第 5 步: 识别制度约束

Ask how much exchange rate flexibility, convertibility, reserve backing, and monetary independence exist.

判断汇率灵活性, 可兑换性, 储备支持与货币政策独立性的程度.

## Step 6: Link Trade and Capital Accounts | 第 6 步: 连接贸易与资本账户

- Trade deficit -> Capital account surplus.
  贸易赤字 -> 资本账户盈余.
- Trade surplus -> Capital account deficit.
  贸易盈余 -> 资本账户赤字.

## Step 7: Evaluate Capital Controls | 第 7 步: 评估资本管制

Identify whether the government targets inflows or outflows, the intended policy objective, and the administrative and credibility costs.

识别政府限制的是流入还是流出, 政策目标是什么, 以及行政与可信度成本.

# CFA Memory Points | CFA 记忆重点

## 1. Quote Direction | 报价方向

For $A/B$, $A$ is the price currency and $B$ is the base currency.

对于 $A/B$, $A$ 是标价货币, $B$ 是基础货币.

Quote rises -> Base currency appreciates.

报价上升 -> 基础货币升值.

## 2. Reciprocal Quote | 倒数报价

$$
B/A=\frac{1}{A/B}
$$

For a 2-sided quote, invert and reverse the bid and offer.

对双向报价取倒数时, 还必须交换买价与卖价位置.

## 3. Real Exchange Rate | 实际汇率

$$
R_{d/f}=S_{d/f}\frac{P_f}{P_d}
$$

Higher real exchange rate -> Foreign goods are more expensive -> Domestic purchasing power is lower.

更高实际汇率 -> 外国商品更昂贵 -> 国内购买力更低.

## 4. Percentage Change | 百分比变化

For $A/B$:

对于 $A/B$:

$$
g_B=\frac{S_1}{S_0}-1
$$

$$
g_A=\frac{S_0}{S_1}-1
$$

The appreciation and depreciation percentages are not equal in magnitude.

升值与贬值百分比的绝对值并不相等.

## 5. Impossible Trinity | 不可能三角

Fixed exchange rate + Full convertibility + Independent monetary policy cannot coexist.

固定汇率 + 完全可兑换 + 独立货币政策无法同时存在.

## 6. Regime Ladder | 制度阶梯

Dollarization or monetary union -> Currency board -> Fixed parity -> Target zone -> Crawling peg or band -> Managed float -> Independent float.

美元化或货币联盟 -> 货币发行局 -> 固定平价 -> 目标区间 -> 爬行盯住或区间 -> 管理浮动 -> 独立浮动.

Moving right generally means more exchange rate flexibility and more monetary independence.

向右移动通常表示更高汇率灵活性与更高货币政策独立性.

## 7. Trade-Capital Mirror | 贸易与资本镜像关系

$$
X-M=(S-I)+(T-G)
$$

Trade deficit -> Capital account surplus. Trade surplus -> Capital account deficit.

贸易赤字 -> 资本账户盈余. 贸易盈余 -> 资本账户赤字.

## 8. Time Horizon | 时间范围

Capital flows dominate exchange rate movements in the short to medium term. Trade flows and real adjustment matter more in the long term.

资本流动主导短期至中期汇率变化. 贸易流动与实际调整在长期更重要.

## 9. Capital Restrictions | 资本限制

Main objectives include preventing capital flight, limiting destabilizing inflows, protecting strategic sectors, supporting a peg, and regaining monetary independence.

主要目标包括防止资本外逃, 限制不稳定流入, 保护战略行业, 支持固定汇率, 以及恢复货币政策独立性.

## 10. Common Mistakes | 常见错误

- Do not interpret an increase in $A/B$ as appreciation of $A$.
  不要将 $A/B$ 上升解释为 $A$ 升值.
- Do not confuse a real exchange rate with a directly traded market quote.
  不要将实际汇率与可直接交易的市场报价混淆.
- Do not assume dollarization imports the anchor country's creditworthiness.
  不要假设美元化会引入锚定货币发行国的信用水平.
- Do not assume an independent float means zero intervention.
  不要假设独立浮动表示完全没有干预.
- Do not treat capital controls as costless or permanently effective.
  不要将资本管制视为无成本或永久有效.
