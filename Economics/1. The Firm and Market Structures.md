# 1. Revenue, Cost, and Economic Profit | 收入, 成本与经济利润

## Core Formulas | 核心公式

$$
TR=P\times Q
$$

$$
AR=\frac{TR}{Q}=P
$$

$$
MR=\frac{\Delta TR}{\Delta Q}
$$

$$
MC=\frac{\Delta TC}{\Delta Q}
$$

$$
ATC=\frac{TC}{Q}
$$

$$
AVC=\frac{TVC}{Q}
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $P$ | Price per unit | 单位价格 |
| $Q$ | Quantity | 产量或销量 |
| $TR$ | Total revenue | 总收入 |
| $AR$ | Average revenue | 平均收入 |
| $MR$ | Marginal revenue | 边际收入 |
| $TC$ | Total cost | 总成本 |
| $TVC$ | Total variable cost | 总可变成本 |
| $TFC$ | Total fixed cost | 总固定成本 |
| $ATC$ | Average total cost | 平均总成本 |
| $AVC$ | Average variable cost | 平均可变成本 |
| $MC$ | Marginal cost | 边际成本 |

## Economic Profit vs. Accounting Profit | 经济利润与会计利润

$$
C_E=C_A+OC
$$

$$
\pi_E=TR-C_E
$$

$$
\pi_A=TR-C_A
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $C_E$ | Total economic cost | 总经济成本 |
| $C_A$ | Total accounting cost | 总会计成本 |
| $OC$ | Implicit opportunity cost | 隐含机会成本 |
| $\pi_E$ | Economic profit | 经济利润 |
| $\pi_A$ | Accounting profit | 会计利润 |

Economic cost includes the opportunity cost of all factors of production. Opportunity cost is the value of the next best alternative forgone.

Economic cost 包括所有生产要素的机会成本. Opportunity cost 是放弃的次优选择的价值.

Zero economic profit means the firm earns normal profit and covers all opportunity costs. It does not imply zero accounting profit.

经济利润为 0 表示企业获得 normal profit 并覆盖全部机会成本. 这不表示会计利润为 0.

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Economic profit is lower than accounting profit when implicit opportunity costs are positive.
- 当隐含机会成本为正时, 经济利润低于会计利润.
- Normal profit is included in economic cost.
- Normal profit 已包含在经济成本中.

# 2. Perfect vs. Imperfect Competition | 完全竞争与不完全竞争

## Revenue Relationships | 收入关系

| Feature | Perfect Competition | Imperfect Competition |
|---|---|---|
| Firm demand curve | Horizontal and perfectly elastic | Downward sloping |
| Pricing power | None | Some or considerable |
| Revenue relationship | $P=AR=MR$ | $P=AR>MR$ |
| Total revenue | Linear, with slope equal to price | In the source's linear-demand case, initially rises, reaches a maximum, then falls |

| 特征 | Perfect Competition | Imperfect Competition |
|---|---|---|
| 企业需求曲线 | 水平且完全弹性 | 向右下方倾斜 |
| 定价权 | 无 | 一定或较强 |
| 收入关系 | $P=AR=MR$ | $P=AR>MR$ |
| 总收入 | 线性增长, 斜率等于价格 | 在原文的线性需求案例中先上升, 达到最大值, 然后下降 |

Under perfect competition, the firm is a price taker and each additional unit increases total revenue by exactly the market price.

在 perfect competition 下, 企业是 price taker. 每多销售一单位产品, 总收入增加的金额正好等于市场价格.

Under imperfect competition, selling another unit requires a lower price. Because the lower price generally applies to prior units as well, marginal revenue is below price.

在 imperfect competition 下, 企业必须降低价格才能多销售一单位产品. 因为较低价格通常也适用于此前销售的单位, 所以边际收入低于价格.

## Total Revenue and Elasticity | 总收入与弹性

For a downward-sloping linear demand curve:

对于向右下方倾斜的线性需求曲线:

| Demand Region | Marginal Revenue | Total Revenue |
|---|---:|---|
| Elastic | $MR>0$ | Increases as quantity increases |
| Unit elastic | $MR=0$ | Is maximized |
| Inelastic | $MR<0$ | Decreases as quantity increases |

| 需求区间 | 边际收入 | 总收入 |
|---|---:|---|
| 富有弹性 | $MR>0$ | 随产量增加而上升 |
| 单位弹性 | $MR=0$ | 达到最大值 |
| 缺乏弹性 | $MR<0$ | 随产量增加而下降 |

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

An imperfectly competitive firm will not choose an output in the inelastic portion of demand because reducing output would raise revenue and lower cost.

Imperfectly competitive firm 不会选择需求曲线缺乏弹性的区间进行生产, 因为减少产量可以同时提高收入并降低成本.

# 3. Profit Maximization | 利润最大化

## General Rule | 一般规则

The profit-maximizing output satisfies both conditions:

利润最大化的产量必须同时满足两个条件:

$$
MR=MC
$$

- Marginal cost must be rising at the intersection.
- 交点处的 marginal cost 必须处于上升阶段.

An intersection where marginal cost is falling is not a profit maximum.

如果交点处 marginal cost 正在下降, 该交点不是利润最大化点.

## Price and Output Procedure | 价格与产量决策

| Market Type | Step 1: Select Output | Step 2: Select Price |
|---|---|---|
| Perfect competition | Choose $Q^*$ where $P=MR=MC$ | Accept the market price |
| Imperfect competition | Choose $Q^*$ where $MR=MC$ | Move to the demand curve at $Q^* to find P^*$ |

| 市场类型 | 第一步: 选择产量 | 第二步: 选择价格 |
|---|---|---|
| Perfect competition | 在 $P=MR=MC$ 处选择 $Q^*$ | 接受市场价格 |
| Imperfect competition | 在 $MR=MC$ 处选择 $Q^*$ | 从 $Q^* 向上找到需求曲线上的 P^*$ |

## Profit at the Optimal Output | 最优产量下的利润

$$
\pi=(P-ATC)Q
$$

| Relationship at $Q^*$ | Result | 中文解释 |
|---|---|---|
| $P>ATC$ | Positive economic profit | 正经济利润 |
| $P=ATC$ | Breakeven, zero economic profit | 盈亏平衡, 经济利润为 0 |
| $AVC<P<ATC$ | Short-run loss, but continue operating | 短期亏损, 但继续生产 |
| $P<AVC$ | Shut down in the short run | 短期停产 |

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Minimizing average total cost is not the general profit-maximization rule.
- 最小化 average total cost 不是一般性的利润最大化规则.
- First find output from $MR=MC$. Under imperfect competition, price comes from the demand curve, not from the marginal revenue curve.
- 先通过 $MR=MC$ 确定产量. 在 imperfect competition 下, 价格来自需求曲线, 而不是 marginal revenue curve.

# 4. Breakeven and Shutdown | 盈亏平衡与停产

## Breakeven Point | 盈亏平衡点

$$
TR=TC
$$

$$
AR=ATC
$$

For a single-price firm, $AR=P$, so breakeven also implies $P=ATC$ at the selected output.

对于统一定价企业, $AR=P$, 因此 breakeven 也表示所选产量下 $P=ATC$.

At breakeven, economic profit is zero, but accounting profit may be positive because economic cost includes opportunity cost.

在 breakeven point, 经济利润为 0, 但会计利润可能为正, 因为经济成本包括机会成本.

## Shutdown Point | 停产点

$$
TR=TVC
$$

$$
AR=AVC
$$

The shutdown point is the minimum point on the AVC curve for a perfectly competitive firm. If market price falls below minimum AVC, the firm should stop production in the short run.

对于 perfectly competitive firm, shutdown point 是 AVC 曲线的最低点. 如果市场价格低于 minimum AVC, 企业应在短期停止生产.

At $AR=AVC$, the firm is indifferent between operating and shutting down because either choice produces a loss equal to unavoidable fixed cost.

当 $AR=AVC$ 时, 企业对继续生产或停产没有差异, 因为两种选择造成的损失都等于无法避免的固定成本.

## Short-Run and Long-Run Decisions | 短期与长期决策

| Revenue-Cost Relationship | Short-Run Decision | Long-Run Decision |
|---|---|---|
| $TR\geq TC$ | Operate | Remain in market |
| $TVC\leq TR<TC$ | Operate at a loss | Exit unless conditions improve |
| $TR<TVC$ | Shut down production | Exit market |

| 收入成本关系 | 短期决策 | 长期决策 |
|---|---|---|
| $TR\geq TC$ | 继续生产 | 留在市场 |
| $TVC\leq TR<TC$ | 亏损生产 | 如果条件未改善则退出 |
| $TR<TVC$ | 停止生产 | 退出市场 |

## Why Operate at a Loss? | 为什么亏损仍继续生产

Unavoidable fixed costs are sunk in the short run. If revenue covers variable cost and contributes something toward fixed cost, operating produces a smaller loss than shutting down.

短期内无法避免的 fixed costs 属于 sunk costs. 如果收入能够覆盖可变成本并补偿部分固定成本, 继续生产造成的损失小于停产损失.

If the firm shuts down, its short-run loss equals unavoidable fixed cost.

如果企业停产, 其短期损失等于无法避免的固定成本.

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Breakeven compares revenue with total cost.
- Breakeven 比较收入与总成本.
- Shutdown compares revenue with variable cost.
- Shutdown 比较收入与可变成本.
- Ignore sunk fixed costs in the short-run shutdown decision.
- 在短期停产决策中忽略 sunk fixed costs.
- Shutdown is a short-run production decision. Exit is a long-run market decision.
- Shutdown 是短期生产决策. Exit 是长期市场决策.

## WR International | WR International

Given | 已知:

The source provides total revenue and total economic cost for output levels from 0 to 100 units.

原文提供产量从 0 到 100 单位时的总收入和总经济成本.

| Quantity | Profit |
|---:|---:|
| 40 | 0 |
| 50 | 17,500 |
| 60 | 30,000 |
| 70 | 27,500 |
| 80 | 0 |

Answer | 答案:

- Initial breakeven occurs at 40 units.
- 第一个 breakeven point 为 40 单位.
- The profitable range in the table is above 40 and below 80 units.
- 表中盈利区间为高于 40 单位且低于 80 单位.
- Maximum profit is 30,000 at 60 units.
- 最大利润为 30,000, 对应产量为 60 单位.

- 产量低于 40 或高于 80 单位时出现经济亏损.

## London Business | London Business

Given | 已知:

| Item | Amount |
|---|---:|
| Revenue | GBP 2.0 million |
| Total cost | GBP 2.5 million |
| Total variable cost | GBP 1.5 million |

因为收入高于可变成本, 企业短期应继续生产. 企业覆盖全部可变成本以及 GBP 0.5 million 的固定成本. 如果条件未改善, 长期应退出市场.

如果收入下降至 GBP 1.3 million, 收入低于 GBP 1.5 million 的可变成本. 企业短期应停产, 长期应退出市场.

# 6. Short Run, Long Run, and Scale | 短期, 长期与规模

## Time Horizons | 时间范围

| Horizon | Definition | 中文解释 |
|---|---|---|
| Short run | At least one factor of production is fixed | 至少一种生产要素固定 |
| Long run | All factors are variable and firms can enter or exit | 所有生产要素可变, 企业可以进入或退出市场 |

The firm always operates in the short run but plans in the long run. The time required to reach the long run differs by industry.

企业始终在短期中经营, 但在长期中规划. 达到长期所需的时间因行业而异.

## Cost Curves | 成本曲线

Each plant size has its own short-run total cost and short-run average total cost curves. The long-run cost curve selects the lowest available cost for each output level.

每一种工厂规模都有对应的 short-run total cost 和 short-run average total cost 曲线. 长期成本曲线在每个产量水平选择可达到的最低成本.

The long-run total cost curve is the lower envelope of short-run total cost curves. The long-run average total cost curve is the lower envelope of short-run average total cost curves.

Long-run total cost curve 是各 short-run total cost curves 的下包络线. Long-run average total cost curve 是各 short-run average total cost curves 的下包络线.

## Economies and Diseconomies of Scale | 规模经济与规模不经济

| LRAC Behavior | Scale Effect | 中文解释 |
|---|---|---|
| Falling as output rises | Economies of scale | 产量增加时单位成本下降 |
| Constant as output rises | Constant LRAC | 产量增加时单位成本不变 |
| Rising as output rises | Diseconomies of scale | 产量增加时单位成本上升 |

## Returns to Scale vs. Economies of Scale | 规模报酬与规模经济

| Concept | Relationship | 中文解释 |
|---|---|---|
| Increasing returns to scale | Output increases proportionately more than inputs | 产量增长比例大于投入增长比例 |
| Constant returns to scale | Output increases in the same proportion as inputs | 产量与投入按相同比例增长 |
| Decreasing returns to scale | Output increases proportionately less than inputs | 产量增长比例小于投入增长比例 |

Returns to scale describe the production relationship between inputs and output. Economies of scale describe the cost relationship between output and LRAC.

Returns to scale 描述投入与产出之间的生产关系. Economies of scale 描述产量与 LRAC 之间的成本关系.

## Sources of Economies of Scale | 规模经济来源

- Increasing returns to scale.
- 规模报酬递增.
- Specialization and division of labor.
- 专业化与劳动分工.
- More efficient equipment and improved technology.
- 更高效的设备与更先进的技术.
- Better use of by-products, energy, quality control, and market information.
- 更有效利用副产品, 能源, 质量控制和市场信息.
- Input discounts from purchasing larger quantities.
- 大量采购带来的投入品折扣.

## Sources of Diseconomies of Scale | 规模不经济来源

- Decreasing returns to scale.
- 规模报酬递减.
- Management and communication problems.
- 管理与沟通问题.
- Overlapping or duplicated functions and product lines.
- 职能或产品线重叠与重复.
- Higher input prices caused by supply constraints.
- 供应限制导致投入品价格上升.

## Minimum Efficient Scale | 最小有效规模

Minimum efficient scale is the smallest output at which LRAC reaches its minimum.

Minimum efficient scale 是 LRAC 达到最低点时对应的最小产量.

Under perfect competition, long-run pressure forces firms toward the minimum point on LRAC. A firm that cannot operate at least cost faces a threat to long-run viability.

在 perfect competition 下, 长期竞争压力迫使企业趋向 LRAC 的最低点. 无法以最低成本生产的企业其长期生存能力会受到威胁.

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Economies and diseconomies may occur at the same time. The direction of LRAC depends on which effect dominates.
- Economies 和 diseconomies 可能同时存在. LRAC 的方向取决于哪种效应占主导.
- Minimum efficient scale is the smallest output at minimum LRAC, not every output on a flat minimum range.
- Minimum efficient scale 是 LRAC 最低区间中的最小产量, 而不是该区间中的每一个产量.

# 7. Four Market Structures | 四种市场结构

## Comparison | 对比

| Structure | Sellers | Product | Entry Barriers | Pricing Power | Non-Price Competition |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Perfect competition | Many | Homogeneous | Very low | None | None |
| Monopolistic competition | Many | Differentiated close substitutes | Low | Some | Advertising and differentiation |
| Oligopoly | Few | Homogeneous or differentiated substitutes | High | Some or considerable | Advertising, differentiation, and strategy |
| Pure monopoly | One | Unique, with no close substitute | Very high | Considerable | May include advertising |

| 市场结构 | 卖方数量 | 产品 | 进入壁垒 | 定价权 | 非价格竞争 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Perfect competition | 很多 | 同质 | 很低 | 无 | 无 |
| Monopolistic competition | 很多 | 差异化的近似替代品 | 低 | 一定 | 广告与差异化 |
| Oligopoly | 少数 | 同质或差异化替代品 | 高 | 一定或较强 | 广告, 差异化与策略互动 |
| Pure monopoly | 一个 | 独特且没有近似替代品 | 很高 | 较强 | 可能包括广告 |

## Long-Run Comparison | 长期对比

| Structure | Long-Run Economic Profit | Output and Cost Feature |
|---|---|---|
| Perfect competition | Zero | $P=MC$ and production at minimum LRAC |
| Monopolistic competition | Zero | $P>MC$ and output below minimum ATC |
| Oligopoly | May remain positive | Depends on barriers and strategic behavior |
| Pure monopoly | May remain positive | Protected by very high barriers to entry |

| 市场结构 | 长期经济利润 | 产量与成本特征 |
|---|---|---|
| Perfect competition | 为 0 | $P=MC$, 并在 minimum LRAC 生产 |
| Monopolistic competition | 为 0 | $P>MC$, 产量低于 minimum ATC 对应产量 |
| Oligopoly | 可能持续为正 | 取决于进入壁垒和战略行为 |
| Pure monopoly | 可能持续为正 | 受到极高进入壁垒保护 |

## Market Structure Determinants | 市场结构决定因素

1. Number and relative size of firms.
2. Degree of product differentiation.
3. Seller's power over price.
4. Strength of barriers to entry and exit.
5. Degree of non-price competition.

1. 企业数量与相对规模.
2. 产品差异化程度.
3. 卖方对价格的控制力.
4. 进入和退出壁垒的强度.
5. 非价格竞争程度.

Entry barriers may include large capital requirements, patents, and high exit costs associated with assets that cannot be redeployed.

进入壁垒可能包括巨额资本要求, 专利, 以及无法重新配置的资产所产生的高退出成本.

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- More sellers and lower barriers generally mean stronger competition and faster erosion of economic profit.
- 卖方越多且进入壁垒越低, 竞争通常越强, 经济利润消失越快.
- Fewer sellers do not automatically mean strong market power. A credible threat of entry can force competitive behavior.
- 卖方较少不一定表示市场势力强. 可信的进入威胁可能迫使企业采取竞争性行为.

# 8. Perfect Competition and Pure Monopoly | 完全竞争与纯垄断

## Perfect Competition | 完全竞争

A perfectly competitive firm is a very small seller among many firms producing an identical product. It cannot influence market price and faces a horizontal demand curve.

Perfectly competitive firm 是众多同质产品生产者中的一个很小卖方. 企业无法影响市场价格, 并面对水平需求曲线.

$$
P=AR=MR
$$

At the profit-maximizing output:

在利润最大化产量处:

$$
P=MC
$$

Short-run economic profit or loss is possible. In the long run, free entry and exit drive economic profit to zero and price toward minimum LRAC.

短期可能出现经济利润或亏损. 长期中, 自由进入和退出使经济利润趋于 0, 并使价格趋向 minimum LRAC.

## Pure Monopoly | 纯垄断

A pure monopoly has one seller, no close substitute, and very high barriers to entry. The firm faces the market demand curve and has considerable control over price and output.

Pure monopoly 只有一个卖方, 没有近似替代品, 并且进入壁垒很高. 企业面对市场需求曲线, 对价格和产量拥有较强控制力.

The monopolist chooses output where $MR=MC$ and then sets price from the demand curve. Because $P>MR$, the monopolist generally produces less and charges more than a perfectly competitive market with comparable demand and cost.

Monopolist 在 $MR=MC$ 处选择产量, 然后从需求曲线确定价格. 因为 $P>MR$, 与需求和成本相近的 perfectly competitive market 相比, monopolist 通常产量更低且价格更高.

Barriers to entry can preserve positive economic profit in the long run. Many pure monopolies, such as local electric utilities, are regulated and may be permitted to earn a normal return.

进入壁垒可以使正经济利润长期存在. 许多 pure monopolies, 例如当地电力企业, 受到监管并可能被允许获得正常回报.

## Innovation Trade-Off | 创新权衡

Perfect competition tends to provide greater quantity and lower price. Less competitive structures may provide stronger incentives to fund costly research, innovation, and product development.

Perfect competition 通常提供更高产量和更低价格. 竞争程度较低的市场结构可能为高成本研究, 创新和产品开发提供更强激励.

# 9. Monopolistic Competition | 垄断竞争

## Characteristics | 特征

- Many buyers and sellers.
- 买方与卖方数量很多.
- Products are differentiated but remain close substitutes.
- 产品具有差异化, 但仍然是近似替代品.
- Entry and exit costs are fairly low.
- 进入和退出成本较低.
- Firms have some pricing power.
- 企业拥有一定定价权.
- Advertising, branding, and other non-price strategies are important.
- 广告, 品牌和其他非价格策略很重要.

Successful differentiation creates customer loyalty, makes demand less elastic, and increases pricing power. Strong differentiation makes the firm behave more like a monopoly.

成功的差异化可以建立客户忠诚度, 降低需求弹性并提高定价权. 差异化越强, 企业行为越接近 monopoly.

## Demand, Supply, and Optimal Decision | 需求, 供给与最优决策

The firm's demand curve is downward sloping. There is no well-defined supply curve because quantity supplied depends on both demand and marginal cost.

企业需求曲线向右下方倾斜. 该市场不存在明确的 supply curve, 因为供给量同时取决于需求和边际成本.

$$
MR=MC
$$

After selecting $Q^*  from $MR=MC$, the firm finds  $P^*$ from the demand curve.

企业通过 $MR=MC$ 选择 $Q^* 后, 再从需求曲线确定  $P^*$.

$$
\pi=(P-ATC)Q
$$

## Short Run vs. Long Run | 短期与长期

In the short run, positive or negative economic profit is possible. In the long run, positive profit attracts entry, reducing each incumbent's demand until economic profit reaches zero.

短期可能出现正或负经济利润. 长期中, 正利润吸引新企业进入, 从而降低现有企业的需求, 直至经济利润降至 0.

Long-run equilibrium satisfies $MR=MC$ and $P=ATC$, but output is below the quantity that minimizes ATC. The firm therefore has excess capacity.

长期均衡满足 $MR=MC$ 和 $P=ATC$, 但产量低于使 ATC 最小的产量. 因此企业存在 excess capacity.

## Benefits and Costs | 优点与成本

| Benefit | Cost |
|---|---|
| Variety and products tailored to different preferences | Higher price and lower output than perfect competition |
| Incentive to innovate and experiment | Advertising and differentiation costs |

| 优点 | 成本 |
|---|---|
| 提供多样性并满足不同偏好 | 相比 perfect competition 价格更高且产量更低 |
| 激励创新与产品试验 | 产生广告和差异化成本 |

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Product differentiation is the source of pricing power.
- Product differentiation 是定价权的来源.
- Long-run zero economic profit does not mean production occurs at minimum ATC.
- 长期经济利润为 0 不表示企业在 minimum ATC 生产.
- The firm has no independent supply curve.
- 企业没有独立的供给曲线.

# 10. Oligopoly | 寡头垄断

## Characteristics | 特征

- A few firms dominate the relevant market.
- 少数企业主导相关市场.
- Products may be homogeneous or differentiated substitutes.
- 产品可能是同质或差异化替代品.
- Entry barriers are high.
- 进入壁垒较高.
- Firms have pricing power and rely heavily on non-price strategy.
- 企业拥有定价权, 并高度依赖非价格策略.
- Each firm's decision depends on expected rival reactions.
- 每家企业的决策都取决于对竞争对手反应的预期.

Strategic interdependence is the defining feature of oligopoly. There is no single demand, supply, price, or output model that fits every oligopoly.

Strategic interdependence 是 oligopoly 的核心特征. 不存在适用于所有 oligopoly 的单一需求, 供给, 价格或产量模型.

The general rule remains to select output where $MR=MC$ and determine price from the relevant demand curve.

一般规则仍然是在 $MR=MC$ 处选择产量, 并从相关需求曲线确定价格.

## Long-Run Strategy | 长期策略

Long-run economic profit is possible because entry barriers are high. However, new entry and improved rival efficiency may reduce the dominant firm's market share and profitability over time.

由于进入壁垒较高, 长期经济利润可能持续存在. 但是新企业进入和竞争对手效率提高, 可能逐步降低 dominant firm 的市场份额和盈利能力.

Price wars usually reduce revenue for all participants, while market-share gains may be temporary. Innovation may help preserve leadership, although it can be costly.

价格战通常降低所有参与者的收入, 而市场份额收益可能只是暂时的. 创新可能帮助维持领导地位, 但成本可能较高.

## Main Strategic Models | 主要战略模型

| Model | Key Assumption | Main Result |
|---|---|---|
| Kinked demand | Rivals match price cuts but ignore price increases | Price rigidity and discontinuous MR |
| Cournot | Firms choose output simultaneously and treat rivals' output as fixed | Stable output equilibrium between monopoly and competition |
| Nash | No firm can gain by changing strategy unilaterally, given rivals' strategies | Non-cooperative equilibrium |
| Stackelberg | Leader chooses output first, follower chooses after observing | First-mover advantage for leader |
| Cartel | Firms coordinate output or price | Cooperative result resembling monopoly |
| Dominant firm | Price leader chooses price, smaller firms follow | Leader behaves like a monopoly in its market segment |

| 模型 | 核心假设 | 主要结果 |
|---|---|---|
| Kinked demand | 对手跟随降价, 但忽略提价 | 价格刚性与不连续 MR |
| Cournot | 企业同时选择产量, 并假设对手产量不变 | 产量均衡位于 monopoly 与 competition 之间 |
| Nash | 给定对手策略, 任何企业都无法通过单方面改变策略获利 | 非合作均衡 |
| Stackelberg | 领导者先选择产量, 跟随者观察后选择 | 领导者具有先行者优势 |
| Cartel | 企业协调产量或价格 | 类似 monopoly 的合作结果 |
| Dominant firm | 价格领导者定价, 小企业跟随 | 领导者在其市场区间内类似 monopoly |

# 11. Kinked Demand Curve | 折弯需求曲线

## Rival Reactions | 对手反应

- If the firm raises price, rivals do not follow. Customers switch away, so demand above the kink is relatively elastic.
- 如果企业提价, 对手不跟随. 客户转向其他企业, 因此折点上方的需求相对富有弹性.
- If the firm cuts price, rivals match the cut. Market share changes little, so demand below the kink is relatively inelastic.
- 如果企业降价, 对手跟随降价. 市场份额变化很小, 因此折点下方的需求相对缺乏弹性.

## Result | 结果

The two demand segments create a kink and a discontinuity in the marginal revenue curve. If marginal cost changes within the MR gap, optimal price and output remain unchanged.

两段需求曲线形成折点, 并使 marginal revenue curve 出现不连续区间. 如果 marginal cost 在 MR 缺口范围内变化, 最优价格和产量保持不变.

The model helps explain stable oligopoly prices but cannot explain how the original prevailing price was determined.

该模型可以解释 oligopoly 中的价格稳定性, 但无法解释最初的市场价格如何形成.

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

Above the kink is more elastic. Below the kink is less elastic.

折点上方需求更富有弹性. 折点下方需求更缺乏弹性.

# 12. Cournot Model | Cournot 模型

## Definition | 定义

Each firm selects its profit-maximizing output assuming the other firms will not change output. In a duopoly, decisions are simultaneous.

每家企业在假设其他企业产量不变的情况下选择自身利润最大化产量. 在 duopoly 中, 企业同时决策.

## Worked Example | 例题

Given | 已知:

$$
Q_D=450-P
$$

$$
P=450-Q_D
$$

$$
Q_D=q_1+q_2
$$

$$
MC=30
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $Q_D$ | Total market quantity demanded | 市场总需求量 |
| $q_1$ | Output of Firm 1 | 企业 1 的产量 |
| $q_2$ | Output of Firm 2 | 企业 2 的产量 |

Firm 1 total revenue | 企业 1 总收入:

$$
TR_1=(450-q_1-q_2)q_1
$$

Firm 1 marginal revenue | 企业 1 边际收入:

$$
MR_1=450-2q_1-q_2
$$

Firm 2 marginal revenue | 企业 2 边际收入:

$$
MR_2=450-q_1-2q_2
$$

Set each marginal revenue equal to marginal cost:

将每家企业的边际收入设为边际成本:

$$
450-2q_1-q_2=30
$$

$$
450-q_1-2q_2=30
$$

By symmetry:

根据对称性:

$$
q_1=q_2=140
$$

$$
Q_D=280
$$

$$
P=450-280=170
$$

## Market Comparison | 市场比较

| Structure | Total Output | Price |
|---|---:|---:|
| Monopoly | 210 | 240 |
| Cournot duopoly | 280 | 170 |
| Perfect competition | 420 | 30 |

Subscripts $M$, $C$, and $PC$ denote monopoly, Cournot, and perfect competition.

下标 $M$, $C$ 和 $PC$ 分别表示 monopoly, Cournot 和 perfect competition.

$$
Q_M<Q_C<Q_{PC}
$$

$$
P_M>P_C>P_{PC}
$$

As the number of Cournot firms increases, equilibrium moves toward the perfectly competitive outcome.

随着 Cournot 企业数量增加, 均衡结果趋向 perfectly competitive outcome.

# 13. Nash Equilibrium, Game Theory, and Stackelberg | Nash 均衡, 博弈论与 Stackelberg

## Game Theory | 博弈论

Game theory is the set of tools used to incorporate expected rival responses into strategic decisions.

Game theory 是在战略决策中纳入竞争对手预期反应的一组工具.

## Nash Equilibrium | Nash 均衡

A Nash equilibrium occurs when each participant is choosing the best available strategy given the strategies of all rivals. No participant can increase profit through a unilateral strategy change.

Nash equilibrium 表示每个参与者在给定所有对手策略时都选择了自身最佳策略. 任何参与者都无法通过单方面改变策略提高利润.

Nash equilibrium is non-cooperative. The result does not necessarily maximize joint industry profit.

Nash equilibrium 是非合作均衡. 该结果不一定使行业联合利润最大化.

## Stackelberg Model | Stackelberg 模型

Cournot assumes simultaneous output decisions. Stackelberg assumes sequential decisions: the leader chooses first and the follower responds after observing the leader.

Cournot 假设企业同时决定产量. Stackelberg 假设企业顺序决策: 领导者先行动, 跟随者观察后回应.

The Stackelberg leader has a first-mover advantage and can choose aggressive output that forces the follower to reduce production. The leader earns more and the follower earns less than under Cournot.

Stackelberg 领导者具有先行者优势, 并可以选择较高产量迫使跟随者减少生产. 与 Cournot 相比, 领导者利润更高, 跟随者利润更低.

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Cournot: simultaneous quantity decisions.
- Cournot: 同时进行产量决策.
- Stackelberg: sequential quantity decisions.
- Stackelberg: 顺序进行产量决策.
- Nash: no profitable unilateral deviation.
- Nash: 不存在有利可图的单方面偏离.

# 14. Collusion and Cartels | 合谋与卡特尔

## Definition | 定义

Collusion occurs when firms coordinate pricing or output to increase joint profit, reduce uncertainty, or strengthen barriers to entry. An open and formal collusive agreement creates a cartel.

Collusion 表示企业协调价格或产量, 以提高联合利润, 降低不确定性或强化进入壁垒. 公开且正式的合谋协议形成 cartel.

An effective cartel behaves like a monopoly and seeks a cooperative equilibrium. Collusion is unlawful in many jurisdictions.

有效 cartel 的行为类似 monopoly, 并追求合作均衡. Collusion 在许多司法管辖区属于违法行为.

## Conditions Favoring Successful Collusion | 有利于合谋成功的条件

| More Likely | Less Likely |
|---|---|
| Few sellers or one dominant seller | Many sellers or similar-sized rivals |
| Homogeneous products | Highly differentiated products |
| Similar cost structures | Different cost structures |
| Frequent, regular, small orders | Large or irregular orders that reward cheating |
| Strong retaliation against cheating | Weak retaliation |
| Weak external competition | Strong threat from outside entrants |

| 更可能成功 | 更不可能成功 |
|---|---|
| 卖方很少或存在一个主导企业 | 卖方较多或对手规模相近 |
| 产品同质 | 产品高度差异化 |
| 成本结构相似 | 成本结构不同 |
| 订单频繁, 规律且规模较小 | 大额或不规律订单提高违约收益 |
| 对违约行为进行强烈报复 | 报复力度较弱 |
| 外部竞争较弱 | 外部进入威胁较强 |

## Oligopoly Behavior Extremes | Oligopoly 行为的两个极端

- Easy entry, weak brands, and limited economies of scale can make a few-firm market behave like perfect competition.
- 容易进入, 品牌较弱且规模经济有限, 可能使少数企业市场表现得像 perfect competition.
- Effective collusion can make an oligopoly behave like a monopoly.
- 有效合谋可能使 oligopoly 表现得像 monopoly.

# 15. Dominant Firm and Price Leadership | 主导企业与价格领导

## Characteristics | 特征

A dominant firm generally has at least 40% market share and may possess greater capacity, lower cost, first-mover status, or stronger customer loyalty.

Dominant firm 通常拥有至少 40% 的市场份额, 并可能具备更大产能, 更低成本, 先行者地位或更强客户忠诚度.

The dominant firm acts as price maker, while smaller firms follow to avoid a price war with a lower-cost producer.

Dominant firm 充当 price maker, 较小企业选择跟随, 以避免与低成本生产者发生价格战.

## Optimal Decision | 最优决策

$$
MR_L=MC_L
$$

The leader selects $Q_L$ from this equality and finds $P_L$ from its demand curve. Followers supply the residual market demand.

领导者通过该等式选择 $Q_L$, 并从自身需求曲线确定 $P_L$. 跟随者提供剩余市场需求.

$$
Q_F=Q_T-Q_L
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $Q_T$ | Total market quantity | 市场总产量 |
| $Q_L$ | Leader output | 领导者产量 |
| $Q_F$ | Followers' combined output | 跟随者总产量 |

Neither the leader nor the followers have a supply curve independent of demand conditions.

领导者和跟随者都不存在独立于需求条件的供给曲线.

# 16. Identifying Market Structure | 识别市场结构

## Practical Checklist | 实务检查清单

1. Define the relevant product and geographic market.
2. Count sellers and assess their relative size.
3. Evaluate product differentiation and substitutes.
4. Assess pricing power.
5. Assess entry and exit barriers.
6. Examine non-price competition and strategic interaction.
7. Consider regulation and the credible threat of entry.

1. 界定相关产品市场和地理市场.
2. 计算卖方数量并评估其相对规模.
3. 评估产品差异化和替代品.
4. 评估定价权.
5. 评估进入和退出壁垒.
6. 检查非价格竞争和战略互动.
7. 考虑监管和可信的进入威胁.

Market boundaries matter. Digitized services may have global markets, while products with high transportation or time costs may have local markets.

市场边界非常重要. 数字化服务可能面向全球市场, 而运输成本或时间成本较高的产品可能只面向本地市场.

## Porter Five Forces | Porter 五力

1. Threat of entry.
2. Power of suppliers.
3. Power of buyers.
4. Threat of substitutes.
5. Rivalry among existing competitors.

1. 新进入者威胁.
2. 供应商议价能力.
3. 买方议价能力.
4. 替代品威胁.
5. 现有竞争者之间的竞争.

An economic moat protects profitability through barriers such as strong brands, patents, or customer switching costs.

Economic moat 通过强大品牌, 专利或客户转换成本等壁垒保护盈利能力.

## Competition Law | 竞争法

Analysts evaluating a proposed merger should consider whether regulators may block it because the combined firm would create excessive market concentration or monopoly power.

分析拟议 merger 时, 分析师应考虑监管机构是否会因为合并后企业造成过度市场集中或 monopoly power 而阻止交易.

# 17. Measuring Market Power | 衡量市场势力

## Econometric Approach | 计量方法

The theoretically preferred approach estimates demand and supply elasticities. Highly elastic demand suggests weaker market power, while inelastic demand may indicate stronger pricing power.

理论上更理想的方法是估计需求和供给弹性. 需求高度富有弹性通常表示市场势力较弱, 需求缺乏弹性可能表示定价权较强.

Observed price and quantity are jointly determined by supply and demand, creating an endogeneity problem. Estimation therefore requires separate demand and supply equations.

观察到的价格和数量由供给与需求共同决定, 因而存在 endogeneity problem. 估计需要分别建立需求方程和供给方程.

## Econometric Limitations | 计量方法局限

- Time-series estimation requires many observations, but market structure may change over time.
- Time-series estimation 需要大量观测值, 但市场结构可能随时间变化.
- Historical elasticities may not describe the market after a merger or structural change.
- 历史弹性可能无法描述 merger 或结构变化后的市场.
- Cross-sectional estimation requires extensive data collection.
- Cross-sectional estimation 需要大量数据收集.
- Results can be sensitive to model specification and explanatory-variable choices.
- 结果可能对模型设定和解释变量选择非常敏感.

# 18. Concentration Ratio | 集中度比率

## Formula | 公式

$$
CR_N=\sum_{i=1}^{N}s_i
$$

| Symbol | Meaning | 中文含义 |
|---|---|---|
| $CR_N$ | Concentration ratio for the largest $N$ firms | 最大 $N$ 家企业的集中度比率 |
| $s_i$ | Market share of Firm $i$ | 企业 $i$ 的市场份额 |

The concentration ratio is the combined market share of the largest $N$ firms. It ranges from 0 to 100% when shares are expressed as percentages.

Concentration ratio 是最大 $N$ 家企业市场份额之和. 当市场份额用百分比表示时, 取值范围为 0 至 100%.

## Example | 例题

Given market shares of 35%, 25%, 20%, 10%, and four firms with 2.5% each:

已知市场份额分别为 35%, 25%, 20%, 10%, 以及另外四家各 2.5%:

$$
CR_3=35\%+25\%+20\%=80\%
$$

$$
CR_4=35\%+25\%+20\%+10\%=90\%
$$

If the two largest firms merge:

如果最大的两家企业合并:

$$
CR_3=60\%+20\%+10\%=90\%
$$

## Advantages and Limitations | 优点与局限

| Advantage | Limitation |
|---|---|
| Simple to calculate and interpret | Does not directly measure pricing power |
| Useful first screen for concentration | Ignores demand elasticity and threat of entry |
| Requires limited data | May change only slightly after mergers among top firms |

| 优点 | 局限 |
|---|---|
| 计算和解释简单 | 不直接衡量定价权 |
| 可用于初步筛查市场集中度 | 忽略需求弹性和进入威胁 |
| 数据要求较低 | 最大企业之间合并后可能只发生轻微变化 |

# 19. Herfindahl-Hirschman Index | Herfindahl-Hirschman 指数

## Formula | 公式

$$
HHI_N=\sum_{i=1}^{N}s_i^2
$$

Use market shares as decimals under the convention used in this module.

按照本模块的计算惯例, 市场份额使用小数表示.

If one firm controls the entire market:

如果一家企业控制整个市场:

$$
HHI=1
$$

If all $M$ firms have equal shares and all are included:

如果全部 $M$ 家企业份额相等且全部纳入计算:

$$
HHI=\frac{1}{M}
$$

## Merger Example | 合并例题

Before the merger, the top three shares are 35%, 25%, and 20%:

合并前, 最大三家份额为 35%, 25% 和 20%:

$$
HHI_3=0.35^2+0.25^2+0.20^2=0.225
$$

After the two largest firms merge, the top three shares are 60%, 20%, and 10%:

最大的两家企业合并后, 最大三家份额为 60%, 20% 和 10%:

$$
HHI_3=0.60^2+0.20^2+0.10^2=0.410
$$

HHI rises much more sharply than the concentration ratio because squaring gives greater weight to the largest firms.

HHI 的上升幅度明显大于 concentration ratio, 因为平方处理赋予最大企业更高权重.

## Advantages and Limitations | 优点与局限

| Advantage | Limitation |
|---|---|
| Reflects both number and relative size of firms | Does not measure demand elasticity |
| More sensitive to mergers involving large firms | Does not incorporate the threat of entry or low barriers |
| Widely used by competition regulators | Depends on correct market definition and share data |

| 优点 | 局限 |
|---|---|
| 同时反映企业数量与相对规模 | 不衡量需求弹性 |
| 对大型企业之间的合并更敏感 | 不考虑进入威胁或低进入壁垒 |
| 被竞争监管机构广泛使用 | 依赖正确的市场界定和份额数据 |

## CFA Exam Point | CFA 考试重点

- Square decimal market shares, not percentage numbers, when the answer is expressed from 0 to 1.
- 当答案范围为 0 至 1 时, 应对小数形式的市场份额平方, 而不是直接对百分数数值平方.
- HHI is more informative than a simple concentration ratio for changes in relative firm size, but neither measure proves market power.
- 对企业相对规模变化而言, HHI 比简单 concentration ratio 更有信息量, 但两者都不能证明市场势力.

# CFA Memory Points | CFA 记忆重点

## 1. Profit Maximization | 利润最大化

$$
MR=MC
$$

MC must be rising. Under imperfect competition, find price from the demand curve after selecting output.

MC 必须处于上升阶段. 在 imperfect competition 下, 先选择产量, 再从需求曲线确定价格.

## 2. Breakeven | 盈亏平衡

$$
TR=TC
$$

$$
AR=ATC
$$

Breakeven means zero economic profit, not necessarily zero accounting profit.

Breakeven 表示经济利润为 0, 但会计利润不一定为 0.

## 3. Shutdown | 停产

$$
TR=TVC
$$

$$
AR=AVC
$$

Below this point, shut down in the short run. If $TVC\leq TR<TC$, continue operating in the short run but exit in the long run unless conditions improve.

低于该点时, 企业短期停产. 如果 $TVC\leq TR<TC$, 企业短期继续生产, 但如果条件未改善则长期退出.

## 4. Market Structure Shortcut | 市场结构速记

- Many firms and homogeneous products: perfect competition.
- 企业很多且产品同质: perfect competition.
- Many firms and differentiated products: monopolistic competition.
- 企业很多且产品差异化: monopolistic competition.
- Few strategically interdependent firms: oligopoly.
- 少数企业且存在战略相互依赖: oligopoly.
- One firm and no close substitute: pure monopoly.
- 一家企业且没有近似替代品: pure monopoly.

## 5. Monopolistic Competition Trap | 垄断竞争陷阱

Long-run economic profit is zero, but the firm does not produce at minimum ATC and has excess capacity.

长期经济利润为 0, 但企业不在 minimum ATC 生产, 并存在 excess capacity.

## 6. Oligopoly Models | Oligopoly 模型

- Kinked demand explains price rigidity but not the original price.
- Kinked demand 解释价格刚性, 但不解释最初价格.
- Cournot is simultaneous.
- Cournot 是同时决策.
- Stackelberg is sequential.
- Stackelberg 是顺序决策.
- Nash means no profitable unilateral deviation.
- Nash 表示不存在有利可图的单方面偏离.
- Cartel is cooperative and resembles monopoly.
- Cartel 是合作安排, 其行为类似 monopoly.

## 7. Scale | 规模

Falling LRAC means economies of scale. Rising LRAC means diseconomies of scale. Minimum efficient scale is the smallest output at minimum LRAC.

LRAC 下降表示 economies of scale. LRAC 上升表示 diseconomies of scale. Minimum efficient scale 是 LRAC 最低点对应的最小产量.

## 8. Concentration Measures | 集中度指标

$$
CR_N=\sum_{i=1}^{N}s_i
$$

$$
HHI_N=\sum_{i=1}^{N}s_i^2
$$

HHI gives more weight to large firms, but both measures ignore demand elasticity and may fail to reflect entry threats.

HHI 对大型企业赋予更高权重, 但两种指标都忽略需求弹性, 并且可能无法反映进入威胁.

## 9. Common Mistake | 常见错误

Do not confuse a short-run shutdown with a long-run exit, or a zero economic profit with a zero accounting profit.

不要混淆短期 shutdown 与长期 exit, 也不要混淆经济利润为 0 与会计利润为 0.

## 10. Exam Shortcut | 考试技巧

For any imperfectly competitive firm, use the same sequence: find $Q^*$ from $MR=MC$, find $P^*$ from demand, and compare price with ATC and AVC.

对于任何 imperfectly competitive firm, 使用相同顺序: 通过 $MR=MC$ 找到 $Q^*$, 从 demand 找到 $P^*$, 然后将价格与 ATC 和 AVC 比较.
