# 1. Monetary Policy vs Fiscal Policy | 货币政策与财政政策

| Dimension | Monetary Policy | Fiscal Policy |
|---|---|---|
| Decision maker | Central bank | Government |
| 决策者 | 中央银行 | 政府 |
| Main tools | Policy rates, bank reserves, reserve requirements, money and credit conditions | Government spending, transfer payments, and taxes |
| 主要工具 | 政策利率, 银行准备金, 准备金要求, 货币与信贷条件 | 政府支出, 转移支付, 税收 |
| Main transmission | Interest rates, credit, asset prices, and expectations | Aggregate demand, disposable income, and resource allocation |
| 主要传导机制 | 利率, 信贷, 资产价格, 预期 | 总需求, 可支配收入, 资源配置 |
| Additional role | Primarily macroeconomic stabilization and price stability | Stabilization plus redistribution of income and wealth |
| 额外职能 | 主要用于宏观稳定与价格稳定 | 除稳定经济外, 还可重新分配收入与财富 |

Both policies generally seek stable positive growth, low and stable inflation, and reduced economic fluctuations.

两者通常都以稳定的正增长, 低且稳定的通胀, 以及降低经济波动为目标.

**Exam trap:** Monetary policy influences money and credit. Fiscal policy changes government taxation and spending.

**考试陷阱:** 货币政策影响货币和信贷. 财政政策改变政府税收和支出.

---

# 2. Roles and Objectives of Fiscal Policy | 财政政策的职能与目标

Fiscal policy can affect:

财政政策可以影响:

1. Aggregate demand and real GDP.
   总需求和实际 GDP.
2. Distribution of income and wealth.
   收入和财富分配.
3. Allocation of resources across sectors and economic agents.
   不同行业和经济主体之间的资源配置.
4. Long-run productive capacity through infrastructure, education, and other capital investment.
   通过基础设施, 教育和其他资本投资影响长期生产能力.

## 2.1 Expansionary and Contractionary Policy | 扩张性与紧缩性政策

| Fiscal stance | Typical actions | Intended effect |
|---|---|---|
| Expansionary | Increase government spending or transfers, reduce taxes | Increase aggregate demand, output, and employment |
| 扩张性 | 增加政府支出或转移支付, 降低税收 | 提高总需求, 产出和就业 |
| Contractionary | Reduce government spending or transfers, increase taxes | Reduce aggregate demand and inflationary pressure |
| 紧缩性 | 减少政府支出或转移支付, 提高税收 | 降低总需求和通胀压力 |

Expansionary fiscal policy is generally most effective when substantial unused capacity and unemployment exist. Near full employment, it is more likely to raise wages and prices than real output.

当经济存在大量闲置产能和失业时, 扩张性财政政策通常最有效. 接近充分就业时, 政策更可能推高工资和价格, 而不是实际产出.

Examples of expansionary measures include:

扩张性措施包括:

- Personal income tax cuts that increase disposable income.
  降低个人所得税, 从而提高可支配收入.
- Indirect tax cuts that reduce prices and increase real purchasing power.
  降低间接税, 从而降低价格并提高实际购买力.
- Corporate tax cuts intended to increase profits and capital expenditure.
  降低公司税, 以提高利润和资本支出.
- New spending on infrastructure and social goods.
  增加基础设施和社会公共品支出.

The impact is uncertain. For example, households may save a tax cut during a recession because of precautionary motives.

政策效果存在不确定性. 例如, 经济衰退期间, 家庭可能出于预防动机将减税所得储蓄起来.

## 2.2 Keynesian and Monetarist Views | 凯恩斯主义与货币主义观点

- Keynesians: Fiscal policy can strongly affect aggregate demand, output, and employment when spare capacity is substantial.
  凯恩斯主义者: 当闲置产能较多时, 财政政策可以显著影响总需求, 产出和就业.
- Monetarists: Fiscal changes have mainly temporary effects on aggregate demand, and monetary policy is more effective for controlling inflation.
  货币主义者: 财政变化对总需求的影响主要是暂时的, 货币政策在控制通胀方面更有效.

# 3. Government Budget, Deficit, and Debt | 政府预算, 赤字与债务

Let government revenue be $R$, government expenditure be $E$, and the budget balance be $BB$.

设政府收入为 $R$, 政府支出为 $E$, 预算余额为 $BB$.

$$
BB=R-E
$$

- $R-E>0$: Budget surplus.
  $R-E>0$: 预算盈余.
- $R-E=0$: Balanced budget.
  $R-E=0$: 预算平衡.
- $R-E<0$: Budget deficit.
  $R-E<0$: 预算赤字.

Government debt is a stock accumulated from past borrowing. A budget deficit is a flow measured over a period.

政府债务是由过去借款累积形成的存量. 预算赤字是在一段时期内衡量的流量.

**Exam trap:** Debt and deficit are not interchangeable. A current deficit normally adds to outstanding debt, while a surplus reduces it.

**考试陷阱:** 债务和赤字不可互换. 当期赤字通常增加未偿债务, 盈余则减少债务.

## 3.1 Automatic Stabilizers | 自动稳定器

Automatic stabilizers change the budget balance countercyclically without new legislation.

自动稳定器无需新立法, 即可使预算余额产生逆周期变化.

- Recession: Tax revenue falls, unemployment benefits rise, and the deficit increases.
  衰退期: 税收收入下降, 失业救济增加, 赤字扩大.
- Boom: Tax revenue rises, transfer payments fall, and the surplus increases or deficit decreases.
  繁荣期: 税收收入上升, 转移支付下降, 盈余增加或赤字减少.

Automatic stabilizers dampen output fluctuations and do not face recognition or action lags. They are different from discretionary fiscal policy, which requires an active policy decision.

自动稳定器可以缓和产出波动, 且不面临识别时滞或行动时滞. 自动稳定器不同于需要主动决策的相机抉择财政政策.

## 3.2 Determining the Fiscal Stance | 判断财政立场

As a basic rule:

基本判断规则:

- A rising deficit or falling surplus is usually expansionary.
  赤字扩大或盈余减少通常属于扩张性.
- A falling deficit or rising surplus is usually contractionary.
  赤字减少或盈余增加通常属于紧缩性.

However, the actual budget balance can change because of the business cycle rather than a discretionary policy change. Therefore, economists often use the structural budget balance.

但是, 实际预算余额可能因商业周期而变化, 并非源于相机抉择政策. 因此, 经济学家通常使用结构性预算余额.

**Structural budget deficit:** The deficit that would exist if the economy were operating at full employment or potential output.

**结构性预算赤字:** 经济处于充分就业或潜在产出水平时仍会存在的赤字.

A cyclical deficit caused by recession is not, by itself, evidence that the government deliberately adopted an expansionary policy.

由衰退引起的周期性赤字本身不能证明政府主动采取了扩张性政策.

Actual deficits may also overstate the real fiscal burden because reported interest expenditure is nominal. Inflation reduces the real value of outstanding nominal debt, so real interest payments can be more informative.

实际赤字也可能夸大真实财政负担, 因为报告的利息支出是名义值. 通胀会降低未偿名义债务的实际价值, 因此实际利息支出可能更具信息价值.

# 4. Does National Debt Relative to GDP Matter? | 国债占 GDP 比率是否重要

Debt sustainability depends not only on the debt level, but also on economic growth, interest rates, inflation, investor confidence, and how borrowed funds are used.

债务可持续性不仅取决于债务规模, 还取决于经济增长, 利率, 通胀, 投资者信心, 以及借款资金的用途.

If the real interest rate on government debt exceeds the real growth rate of the economy, the debt-to-GDP ratio tends to worsen unless the government runs a sufficient primary surplus.

如果政府债务的实际利率高于经济实际增长率, 除非政府维持足够的基本盈余, 否则债务占 GDP 比率趋于恶化.

Inflation raises nominal GDP and reduces the real value of nominal debt. Deflation has the opposite effect and can keep the debt ratio elevated.

通胀会提高名义 GDP 并降低名义债务的实际价值. 通缩具有相反作用, 可能使债务比率长期维持高位.

## 4.1 Arguments for Less Concern | 不必过度担忧的理由

- Debt may be held mainly by domestic residents, making much of the interest a transfer within the country.
  债务可能主要由本国居民持有, 因而大量利息支付属于国内转移.
- Borrowing may finance productive physical or human capital and increase future output and tax revenue.
  借款可能用于生产性实物资本或人力资本, 从而提高未来产出和税收.
- Fiscal deficits may support employment when resources are idle.
  当资源闲置时, 财政赤字可以支持就业.
- Tax reform associated with deficits may reduce existing economic distortions.
  与赤字相关的税制改革可能减少现有经济扭曲.
- Under Ricardian equivalence, households save current tax cuts in anticipation of future taxes, offsetting the demand effect.
  根据李嘉图等价, 家庭预期未来税收上升, 因而将当前减税所得储蓄起来, 抵消需求效应.

## 4.2 Arguments for Greater Concern | 应当担忧的理由

- Future tax increases may reduce incentives to work, save, invest, and undertake entrepreneurial activity.
  未来加税可能削弱工作, 储蓄, 投资和创业激励.
- Government borrowing may crowd out private investment by raising interest rates.
  政府借款可能推高利率, 从而挤出私人投资.
- Loss of market confidence can sharply increase debt-servicing costs.
  市场信心丧失可能显著提高偿债成本.
- Monetary financing of deficits can lead to high inflation.
  通过货币发行融资赤字可能导致高通胀.
- Persistent debt can damage long-run capital accumulation and growth.
  持续高债务可能损害长期资本积累和经济增长.

**Time horizon matters:** Crowding out and tax distortions may be limited in the short run but substantial if deficits persist.

**时间范围很重要:** 挤出效应和税收扭曲在短期内可能有限, 但如果赤字持续存在, 长期影响可能很大.

# 5. Fiscal Policy Tools | 财政政策工具

## 5.1 Government Spending | 政府支出

| Tool | Description | Key exam point |
|---|---|---|
| Transfer payments | Welfare, pensions, unemployment benefits, and income support | Redistribute income. Not payment for current production, so not included directly in GDP |
| 转移支付 | 福利, 养老金, 失业救济和收入补助 | 重新分配收入. 不属于当期生产报酬, 因此不直接计入 GDP |
| Current spending | Recurring purchases such as health, education, and defense | Directly purchases goods and services |
| 经常性支出 | 医疗, 教育和国防等经常性采购 | 直接购买商品和服务 |
| Capital expenditure | Roads, hospitals, schools, and other infrastructure | Slow to implement, but increases productive capacity |
| 资本支出 | 道路, 医院, 学校和其他基础设施 | 实施缓慢, 但能提高生产能力 |

Government spending may provide public goods, build infrastructure, redistribute income, stabilize demand, or support innovative high-risk sectors.

政府支出可以提供公共品, 建设基础设施, 重新分配收入, 稳定需求, 或支持创新型高风险行业.

## 5.2 Taxes | 税收

- Direct taxes are levied on income, wealth, corporate profits, capital gains, labor income, and property.
  直接税针对收入, 财富, 公司利润, 资本利得, 劳动收入和财产征收.
- Indirect taxes are levied on spending, including sales taxes, value-added taxes, and excise duties.
  间接税针对支出征收, 包括销售税, 增值税和消费税.

Four desirable attributes of a tax system:

税制的 4 项理想属性:

1. Simplicity: Easy to comply with, enforce, and understand.
   简单性: 易于遵守, 执行和理解.
2. Efficiency: Minimizes distortions to work, saving, investment, and consumption choices.
   效率性: 尽量减少对工作, 储蓄, 投资和消费选择的扭曲.
3. Fairness: Horizontal equity for similar taxpayers and vertical equity across income levels.
   公平性: 相似纳税人之间具有横向公平, 不同收入水平之间具有纵向公平.
4. Revenue sufficiency: Raises enough revenue to finance government obligations.
   收入充足性: 筹集足够收入以履行政府责任.

Trade-offs exist. For example, raising marginal tax rates may increase revenue and progressivity but weaken incentives and efficiency.

这些目标之间存在权衡. 例如, 提高边际税率可能增加收入并提高累进性, 但会削弱激励和效率.

## 5.3 Advantages and Disadvantages | 优缺点

| Tool | Advantage | Disadvantage |
|---|---|---|
| Indirect taxes | Can be changed quickly, affect spending immediately, and support social or environmental objectives | Can be regressive and distort consumption |
| 间接税 | 可以快速调整, 立即影响支出, 并支持社会或环境目标 | 可能具有累退性并扭曲消费 |
| Direct taxes | Can redistribute income and influence labor or investment incentives | Payroll systems and legislation may require long implementation time |
| 直接税 | 可以重新分配收入并影响劳动或投资激励 | 工资系统和立法调整可能需要较长时间 |
| Transfers | Target support toward high-MPC households and strengthen automatic stabilization | May be slow to change and may affect work incentives |
| 转移支付 | 可以定向支持高 MPC 家庭并增强自动稳定作用 | 调整可能较慢, 且可能影响工作激励 |
| Capital spending | Directly raises demand and adds to future productive capacity | Planning, approval, and construction can take years |
| 资本支出 | 直接提高需求并增加未来生产能力 | 规划, 审批和建设可能耗时多年 |

Direct government spending normally has a larger immediate effect on aggregate demand than an equal tax cut or transfer increase. Tax cuts and transfers are more powerful when targeted at low-income households with a high MPC.

直接政府支出对总需求的即时影响通常大于等额减税或转移支付增加. 如果减税或转移支付面向 MPC 较高的低收入家庭, 其效果会更强.

Announcements can affect expectations before implementation. For example, a future tax increase may reduce current consumption.

政策公告可以在实施前影响预期. 例如, 未来加税可能降低当前消费.

# 6. Fiscal Multiplier | 财政乘数

Let:

设:

- $Y$ = national income or output.
  $Y$ = 国民收入或产出.
- $Y_D$ = disposable income.
  $Y_D$ = 可支配收入.
- $G$ = government purchases.
  $G$ = 政府购买.
- $T$ = taxes.
  $T$ = 税收.
- $B$ = transfer payments.
  $B$ = 转移支付.
- $NT=T-B$ = net taxes.
  $NT=T-B$ = 净税收.
- $t$ = net tax rate.
  $t$ = 净税率.
- $c$ = marginal propensity to consume, or MPC.
  $c$ = 边际消费倾向, 即 MPC.
- $s$ = marginal propensity to save, or MPS.
  $s$ = 边际储蓄倾向, 即 MPS.

$$
c+s=1
$$

$$
s=1-c
$$

If net taxes are proportional to income:

如果净税收与收入成比例:

$$
NT=tY
$$

$$
Y_D=Y-NT=(1-t)Y
$$

Ignoring taxes, the spending multiplier is:

忽略税收时, 支出乘数为:

$$
k=\frac{1}{1-c}=\frac{1}{s}
$$

With a proportional net tax rate, each spending round is reduced by both saving and taxes:

存在比例净税率时, 每一轮支出同时受到储蓄和税收的削弱:

$$
k_G=\frac{1}{1-c(1-t)}
$$

The change in output caused by a change in government spending is:

政府支出变化引起的产出变化为:

$$
\Delta Y=k_G\Delta G
$$

### Example | 例题

Given $c=0.90$, $t=0.20$, and an increase in government spending of USD 1 billion:

给定 $c=0.90$, $t=0.20$, 且政府支出增加 USD 1 billion:

$$
k_G=\frac{1}{1-0.90(1-0.20)}=\frac{1}{0.28}=3.57
$$

Therefore, the USD1 billion increase in government spending raises output by USD3.57 billion in this model.

因此, 在该模型中, 政府支出增加 USD1 billion 会使产出增加 USD3.57 billion.

Higher $c$ increases the multiplier. Higher $s$ or $t$ reduces the multiplier because more income leaks out of the spending cycle.

更高的 $c$ 会提高乘数. 更高的 $s$ 或 $t$ 会降低乘数, 因为更多收入从支出循环中漏出.

**Exam trap:** Transfer payments do not enter GDP directly. They affect GDP only when recipients spend part of the resulting disposable income.

**考试陷阱:** 转移支付不直接计入 GDP. 只有当受益人支出由此增加的部分可支配收入时, 转移支付才会影响 GDP.

## 6.1 Balanced Budget Multiplier | 平衡预算乘数

If government purchases and taxes increase by the same amount, output still rises because the full increase in $G$ enters aggregate demand, while households reduce consumption by only the MPC times the tax increase.

如果政府购买和税收等额增加, 产出仍会上升. 原因是 $G$ 的全部增量进入总需求, 而家庭消费仅减少税收增量乘以 MPC 的金额.

For a genuinely balanced budget change:

对于真正的平衡预算变动:

$$
k_{BB}=1
$$

$$
\Delta Y=\Delta G=\Delta T
$$

**Exam trap:** A balanced budget does not imply a neutral effect on output. The balanced budget multiplier equals 1.

**考试陷阱:** 预算平衡并不意味着对产出的影响为 0. 平衡预算乘数等于 1.

## 6.2 Ricardian Equivalence | 李嘉图等价

If households understand that debt-financed tax cuts require future taxes, they may save the entire current tax cut. In that case, bond financing is equivalent to delayed taxation and the intended increase in consumption may not occur.

如果家庭认识到债务融资的减税需要未来加税偿还, 他们可能将当前减税所得全部储蓄. 在这种情况下, 债券融资等同于推迟征税, 预期的消费增长可能不会发生.

Ricardian equivalence is a theoretical benchmark, not an assumption that always holds in practice.

李嘉图等价是理论基准, 并非在现实中总是成立的假设.

# 7. Fiscal Policy Implementation | 财政政策实施

## 7.1 Three Policy Lags | 3 类政策时滞

| Lag | Meaning | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Recognition lag | Time required to identify and confirm an economic problem | Economic data arrive late and are revised |
| 识别时滞 | 识别并确认经济问题所需的时间 | 经济数据发布滞后且会被修订 |
| Action lag | Time from the policy decision to implementation | Legislation, budgeting, and project approval |
| 行动时滞 | 从政策决策到实施所需的时间 | 立法, 预算和项目审批 |
| Impact lag | Time from implementation to observable economic effects | Spending gradually changes output and employment |
| 影响时滞 | 从政策实施到经济效果显现所需的时间 | 支出逐步影响产出和就业 |

Automatic stabilizers avoid recognition and action lags. Discretionary fiscal policy is exposed to all 3 lags.

自动稳定器不面临识别时滞和行动时滞. 相机抉择财政政策面临全部 3 类时滞.

## 7.2 Why Fine-Tuning Is Difficult | 为什么难以精确调控

- Economic data are delayed, incomplete, and subject to revision.
  经济数据存在滞后, 不完整, 且会被修订.
- Forecasting models are uncertain and may produce different estimates.
  预测模型存在不确定性, 不同模型可能得出不同估计.
- Consumption, investment, exports, and expectations may change independently of policy.
  消费, 投资, 出口和预期可能独立于政策而变化.
- The size and timing of fiscal multipliers are uncertain.
  财政乘数的规模和发生时间存在不确定性.
- Full employment and potential output are difficult to measure.
  充分就业和潜在产出难以准确衡量.
- Stimulus near full employment may cause inflation rather than real growth.
  接近充分就业时, 刺激政策可能引发通胀, 而非实际增长.
- If unemployment reflects supply constraints rather than deficient demand, demand stimulus may be ineffective and inflationary.
  如果失业源于供给约束而非需求不足, 需求刺激可能无效并引发通胀.
- A large existing deficit may reduce market confidence and increase government borrowing costs.
  已经较大的赤字可能削弱市场信心并提高政府借款成本.
- Government borrowing may crowd out private investment.
  政府借款可能挤出私人投资.
- Political objectives and implementation constraints may delay or distort policy.
  政治目标和实施约束可能延迟或扭曲政策.

**Key conclusion:** Fiscal policy cannot completely stabilize aggregate demand because information, timing, behavioral responses, and structural constraints cannot be known or controlled precisely.

**核心结论:** 财政政策无法完全稳定总需求, 因为信息, 时间, 行为反应和结构性约束无法被精确掌握或控制.

# 8. Exam Decision Framework | 考试判断框架

## Step 1: Identify the policy instrument | 第 1 步: 识别政策工具

- Central bank action affecting money or credit -> Monetary policy.
  中央银行影响货币或信贷的行动 -> 货币政策.
- Government tax, transfer, or spending action -> Fiscal policy.
  政府的税收, 转移支付或支出行动 -> 财政政策.

## Step 2: Determine the direction of aggregate demand | 第 2 步: 判断总需求方向

- Higher $G$ or $B$, or lower $T$ -> Usually expansionary.
  更高的 $G$ 或 $B$, 或更低的 $T$ -> 通常为扩张性.
- Lower $G$ or $B$, or higher $T$ -> Usually contractionary.
  更低的 $G$ 或 $B$, 或更高的 $T$ -> 通常为紧缩性.

## Step 3: Separate discretionary policy from the cycle | 第 3 步: 区分主动政策与周期影响

Ask whether the budget change came from a new policy decision or from automatic stabilizers. Use the structural budget balance to assess discretionary stance.

判断预算变化来自新的政策决策, 还是来自自动稳定器. 使用结构性预算余额评估相机抉择财政立场.

## Step 4: Assess effectiveness | 第 4 步: 评估有效性

Check spare capacity, MPC, tax leakages, policy lags, crowding out, confidence, and monetary policy interaction.

检查闲置产能, MPC, 税收漏出, 政策时滞, 挤出效应, 市场信心, 以及与货币政策的相互作用.

## Step 5: Distinguish short-run demand from long-run supply | 第 5 步: 区分短期需求与长期供给

Capital expenditure may be too slow for immediate stabilization, but it can raise long-run productive capacity. Tax or transfer changes may act faster, but do not directly add to capital stock.

资本支出对于即时稳定可能过于缓慢, 但可以提高长期生产能力. 税收或转移支付调整可能更快, 但不会直接增加资本存量.

# 9. Common Exam Traps | 常见考试陷阱

1. A larger actual deficit does not always mean discretionary expansion. It may reflect a recession and automatic stabilizers.
   更大的实际赤字并不总是代表主动扩张. 它可能反映衰退和自动稳定器.
2. An increase in government spending is not always expansionary in net terms if taxes rise by an even larger amount.
   如果税收增加更多, 政府支出增加并不一定在净效应上属于扩张性.
3. Transfer payments are fiscal tools but are not direct government purchases in GDP.
   转移支付是财政政策工具, 但不是 GDP 中的政府直接购买.
4. Capital spending has a long action lag but can increase potential output.
   资本支出具有较长的行动时滞, 但可以提高潜在产出.
5. Indirect taxes can often be changed quickly. Direct tax and transfer systems usually require more notice and administrative adjustment.
   间接税通常可以快速调整. 直接税和转移支付制度通常需要更长通知期和行政调整.
6. A balanced budget change can increase output because the balanced budget multiplier equals 1.
   平衡预算变动可以提高产出, 因为平衡预算乘数等于 1.
7. Fiscal expansion has the largest real output effect when unemployment and spare capacity are substantial.
   当失业和闲置产能较高时, 财政扩张对实际产出的影响最大.
8. Crowding out is an argument against persistent fiscal deficits, especially near full employment.
   挤出效应是反对持续财政赤字的理由, 尤其是在接近充分就业时.

# CFA Memory Points | CFA 记忆重点

- Monetary policy = central bank + money and credit. Fiscal policy = government + taxation and spending.
  货币政策 = 中央银行 + 货币和信贷. 财政政策 = 政府 + 税收和支出.
- Expansionary fiscal policy increases aggregate demand through higher spending or transfers, or lower taxes.
  扩张性财政政策通过增加支出或转移支付, 或降低税收来提高总需求.
- Actual deficit includes cyclical effects. Structural deficit measures the stance at full employment.
  实际赤字包含周期性影响. 结构性赤字衡量充分就业水平下的财政立场.
- Automatic stabilizers are fast and countercyclical. Discretionary policy faces recognition, action, and impact lags.
  自动稳定器反应快且具有逆周期性. 相机抉择政策面临识别, 行动和影响时滞.
- Debt is a stock. Deficit is a flow.
  债务是存量. 赤字是流量.
- Debt sustainability worsens when the real interest rate persistently exceeds real economic growth, other things equal.
  其他条件不变时, 如果实际利率持续高于实际经济增长率, 债务可持续性会恶化.
- Higher MPC increases the multiplier. Higher saving and taxes reduce it.
  更高的 MPC 提高乘数. 更高的储蓄和税收降低乘数.

$$
k_G=\frac{1}{1-c(1-t)}
$$

- The balanced budget multiplier equals 1.
  平衡预算乘数等于 1.
- Near full employment, fiscal expansion is more likely to cause inflation and crowding out.
  接近充分就业时, 财政扩张更可能引发通胀和挤出效应.
- Capital spending is slow but can raise productive capacity. Indirect taxes can usually be changed quickly.
  资本支出较慢, 但可以提高生产能力. 间接税通常可以快速调整.
