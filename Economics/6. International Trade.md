# 1. Why International Trade Matters | 国际贸易为何重要

Trade policy affects the volume and value of trade and therefore affects corporate revenue, costs, pricing power, profitability, growth, and investment returns.

贸易政策影响贸易量和贸易价值, 因而会影响企业收入, 成本, 定价能力, 盈利能力, 增长和投资收益.

Investors should monitor policy changes because restrictions can:

投资者应当关注政策变化, 因为贸易限制可以:

- Change demand for domestic and foreign products.
  改变对国内外产品的需求.
- Alter input and production costs.
  改变投入与生产成本.
- Change industry structure and competition.
  改变行业结构与竞争.
- Create licensing, documentation, and approval delays.
  造成许可, 文件和审批延误.
- Affect access to export markets and essential imported inputs.
  影响出口市场与必要进口投入品的获取.

# 2. Benefits of International Trade | 国际贸易的收益

## 2.1 Gains from Exchange | 交换收益

Trade allows a country to export goods for which it receives an attractive price and import goods that would be more costly to produce domestically.

贸易使一个国家可以出口能够获得有利价格的商品, 并进口在国内生产成本更高的商品.

Resources shift toward export industries and away from less-efficient import-competing industries. This increases total output and allows consumption of a larger bundle of goods.

资源从效率较低的进口竞争行业转向出口行业. 这会增加总产出, 并使消费者可以消费更大的商品组合.

## 2.2 Specialization and Comparative Advantage | 专业化与比较优势

Countries gain by specializing in goods they can produce at lower relative opportunity cost and trading for other goods.

各国通过专业生产具有较低相对机会成本的商品, 并交换其他商品而获益.

- Ricardian model: Comparative advantage arises from differences in technology.
  Ricardian model: 比较优势来自技术差异.
- Heckscher-Ohlin model: Comparative advantage arises from differences in factor endowments.
  Heckscher-Ohlin model: 比较优势来自要素禀赋差异.

**Exam point:** Comparative advantage depends on relative cost, not absolute cost.

**考试重点:** 比较优势取决于相对成本, 而不是绝对成本.

## 2.3 Economies of Scale | 规模经济

Access to a larger market increases output and can reduce average cost in industries with increasing returns to scale.

进入更大市场会提高产出, 并可能降低具有规模报酬递增行业的平均成本.

Capital-intensive industries, such as automobiles and steel, can benefit substantially from larger production volumes.

汽车和钢铁等资本密集型行业可以从更大生产规模中显著受益.

## 2.4 Product Variety and Intra-Industry Trade | 产品多样性与产业内贸易

Intra-industry trade occurs when a country both imports and exports products within the same category, such as different automobile models.

产业内贸易是一个国家同时进口和出口同一类别的不同产品, 例如不同汽车车型.

Under monopolistic competition:

在垄断竞争下:

- Firms produce differentiated products.
  企业生产差异化产品.
- Consumers gain access to more varieties.
  消费者可以获得更多产品种类.
- Firms sell into larger markets and achieve economies of scale.
  企业进入更大市场并实现规模经济.
- Long-run economic profit remains zero because entry is unrestricted.
  由于进入不受限制, 长期经济利润仍为 0.

## 2.5 Competition and Efficient Resource Allocation | 竞争与资源有效配置

Foreign competition reduces domestic monopoly power and pressures firms to improve efficiency, quality, and innovation.

外国竞争降低国内企业的垄断能力, 并促使企业提高效率, 质量和创新.

Trade can also generate:

贸易还可以带来:

- Learning by doing.
  干中学.
- Technology and knowledge spillovers.
  技术与知识溢出.
- Greater productivity.
  更高生产率.
- Better institutions and policy incentives.
  更好的制度与政策激励.
- Increased foreign investment.
  更多外国投资.

# 3. Costs and Distributional Effects of Trade | 贸易成本与分配效应

Trade can increase total national welfare without making every stakeholder better off.

贸易可以提高国家总体福利, 但并不意味着每个利益相关者都会受益.

## Winners | 受益者

- Consumers who obtain lower prices and greater variety.
  获得更低价格与更多产品选择的消费者.
- Export industries and workers with transferable skills.
  出口行业与拥有可转移技能的劳动者.
- Efficient firms that gain access to larger markets.
  进入更大市场的高效率企业.

## Losers | 受损者

- Less-efficient import-competing firms.
  效率较低的进口竞争企业.
- Workers with industry-specific skills in contracting sectors.
  萎缩行业中拥有行业特定技能的劳动者.
- Communities dependent on industries facing import competition.
  依赖进口竞争行业的社区.

## Adjustment Costs | 调整成本

Trade liberalization can cause temporary or persistent unemployment, retraining costs, business exits, and income inequality.

贸易自由化可能造成暂时或持续失业, 再培训成本, 企业退出和收入不平等.

Resources may eventually move to expanding industries, but some workers may never recover their previous income because their industry-specific skills lose value.

资源最终可能转移到扩张行业, 但部分劳动者可能永远无法恢复原收入, 因为其行业特定技能价值下降.

## Compensation Principle | 补偿原则

If total gains exceed total losses, winners could theoretically compensate losers and still remain better off. This establishes an aggregate welfare gain, not an actual guarantee of compensation.

如果总收益超过总损失, 受益者理论上可以补偿受损者并仍然保持净收益. 这说明总体福利增加, 但不保证补偿实际发生.

**Exam trap:** "Gains from trade" means aggregate benefits exceed aggregate losses. It does not mean all consumers, producers, workers, or regions benefit equally.

**考试陷阱:** "Gains from trade" 表示总体收益大于总体损失. 它不表示所有消费者, 生产者, 劳动者或地区都同等受益.

# 4. Trade Restrictions | 贸易限制

Trade restrictions are government policies that limit the ability of domestic households and firms to trade freely with other countries.

贸易限制是政府限制国内家庭与企业自由开展国际贸易的政策.

## Common Motives | 常见动机

- Protect established domestic industries.
  保护已有国内行业.
- Protect infant industries until they mature.
  在幼稚产业成熟前对其提供保护.
- Protect or increase domestic employment.
  保护或增加国内就业.
- Protect strategic industries for national security.
  出于国家安全目的保护战略行业.
- Generate government revenue.
  增加政府收入.
- Reduce a trade deficit.
  减少贸易赤字.
- Retaliate against foreign trade restrictions.
  报复外国贸易限制.

## Main Types | 主要类型

| Restriction | Definition |
|---|---|
| Tariff | Tax imposed by the importing government on imported goods |
| 关税 | 进口国政府对进口商品征收的税 |
| Import quota | Maximum quantity of a good that may be imported |
| 进口配额 | 允许进口的商品数量上限 |
| Voluntary export restraint, or VER | Exporting country agrees to limit exports |
| 自愿出口限制, 即 VER | 出口国同意限制出口数量 |
| Export subsidy | Government payment to a firm for each unit exported |
| 出口补贴 | 政府对企业每出口一单位商品提供支付 |
| Domestic content provision | Requires a specified share of value added or components to be domestic |
| 国内成分要求 | 要求规定比例的增加值或零部件来自国内 |
| Embargo | Prohibits trade in specified goods or with specified countries |
| 禁运 | 禁止特定商品贸易或与特定国家开展贸易 |

Capital restrictions limit foreign ownership of domestic assets or domestic ownership of foreign assets. They restrict financial markets rather than goods markets.

资本限制约束外国人持有国内资产, 或国内居民持有外国资产. 它限制金融市场, 而不是商品市场.

# 5. Tariffs | 关税

## Definition | 定义

A tariff is a tax levied on imported goods. For a small country that cannot influence the world price, the domestic price rises by the tariff amount.

关税是对进口商品征收的税. 对无法影响世界价格的小国而言, 国内价格会上升相当于关税的金额.

Let:

设:

| Symbol | Definition |
|---|---|
| $P_W$ | World price under free trade |
| $P_W$ | 自由贸易下的世界价格 |
| $t$ | Per-unit tariff |
| $t$ | 单位关税 |
| $P_T$ | Domestic price after the tariff |
| $P_T$ | 征收关税后的国内价格 |
| $Q_S$ | Domestic quantity supplied |
| $Q_S$ | 国内供给量 |
| $Q_D$ | Domestic quantity demanded |
| $Q_D$ | 国内需求量 |
| $M$ | Imports |
| $M$ | 进口量 |

$$
P_T=P_W+t
$$

$$
M=Q_D-Q_S
$$

## Directional Effects in a Small Country | 小国中的方向影响

Tariff -> Domestic price rises -> Domestic production rises -> Domestic consumption falls -> Imports fall.

关税 -> 国内价格上升 -> 国内生产增加 -> 国内消费下降 -> 进口减少.

## Welfare Effects | 福利影响

| Stakeholder | Effect |
|---|---|
| Consumers | Lose consumer surplus |
| 消费者 | 消费者剩余减少 |
| Domestic producers | Gain producer surplus |
| 国内生产者 | 生产者剩余增加 |
| Government | Receives tariff revenue |
| 政府 | 获得关税收入 |
| National welfare in a small country | Decreases because of deadweight loss |
| 小国国家福利 | 因无谓损失而下降 |

Using the standard areas $A$, $B$, $C$, and $D$:

使用标准区域 $A$, $B$, $C$ 和 $D$:

$$
\Delta CS=-(A+B+C+D)
$$

$$
\Delta PS=+A
$$

$$
\Delta GR=+C
$$

$$
\Delta W=-(B+D)
$$

Here, $CS$ is consumer surplus, $PS$ is producer surplus, $GR$ is government revenue, and $W$ is national welfare.

其中, $CS$ 为消费者剩余, $PS$ 为生产者剩余, $GR$ 为政府收入, $W$ 为国家福利.

## Sources of Deadweight Loss | 无谓损失来源

- Production inefficiency, $B$: Higher domestic prices keep inefficient producers in the market.
  生产无效率, $B$: 更高国内价格使低效率生产者留在市场.
- Consumption inefficiency, $D$: Consumers willing to pay more than the world price but less than the tariff-inclusive price no longer buy.
  消费无效率, $D$: 愿意支付高于世界价格但低于含税价格的消费者不再购买.

## Tariff Revenue | 关税收入

$$
GR=tM
$$

## Small Country vs Large Country | 小国与大国

- Small country: Price taker in the world market and cannot affect the world price.
  小国: 世界市场价格接受者, 无法影响世界价格.
- Large country: A major importer that can influence the world price.
  大国: 能够影响世界价格的主要进口国.

A large country may improve its terms of trade because foreign exporters reduce their price after a tariff. Its national welfare could theoretically rise if the terms-of-trade gain exceeds deadweight loss and trading partners do not retaliate.

大国征收关税后, 外国出口商可能降低价格, 从而改善该国贸易条件. 如果贸易条件收益大于无谓损失且贸易伙伴不报复, 该国国家福利理论上可能上升.

Global welfare still falls because the large country's gain comes at an even greater cost to its trading partner.

全球福利仍然下降, 因为大国收益来自贸易伙伴更大的损失.

# 6. Tariff Calculation Example | 关税计算例题

## Given | 已知

A small country has:

一个小国具有以下数据:

| Item | Before Tariff | After Tariff |
|---|---:|---:|
| Domestic price per ton | USD 5 | USD 6 |
| 国内每吨价格 | USD 5 | USD 6 |
| Domestic production | 110,000 tons | 130,000 tons |
| 国内生产 | 110,000 tons | 130,000 tons |
| Domestic consumption | 200,000 tons | 170,000 tons |
| 国内消费 | 200,000 tons | 170,000 tons |
| Imports | 90,000 tons | 40,000 tons |
| 进口 | 90,000 tons | 40,000 tons |

The tariff increases price by USD 1 per ton.

关税使每吨价格提高 USD 1.

## Consumer Surplus Loss | 消费者剩余损失

$$
|\Delta CS|=\frac{200{,}000+170{,}000}{2}\times 1=185{,}000
$$

Consumer surplus decreases by USD 185,000.

消费者剩余减少 USD 185,000.

## Producer Surplus Gain | 生产者剩余增加

$$
\Delta PS=\frac{110{,}000+130{,}000}{2}\times 1=120{,}000
$$

Producer surplus increases by USD 120,000.

生产者剩余增加 USD 120,000.

## Government Revenue | 政府收入

$$
\Delta GR=40{,}000\times 1=40{,}000
$$

Government tariff revenue is USD 40,000.

政府关税收入为 USD 40,000.

## Deadweight Loss | 无谓损失

$$
DWL=185{,}000-120{,}000-40{,}000=25{,}000
$$

The deadweight loss is USD 25,000.

无谓损失为 USD 25,000.

**Check:** Consumer loss must exceed producer gain plus government revenue in the small-country case.

**检查:** 在小国情形下, 消费者损失必须大于生产者收益与政府收入之和.

# 7. Import Quotas | 进口配额

## Definition | 定义

An import quota limits the quantity of a good that may be imported, generally for a specified period. An import license specifies how much the license holder may import.

进口配额限制一段规定时期内可以进口的商品数量. 进口许可证规定持有人可以进口的数量.

For a quota equivalent to a tariff:

对与关税等效的配额而言:

- Domestic price rises.
  国内价格上升.
- Domestic production rises.
  国内生产增加.
- Domestic consumption and imports fall.
  国内消费与进口下降.
- Consumers lose and domestic producers gain.
  消费者受损, 国内生产者受益.

## Quota Rents | 配额租金

Quota rents are profits created when restricted supply allows imports to be sold above their free-trade price.

配额租金是限制供给使进口商品能够以高于自由贸易价格出售而产生的利润.

The welfare result depends on who receives the quota rent:

福利结果取决于配额租金归属:

- Importing government auctions licenses -> Government captures rents -> Welfare loss resembles an equivalent tariff.
  进口国政府拍卖许可证 -> 政府获得租金 -> 福利损失类似等效关税.
- Foreign producers receive licenses or market power -> Foreign producers capture rents -> Importing-country welfare loss is larger.
  外国生产者获得许可证或市场能力 -> 外国生产者获得租金 -> 进口国福利损失更大.

If foreign producers capture area $C$:

如果外国生产者获得区域 $C$:

$$
\Delta W=-(B+C+D)
$$

If the importing government captures area $C$:

如果进口国政府获得区域 $C$:

$$
\Delta W=-(B+D)
$$

**Exam point:** A tariff guarantees government revenue. A quota does not guarantee who receives the quota rents.

**考试重点:** 关税保证政府获得收入. 配额不能保证配额租金归属.

---

# 8. Voluntary Export Restraints | 自愿出口限制

A voluntary export restraint, or VER, is imposed by the exporting country, which agrees to limit exports to the importing country.

自愿出口限制, 即 VER, 由出口国实施, 出口国同意限制对进口国的出口数量.

| Import Quota | VER |
|---|---|
| Imposed by importing country | Imposed by exporting country |
| 由进口国实施 | 由出口国实施 |
| Quota rent ownership can vary | Quota rents generally accrue to exporters |
| 配额租金归属可能不同 | 配额租金通常归出口商 |

A VER raises prices, increases importing-country production, reduces importing-country consumption and imports, and transfers quota rents abroad.

VER 提高价格, 增加进口国生产, 减少进口国消费与进口, 并将配额租金转移到国外.

**Exam point:** From the importing country's perspective, a VER is generally worse than an equivalent tariff because the government receives no revenue and foreign exporters capture the rents.

**考试重点:** 从进口国角度看, VER 通常比等效关税更差, 因为政府没有获得收入, 配额租金由外国出口商获得.

# 9. Export Subsidies | 出口补贴

An export subsidy is a government payment to a firm for each unit exported. Its purpose is to stimulate exports.

出口补贴是政府对企业每出口一单位商品提供的支付. 其目标是刺激出口.

## Effects in the Exporting Country | 对出口国的影响

Export subsidy -> Exporting firms shift sales abroad -> Domestic price rises -> Domestic production rises -> Domestic consumption falls -> Exports rise.

出口补贴 -> 出口企业将销售转向海外 -> 国内价格上升 -> 国内生产增加 -> 国内消费下降 -> 出口增加.

| Stakeholder | Effect |
|---|---|
| Domestic producers | Gain |
| 国内生产者 | 受益 |
| Domestic consumers | Lose |
| 国内消费者 | 受损 |
| Government | Pays the subsidy |
| 政府 | 支付补贴 |
| National welfare | Decreases |
| 国家福利 | 下降 |

Export subsidies distort production and trade away from comparative advantage.

出口补贴扭曲生产和贸易, 使其偏离比较优势.

In a large exporting country, greater exports can reduce the world price. Part of the subsidy benefit is transferred to foreign consumers, making the exporting country's welfare loss larger.

在大型出口国中, 出口增加可能降低世界价格. 部分补贴收益会转移给外国消费者, 从而使出口国福利损失更大.

Countervailing duties are tariffs imposed by an importing country against subsidized foreign exports.

反补贴税是进口国针对接受补贴的外国出口商品征收的关税.

# 11. Regional Trading Blocs | 区域贸易集团

A regional trading bloc is a group of countries that agrees to reduce or progressively eliminate barriers to trade and factor movements among members.

区域贸易集团是一组同意减少或逐步消除成员间贸易与要素流动壁垒的国家.

Regional integration is often faster and politically easier than reaching global agreements among many WTO members.

区域整合通常比在大量 WTO 成员之间达成全球协议更快, 政治争议也更少.

## Motivations | 动机

- Liberalize trade among a manageable number of countries.
  在数量可控的国家之间实现贸易自由化.
- Coordinate and harmonize policy more easily.
  更容易协调和统一政策.
- Gain access to larger markets.
  进入更大市场.
- Strengthen bargaining power and political influence.
  增强谈判能力与政治影响力.
- Promote investment, growth spillovers, and conflict reduction.
  促进投资, 增长溢出并降低冲突.

---

# 12. Levels of Regional Integration | 区域整合层级

The levels are cumulative. Each deeper level includes the features of the previous level.

各层级具有累积性. 每个更深层级都包含前一层级的特征.

| Level | Internal Trade Barriers | Common External Trade Policy | Free Factor Movement | Common Institutions and Coordinated Policy | Common Currency |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| Free trade area, or FTA | Removed | No | No | No | No |
| 自由贸易区, 即 FTA | 取消 | 否 | 否 | 否 | 否 |
| Customs union | Removed | Yes | No | No | No |
| 关税同盟 | 取消 | 是 | 否 | 否 | 否 |
| Common market | Removed | Yes | Yes | No | No |
| 共同市场 | 取消 | 是 | 是 | 否 | 否 |
| Economic union | Removed | Yes | Yes | Yes | Not required |
| 经济联盟 | 取消 | 是 | 是 | 是 | 非必需 |
| Monetary union | Removed | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| 货币联盟 | 取消 | 是 | 是 | 是 | 是 |

## Memory Ladder | 记忆阶梯

FTA -> Customs union -> Common market -> Economic union -> Monetary union.

FTA -> 关税同盟 -> 共同市场 -> 经济联盟 -> 货币联盟.

Add one feature at each step:

每一步增加一个特征:

1. Remove internal trade barriers.
   取消内部贸易壁垒.
2. Add common external trade policy.
   增加共同对外贸易政策.
3. Add free movement of labor and capital.
   增加劳动力与资本自由流动.
4. Add common institutions and coordinated economic policy.
   增加共同制度与协调经济政策.
5. Add a common currency.
   增加共同货币.

**Exam trap:** An FTA allows each member to maintain its own trade policy against non-members. A customs union has a common external trade policy.

**考试陷阱:** FTA 允许每个成员对非成员维持自己的贸易政策. 关税同盟具有共同对外贸易政策.

---

# 13. Trade Creation vs Trade Diversion | 贸易创造与贸易转移

## Trade Creation | 贸易创造

Trade creation occurs when integration replaces higher-cost domestic production with lower-cost imports from a member country.

贸易创造是区域整合使来自成员国的较低成本进口, 取代本国较高成本生产.

Direction:

方向:

Higher-cost domestic production -> Lower-cost member imports -> More efficient resource allocation -> Welfare rises.

较高成本国内生产 -> 较低成本成员国进口 -> 资源配置更有效 -> 福利上升.

## Trade Diversion | 贸易转移

Trade diversion occurs when lower-cost imports from a non-member are replaced by higher-cost imports from a member because the member receives preferential tariff treatment.

贸易转移是由于成员国享受优惠关税待遇, 来自非成员国的较低成本进口被来自成员国的较高成本进口取代.

Direction:

方向:

Lower-cost non-member imports -> Higher-cost member imports -> Less efficient resource allocation -> Welfare falls.

较低成本非成员国进口 -> 较高成本成员国进口 -> 资源配置效率下降 -> 福利下降.

## Net Welfare Effect | 净福利影响

- Trade creation greater than trade diversion -> Net welfare effect is positive.
  贸易创造大于贸易转移 -> 净福利影响为正.
- Trade diversion greater than trade creation -> Net welfare effect may be negative.
  贸易转移大于贸易创造 -> 净福利影响可能为负.

**Exam shortcut:** Creation replaces expensive domestic production. Diversion replaces cheap outsider imports.

**考试技巧:** 创造取代昂贵的国内生产. 转移取代便宜的外部进口.

---

# 14. Benefits and Costs of Trading Blocs | 贸易集团的收益与成本

## Benefits | 收益

- Greater specialization according to comparative advantage.
  根据比较优势实现更高程度专业化.
- Lower monopoly power because of foreign competition.
  外国竞争降低垄断能力.
- Economies of scale from a larger market.
  更大市场带来规模经济.
- Learning by doing, technology transfer, and knowledge spillovers.
  干中学, 技术转移和知识溢出.
- More foreign investment and better intermediate inputs.
  更多外国投资与更优质中间投入品.
- Growth spillovers among member countries.
  成员国之间的增长溢出.
- Greater collective bargaining power.
  更强集体谈判能力.
- Interdependence that may reduce conflict.
  相互依赖可能减少冲突.

## Costs | 成本

- Trade diversion.
  贸易转移.
- Temporary or permanent job losses in import-competing sectors.
  进口竞争行业暂时或永久失业.
- Adjustment and retraining costs.
  调整与再培训成本.
- Loss of policy independence and national sovereignty.
  政策独立性与国家主权减弱.
- Faster transmission of crises among highly integrated members.
  危机在高度整合成员之间更快传播.
- Differences in culture, tastes, and competitive conditions may limit expected gains.
  文化, 偏好和竞争条件差异可能限制预期收益.

## Challenges to Deeper Integration | 深化整合的挑战

1. Cultural differences and historical conflicts can impede political and social integration.
   文化差异与历史冲突可能阻碍政治和社会整合.
2. Deeper integration limits independent economic and social policy.
   更深层整合限制独立经济与社会政策.
3. A monetary union removes independent monetary policy and currency adjustment.
   货币联盟取消独立货币政策与汇率调整.
4. Persistent imbalances can spread instability across the bloc.
   持续失衡可能在集团内传播不稳定.

**Exam point:** Monetary union members cannot independently devalue or revalue their currency to correct national imbalances.

**考试重点:** 货币联盟成员不能独立使本币贬值或升值以纠正本国失衡.

# 15. Investment Implications | 投资影响

## Trade Liberalization | 贸易自由化

Potential beneficiaries:

潜在受益者:

- Efficient exporters.
  高效率出口企业.
- Firms with scalable production.
  生产可规模化的企业.
- Consumers and firms using cheaper or better imported inputs.
  使用更便宜或更优质进口投入品的消费者与企业.
- Companies gaining access to larger regional markets.
  进入更大区域市场的企业.

Potentially harmed:

潜在受损者:

- Inefficient import-competing firms.
  低效率进口竞争企业.
- Firms dependent on protected domestic pricing.
  依赖受保护国内定价的企业.
- Workers and regions concentrated in contracting industries.
  集中于萎缩行业的劳动者与地区.

## Trade Protection | 贸易保护

Domestic producers of protected goods may benefit from higher prices and output. Importers, downstream users, and consumers may face higher costs and lower quantities.

受保护商品的国内生产者可能因更高价格与产出而受益. 进口商, 下游使用者和消费者可能面临更高成本与更低数量.

An investor should examine:

投资者应当检查:

- Whether the firm is a producer, importer, exporter, or downstream user.
  企业是生产者, 进口商, 出口商还是下游使用者.
- Whether quota rents or tariff revenue accrue to the firm or government.
  配额租金或关税收入归企业还是政府.
- Whether essential inputs can be replaced.
  必要投入品能否被替代.
- Whether policy creates delays, licensing costs, or retaliation.
  政策是否造成延误, 许可成本或报复.
- Whether integration creates trade creation or trade diversion.
  区域整合产生贸易创造还是贸易转移.

# 16. Exam Decision Framework | 考试判断框架

## Step 1: Identify the Country and Policy | 第 1 步: 识别国家与政策

Determine whether the question concerns an importing country, exporting country, small country, or large country.

判断题目涉及进口国, 出口国, 小国还是大国.

## Step 2: Trace Price and Quantity | 第 2 步: 追踪价格与数量

- Import tariff, quota, or VER -> Importing-country price rises, production rises, consumption and imports fall.
  进口关税, 配额或 VER -> 进口国价格上升, 生产增加, 消费与进口减少.
- Export subsidy -> Exporting-country price and production rise, consumption falls, and exports rise.
  出口补贴 -> 出口国价格与生产增加, 消费下降, 出口增加.

## Step 3: Identify Rent or Revenue Ownership | 第 3 步: 识别租金或收入归属

- Tariff -> Importing government receives revenue.
  关税 -> 进口国政府获得收入.
- Quota -> Rent ownership depends on license allocation.
  配额 -> 租金归属取决于许可证分配.
- VER -> Foreign exporters generally receive rents.
  VER -> 外国出口商通常获得租金.
- Export subsidy -> Exporting government pays.
  出口补贴 -> 出口国政府支付.

## Step 4: Determine Welfare | 第 4 步: 判断福利

For a small country, tariffs and quotas reduce national welfare. Export subsidies and VERs also reduce national welfare for the relevant country.

对于小国, 关税和配额降低国家福利. 出口补贴与 VER 也会降低相关国家的国家福利.

## Step 5: Identify Integration Level | 第 5 步: 识别整合层级

Check internal tariffs, common external policy, factor mobility, policy coordination, and common currency.

检查内部关税, 共同对外政策, 要素流动, 政策协调和共同货币.

## Step 6: Test Creation vs Diversion | 第 6 步: 检验创造与转移

Ask whether higher-cost domestic production or lower-cost non-member imports are being replaced.

判断被取代的是较高成本国内生产, 还是较低成本非成员国进口.

# CFA Memory Points | CFA 记忆重点

## 1. Core Trade Benefits | 核心贸易收益

Exchange + Specialization + Scale + Variety + Competition + Efficient allocation.

交换 + 专业化 + 规模 + 多样性 + 竞争 + 有效配置.

## 2. Distributional Warning | 分配警告

Trade can raise aggregate welfare while making some workers, firms, and regions worse off.

贸易可以提高总体福利, 同时使部分劳动者, 企业和地区受损.

## 3. Small-Country Tariff | 小国关税

Price rises, production rises, consumption falls, imports fall, consumers lose, producers gain, government gains revenue, and national welfare falls.

价格上升, 生产增加, 消费下降, 进口减少, 消费者受损, 生产者受益, 政府获得收入, 国家福利下降.

$$
\Delta W=-(B+D)
$$

## 4. Tariff vs Quota vs VER | 关税 vs 配额 vs VER

- Tariff revenue belongs to the importing government.
  关税收入归进口国政府.
- Quota rent ownership depends on license allocation.
  配额租金归属取决于许可证分配.
- VER rent generally belongs to foreign exporters.
  VER 租金通常归外国出口商.

## 5. Export Subsidy | 出口补贴

Exporting-country price, production, and exports rise. Consumption and national welfare fall. Government spending rises.

出口国价格, 生产和出口增加. 消费与国家福利下降. 政府支出增加.

## 6. Integration Ladder | 整合阶梯

FTA -> Customs union -> Common market -> Economic union -> Monetary union.

FTA -> 关税同盟 -> 共同市场 -> 经济联盟 -> 货币联盟.

## 7. Creation vs Diversion | 创造 vs 转移

- Trade creation replaces higher-cost domestic production with lower-cost member imports.
  贸易创造以较低成本成员国进口取代较高成本国内生产.
- Trade diversion replaces lower-cost non-member imports with higher-cost member imports.
  贸易转移以较高成本成员国进口取代较低成本非成员国进口.

## 8. Common Mistakes | 常见错误

- Do not confuse small country with small GDP or population. It means a world-price taker for the product.
  不要将小国与较小 GDP 或人口混淆. 小国是该产品世界价格的接受者.
- Do not assume a quota automatically generates government revenue.
  不要假设配额会自动产生政府收入.
- Do not confuse FTA with customs union. Only the customs union has a common external trade policy.
  不要混淆 FTA 与关税同盟. 只有关税同盟具有共同对外贸易政策.
- Do not assume deeper integration is costless. It reduces national policy independence and can transmit crises.
  不要假设更深层整合没有成本. 它会降低国家政策独立性并可能传播危机.
