# 1. Roles of Central Banks | 中央银行的职能

## Core Roles | 核心职能

| Role | Explanation |
|---|---|
| Monopoly supplier of domestic currency | Issues legal tender and protects confidence in fiat money |
| 本币垄断供应者 | 发行法定货币并维护对法定货币的信心 |
| Banker to the government and banks | Holds government and commercial bank accounts and facilitates transactions |
| 政府与银行的银行 | 持有政府与商业银行账户并协助完成交易 |
| Lender of last resort | Provides emergency liquidity when other funding is unavailable |
| 最后贷款人 | 在其他融资来源不可用时提供紧急流动性 |
| Payments system regulator and supervisor | Establishes standards and promotes reliable settlement |
| 支付系统监管者 | 制定标准并确保结算系统可靠运行 |
| Conductor of monetary policy | Influences money, credit, aggregate demand, and inflation |
| 货币政策执行者 | 影响货币, 信贷, 总需求和通胀 |
| Banking system supervisor | May license and supervise banks, although this role can be shared with other agencies |
| 银行体系监管者 | 可能负责银行许可与监管, 但该职能可由其他机构共同承担 |
| Manager of foreign currency and gold reserves | Holds and manages official reserve assets |
| 外汇与黄金储备管理者 | 持有并管理官方储备资产 |

## Money Concepts | 货币概念

- Gold standard: Currency can be converted into a prespecified amount of gold.
  金本位制: 货币可以兑换为预先规定数量的黄金.
- Fiat money: Money is not convertible into a commodity and derives value from government decree and public acceptance.
  法定信用货币: 货币不能兑换为商品, 其价值来自政府法令和公众接受.
- Legal tender: Money that must be accepted when offered in payment.
  法定货币: 用于支付时必须被接受的货币.

## Lender of Last Resort | 最后贷款人

Emergency central bank lending can reduce the risk of bank runs and contagion by assuring depositors and markets that solvent banks can obtain liquidity.

中央银行的紧急贷款可以向存款人和市场表明有偿付能力的银行能够获得流动性, 从而降低银行挤兑与危机传染风险.

**Exam point:** A central bank provides liquidity, but it does not normally set tax rates. It may supervise banks, but it is not necessarily the sole banking supervisor.

**考试重点:** 中央银行提供流动性, 但通常不制定税率. 它可能监管银行, 但不一定是唯一的银行监管机构.

# 2. Objectives of Monetary Policy | 货币政策目标

## Primary Objective | 首要目标

The overarching long-run objective of most central banks is price stability, meaning low and relatively stable inflation.

大多数中央银行的首要长期目标是价格稳定, 即低且相对稳定的通胀.

Other possible objectives include:

其他可能目标包括:

- Financial system stability.
  金融体系稳定.
- Maximum or full employment.
  最大就业或充分就业.
- Sustainable economic growth.
  可持续经济增长.
- Moderate long-term interest rates.
  适度的长期利率.
- Confidence in the currency and payments system.
  对货币与支付系统的信心.

**Exam point:** Fast growth or a current account surplus is not normally the overarching objective. Price stability is the standard answer.

**考试重点:** 快速增长或经常账户盈余通常不是首要目标. 标准答案是价格稳定.

# 3. Monetary Policy Tools | 货币政策工具

Central banks use 3 primary tools:

中央银行使用 3 种主要工具:

## 3.1 Open Market Operations | 公开市场操作

Open market operations are central bank purchases or sales of securities, usually government bonds.

公开市场操作是中央银行买卖证券的行为, 通常交易政府债券.

| Central bank action | Bank reserves | Lending capacity | Money and credit | Policy direction |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| Buys bonds | Increase | Increase | Tend to increase | Expansionary |
| 购买债券 | 增加 | 增加 | 倾向增加 | 扩张性 |
| Sells bonds | Decrease | Decrease | Tend to decrease | Contractionary |
| 出售债券 | 减少 | 减少 | 倾向减少 | 紧缩性 |

**Memory rule:** Central bank buys bonds -> pays cash -> bank reserves rise.

**记忆规则:** 中央银行购买债券 -> 支付现金 -> 银行准备金增加.

## 3.2 Policy Rate and Repo Market | 政策利率与回购市场

The policy rate is the publicly announced short-term rate at which a central bank is willing to lend to commercial banks, although institutional arrangements vary.

政策利率是中央银行公开宣布的短期利率, 通常是其向商业银行贷款的利率, 但各国制度安排有所不同.

A repurchase agreement, or repo, is a collateralized loan. One party sells a security and simultaneously agrees to repurchase it later at a specified price.

回购协议, 即 repo, 是一种抵押贷款. 一方出售证券, 同时约定未来以指定价格购回该证券.

When the central bank raises its policy rate:

当中央银行提高政策利率时:

- Commercial bank funding costs rise.
  商业银行融资成本上升.
- Base rates and customer lending rates tend to rise.
  基准贷款利率与客户贷款利率倾向上升.
- Borrowing, credit creation, and broad money growth tend to fall.
  借款, 信贷创造和广义货币增长倾向下降.
- Aggregate demand and inflationary pressure tend to weaken.
  总需求和通胀压力倾向减弱.

The reverse generally occurs when the central bank cuts its policy rate.

中央银行降低政策利率时, 上述影响通常反向发生.

## 3.3 Reserve Requirements | 法定准备金要求

Reserve requirements specify the proportion of deposits that banks must hold as reserves.

法定准备金要求规定银行必须以准备金形式持有的存款比例.

| Change | Lending capacity | Money creation | Policy direction |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Raise reserve requirement | Decrease | Decrease | Contractionary |
| 提高准备金率 | 减少 | 减少 | 紧缩性 |
| Lower reserve requirement | Increase | Increase | Expansionary |
| 降低准备金率 | 增加 | 增加 | 扩张性 |

Frequent changes are disruptive because banks may need to reduce lending quickly to meet a higher requirement. The tool is used more actively in many emerging markets than in developed markets.

频繁调整会扰乱银行经营, 因为银行可能需要迅速减少贷款以满足更高的准备金要求. 许多新兴市场比发达市场更积极地使用该工具.

# 4. Monetary Transmission Mechanism | 货币传导机制

The monetary transmission mechanism is the process through which central bank actions affect financial conditions, aggregate demand, output, and inflation.

货币传导机制是中央银行行动影响金融条件, 总需求, 产出和通胀的过程.

## Four Interconnected Channels | 4 条相互关联的渠道

1. Interest rate and bank lending channel.
   利率与银行贷款渠道.
2. Asset price and wealth channel.
   资产价格与财富渠道.
3. Exchange rate channel.
   汇率渠道.
4. Expectations channel.
   预期渠道.

These channels are interconnected, not independent.

这些渠道彼此关联, 并非相互独立.

## 4.1 Contractionary Transmission | 紧缩性传导

Policy rate rises -> market rates rise -> borrowing and investment fall -> asset values tend to fall -> consumption weakens -> domestic demand falls -> inflationary pressure falls.

政策利率上升 -> 市场利率上升 -> 借款和投资下降 -> 资产价值倾向下降 -> 消费减弱 -> 国内需求下降 -> 通胀压力下降.

Higher domestic interest rates may also attract capital and cause the currency to appreciate.

较高的国内利率还可能吸引资本流入并导致本币升值.

Currency appreciation tends to:

本币升值倾向于:

- Reduce import prices and inflation.
  降低进口价格和通胀.
- Make exports more expensive to foreign buyers.
  使出口商品对外国买家而言更昂贵.
- Reduce net external demand.
  降低净外部需求.

## 4.2 Expansionary Transmission | 扩张性传导

Policy rate falls -> market rates tend to fall -> borrowing and investment rise -> asset prices and wealth may rise -> consumption rises -> aggregate demand and inflationary pressure rise.

政策利率下降 -> 市场利率倾向下降 -> 借款和投资增加 -> 资产价格和财富可能上升 -> 消费增加 -> 总需求和通胀压力上升.

Lower interest rates may weaken the currency, raise import prices, and support exports.

较低利率可能导致本币贬值, 提高进口价格, 并支持出口.

## 4.3 Expectations | 预期

If economic agents expect further policy tightening, borrowing, consumption, investment, and asset prices may decline before additional rate increases occur.

如果经济主体预期政策将进一步收紧, 借款, 消费, 投资和资产价格可能在后续加息发生前下降.

Credible communication strengthens the transmission of monetary policy by shaping inflation and interest rate expectations.

可信的政策沟通通过影响通胀预期和利率预期来增强货币政策传导.

**Exam point:** The central bank directly sets or targets a short-term policy rate. Import prices, output, and inflation are downstream outcomes.

**考试重点:** 中央银行直接设定或引导短期政策利率. 进口价格, 产出和通胀是下游结果.

# 5. Effective Central Banks | 有效中央银行

## 5.1 Independence | 独立性

- Operational independence: The central bank chooses how to set interest rates and use tools to achieve the target.
  操作独立性: 中央银行自行决定如何设定利率并使用工具实现目标.
- Target independence: The central bank determines the target definition, target level, and time horizon.
  目标独立性: 中央银行自行决定目标定义, 目标水平和实现期限.

A central bank can be operationally independent without being target independent if the government sets the inflation target but the bank selects the policy instruments.

如果政府设定通胀目标, 但中央银行自行选择政策工具, 则中央银行具有操作独立性, 但不具有目标独立性.

Independence reduces incentives to keep rates artificially low for short-term political purposes.

独立性降低了出于短期政治目的而人为维持低利率的动机.

## 5.2 Credibility | 可信度

Credibility means economic agents believe that the central bank will achieve its announced objective.

可信度是指经济主体相信中央银行能够实现其宣布的目标.

Credible targets can become partly self-fulfilling because expected inflation affects wage negotiations, pricing, and nominal contracts.

可信的目标可以在一定程度上自我实现, 因为预期通胀会影响工资谈判, 定价和名义合同.

## 5.3 Transparency | 透明度

Transparent central banks explain their objectives, indicators, forecasts, decisions, and risks. Clear communication improves accountability and helps anchor expectations.

透明的中央银行会解释其目标, 指标, 预测, 决策和风险. 清晰沟通可以提高问责性并稳定预期.

Inflation reports commonly discuss:

通胀报告通常讨论:

- Money and credit conditions.
  货币与信贷状况.
- Financial market conditions.
  金融市场状况.
- Real economic activity and labor markets.
  实际经济活动与劳动力市场.
- Price developments and medium-term forecasts.
  价格变化与中期预测.

**Memory rule:** Effective central bank = Independence + Credibility + Transparency.

**记忆规则:** 有效中央银行 = 独立性 + 可信度 + 透明度.

# 6. Inflation Targeting | 通胀目标制

An inflation-targeting framework announces an explicit inflation objective, often with a permitted range, and uses policy tools to keep forecast inflation consistent with that target.

通胀目标制会宣布明确的通胀目标, 通常还包括允许区间, 并使用政策工具使预测通胀与目标一致.

## Main Features | 主要特征

- An independent and credible central bank.
  独立且可信的中央银行.
- Transparent policy decisions and communication.
  透明的政策决策与沟通.
- Use of broad monetary, financial, and real economic information.
  使用广泛的货币, 金融和实际经济信息.
- A clear, symmetric, forward-looking medium-term target.
  明确, 对称, 前瞻性的中期目标.
- A target above 0 to reduce deflation risk, but low enough to preserve price stability.
  目标高于 0, 以降低通缩风险, 但又足够低, 以维持价格稳定.

Central banks generally target future inflation because reported inflation describes past price changes and monetary policy affects the economy with a lag.

中央银行通常以未来通胀为目标, 因为已公布通胀反映过去的价格变化, 且货币政策存在传导时滞.

## Policy Response | 政策反应

| Forecast condition | Typical response | Stance |
|---|---|---|
| Inflation expected above target because of excessive demand | Raise policy rate, sell bonds, or tighten reserves | Contractionary |
| 需求过强导致预期通胀高于目标 | 提高政策利率, 出售债券, 或提高准备金要求 | 紧缩性 |
| Inflation expected below target and activity is weak | Cut policy rate, buy bonds, or ease reserves | Expansionary |
| 预期通胀低于目标且经济活动疲弱 | 降低政策利率, 购买债券, 或降低准备金要求 | 扩张性 |

## Demand Shock vs Supply Shock | 需求冲击与供给冲击

- Positive demand shock: Consumption and investment rise, increasing output and inflation. Monetary tightening may be appropriate.
  正向需求冲击: 消费和投资增加, 推高产出和通胀. 货币紧缩可能适当.
- Adverse supply shock: Input costs rise while output weakens. Tightening may reduce inflation but worsen the downturn.
  负向供给冲击: 投入成本上升, 同时产出减弱. 紧缩可能降低通胀, 但会加剧经济下行.

**Exam point:** Identify the source of inflation before selecting the policy response. The same rise in inflation can require different responses.

**考试重点:** 在选择政策反应前先识别通胀来源. 相同的通胀上升可能需要不同的政策反应.

# 7. Neutral Interest Rate | 中性利率

The neutral policy rate neither stimulates nor restrains the economy.

中性政策利率既不刺激经济, 也不抑制经济.

Let:

设:

| Symbol | Definition |
|---|---|
| $r_N$ | Nominal neutral policy rate |
| $r_N$ | 名义中性政策利率 |
| $g_T$ | Real trend growth rate |
| $g_T$ | 实际趋势增长率 |
| $\pi_E$ | Long-run expected inflation or credible inflation target |
| $\pi_E$ | 长期预期通胀率或可信的通胀目标 |

$$
r_N=g_T+\pi_E
$$

| Policy rate position | Monetary stance |
|---|---|
| Policy rate below $r_N$ | Expansionary |
| 政策利率低于 $r_N$ | 扩张性 |
| Policy rate equal to $r_N$ | Neutral |
| 政策利率等于 $r_N$ | 中性 |
| Policy rate above $r_N$ | Contractionary |
| 政策利率高于 $r_N$ | 紧缩性 |

## Example | 例题

Given real trend growth of 3% and an inflation target of 2%:

给定实际趋势增长率为 3%, 通胀目标为 2%:

$$
r_N=3\%+2\%=5\%
$$

A 4% policy rate is expansionary, a 5% rate is neutral, and a 6% rate is contractionary.

4% 的政策利率属于扩张性, 5% 属于中性, 6% 属于紧缩性.

**Exam trap:** "High" or "low" must be judged relative to the neutral rate, not relative to zero or to historical rates alone.

**考试陷阱:** 判断利率高低必须以中性利率为基准, 不能只与 0 或历史利率比较.

The neutral rate is estimated, not observed directly, so analysts can disagree about the current policy stance.

中性利率是估计值, 不能被直接观察, 因此分析师可能对当前政策立场存在分歧.

# 8. Exchange Rate Targeting | 汇率目标制

A central bank may maintain a fixed exchange rate or target band against a major currency by buying and selling domestic and foreign currency.

中央银行可以通过买卖本币和外币, 维持对主要货币的固定汇率或目标区间.

## Main Benefit | 主要优点

The country may import the low-inflation credibility and inflation experience of the target-currency country.

该国可以引入目标货币发行国的低通胀可信度和通胀表现.

## Main Cost | 主要代价

Domestic interest rates, money supply, and economic conditions must adjust to defend the exchange rate. Monetary policy independence is reduced.

国内利率, 货币供应量和经济状况必须调整以维护汇率. 货币政策独立性因此降低.

## Defending the Target | 维护汇率目标

If domestic inflation rises above inflation in the target country, the domestic currency faces depreciation pressure.

如果国内通胀高于目标货币发行国的通胀, 本币将面临贬值压力.

To defend the currency, the central bank:

为维护本币, 中央银行会:

1. Sell foreign currency reserves.
   出售外汇储备.
2. Buy domestic currency.
   买入本币.
3. Reduce the domestic money supply.
   减少国内货币供应量.
4. Raise short-term interest rates.
   提高短期利率.

If domestic inflation falls below inflation in the target country, the reverse intervention tends to increase domestic money supply and reduce interest rates.

如果国内通胀低于目标货币发行国的通胀, 反向干预倾向于增加国内货币供应量并降低利率.

## Risks | 风险

- The commitment must be credible.
  政策承诺必须可信.
- Foreign currency reserves are finite.
  外汇储备有限.
- Speculators may attack an unsustainable target.
  投机者可能攻击不可持续的汇率目标.
- Devaluation can damage borrowers with foreign-currency debt.
  本币贬值可能损害持有外币债务的借款人.

**Exam point:** Under an exchange rate target, domestic conditions adapt to the target. The central bank does not retain full freedom to set rates for domestic objectives.

**考试重点:** 在汇率目标制下, 国内经济条件必须适应汇率目标. 中央银行不能完全自由地根据国内目标设定利率.

# 9. Limitations of Monetary Policy | 货币政策的局限

## 9.1 Imperfect Transmission | 传导机制不完善

Central banks control neither household and corporate deposits nor banks' willingness to lend. More reserves do not guarantee more credit or spending.

中央银行既不能控制家庭和企业的存款行为, 也不能控制银行的放贷意愿. 更多准备金并不保证更多信贷或支出.

Short-term rate changes may not move long-term rates in the intended direction because long-term yields depend on expectations of future short-term rates and inflation.

短期利率变化可能无法使长期利率按预期方向移动, 因为长期收益率取决于未来短期利率和通胀预期.

For example, a rate increase that convinces investors of a future recession may reduce expected inflation and lower long-term bond yields.

例如, 如果加息使投资者预期未来发生衰退, 预期通胀可能下降, 长期债券收益率反而可能下降.

## 9.2 Bond Market Vigilantes | 债券市场监督者

If investors believe policy is too loose and inflation risk is rising, they may sell long-term bonds and push yields higher. This market response can offset monetary easing.

如果投资者认为政策过于宽松且通胀风险上升, 他们可能出售长期债券并推高收益率. 这种市场反应可能抵消货币宽松.

## 9.3 Liquidity Trap | 流动性陷阱

A liquidity trap occurs when money demand becomes extremely interest-elastic. Economic agents willingly hold additional money, so injections of reserves fail to reduce rates or stimulate real activity.

当货币需求对利率具有极高弹性时, 流动性陷阱出现. 经济主体愿意持有新增货币, 因而注入准备金无法进一步降低利率或刺激实际经济活动.

## 9.4 Deflation and the Lower Bound | 通缩与利率下限

Deflation is a persistent decline in the general price level.

通缩是总体价格水平持续下降.

Deflation can:

通缩可以:

- Increase the real value of debt.
  提高债务的实际价值.
- Encourage households to postpone consumption.
  鼓励家庭推迟消费.
- Weaken demand and create further deflationary pressure.
  削弱需求并形成进一步通缩压力.

Conventional policy becomes less effective when nominal rates approach their lower bound.

当名义利率接近下限时, 常规货币政策的有效性下降.

## 9.5 Quantitative Easing | 量化宽松

Quantitative easing, or QE, is large-scale central bank asset purchasing financed by newly created reserves.

量化宽松, 即 QE, 是中央银行使用新创造的准备金进行大规模资产购买.

Intended effects include:

预期效果包括:

- Increase bank reserves and broad money.
  增加银行准备金与广义货币.
- Reduce longer-term yields.
  降低较长期收益率.
- Support asset prices, lending, investment, and consumption.
  支持资产价格, 贷款, 投资和消费.

Limitations and risks include:

局限与风险包括:

- Banks may hold excess reserves instead of lending.
  银行可能持有超额准备金而不放贷.
- Borrowers may be unwilling or unable to borrow.
  借款人可能不愿或无法借款.
- Risky asset purchases can expose the central bank to losses and damage confidence.
  购买高风险资产可能使中央银行遭受损失并损害信心.
- Large government bond purchases can blur the distinction between monetary policy and deficit financing.
  大规模购买政府债券可能模糊货币政策与赤字融资之间的界限.

## 9.6 Emerging Market Constraints | 新兴市场约束

- Illiquid government bond and interbank markets.
  政府债券市场与银行间市场流动性不足.
- Rapid structural change and uncertain neutral rates.
  经济结构快速变化且中性利率不确定.
- Financial innovation that changes monetary aggregates.
  金融创新改变货币总量定义.
- Weak inflation-control record and low credibility.
  控制通胀的历史表现较弱且可信度较低.
- Limited central bank independence.
  中央银行独立性有限.

# 10. Interaction of Monetary and Fiscal Policy | 货币政策与财政政策的互动

Both policies affect aggregate demand, but through different channels and with different effects on the composition of output.

两种政策都影响总需求, 但传导渠道不同, 对产出构成的影响也不同.

| Fiscal policy | Monetary policy | Aggregate demand and rates | Composition effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Easy | Tight | Fiscal expansion raises demand, while monetary tightening raises rates and restrains private demand | Public sector share tends to rise |
| 宽松 | 紧缩 | 财政扩张提高需求, 货币紧缩提高利率并抑制私人需求 | 公共部门占比倾向上升 |
| Tight | Easy | Fiscal contraction reduces public demand, while lower rates stimulate private demand | Private sector share tends to rise |
| 紧缩 | 宽松 | 财政紧缩减少公共需求, 较低利率刺激私人需求 | 私人部门占比倾向上升 |
| Easy | Easy | Strongly expansionary, with both public and private demand supported | Both sectors can grow |
| 宽松 | 宽松 | 高度扩张性, 公共和私人需求均获得支持 | 两个部门均可增长 |
| Tight | Tight | Public and private demand both weaken | Strongly contractionary |
| 紧缩 | 紧缩 | 公共和私人需求均减弱 | 高度紧缩性 |

## Policy Mix and Growth | 政策组合与增长

- Tight fiscal plus easy monetary policy can free resources for private investment and support private-sector growth.
  紧缩财政加宽松货币政策可以为私人投资释放资源并支持私人部门增长.
- Easy fiscal policy may be appropriate when public infrastructure or human capital is a constraint on potential output.
  当公共基础设施或人力资本限制潜在产出时, 宽松财政政策可能适当.
- Easy fiscal plus easy monetary policy risks excessive demand and inflation.
  宽松财政加宽松货币政策可能造成需求过强和通胀.
- Easy fiscal plus tight monetary policy can raise interest rates and crowd out private demand.
  宽松财政加紧缩货币政策可能提高利率并挤出私人需求.

## Monetary Accommodation and Fiscal Multipliers | 货币配合与财政乘数

Fiscal stimulus is generally more powerful when the central bank accommodates it by keeping interest rates low.

当中央银行通过维持低利率配合财政刺激时, 财政刺激通常更有力.

Without monetary accommodation, higher aggregate demand may cause the central bank to raise rates, reducing private investment and consumption.

如果没有货币配合, 更高的总需求可能促使中央银行加息, 从而减少私人投资和消费.

Targeted transfers to low-income households tend to have a larger effect than untargeted transfers because recipients usually have a higher marginal propensity to consume.

针对低收入家庭的定向转移支付通常比非定向转移支付影响更大, 因为受益人的边际消费倾向通常更高.

Direct government expenditure normally has a larger demand effect than an equal amount of general transfer payments.

直接政府支出对需求的影响通常大于等额的一般转移支付.

## Ricardian Equivalence | 李嘉图等价

If households fully anticipate future taxes required to repay debt-financed tax cuts, they may save the tax cut. Fiscal policy then has less effect on private spending, which may lead policy makers to favor monetary tools.

如果家庭完全预期到偿还债务融资减税所需的未来税收, 他们可能储蓄减税所得. 此时财政政策对私人支出的影响较小, 政策制定者可能更偏好货币工具.

## Credibility and Commitment | 可信度与政策承诺

Persistent fiscal deficits can raise inflation expectations, long-term interest rates, and crowding out. This reduces the effectiveness of stimulus and lowers potential output.

持续财政赤字可能提高通胀预期与长期利率, 并引发挤出效应. 这会降低刺激政策的有效性并减少潜在产出.

Large-scale central bank purchases of government debt may be viewed as monetization of the deficit, potentially weakening central bank independence and credibility.

中央银行大规模购买政府债务可能被视为赤字货币化, 从而削弱中央银行的独立性和可信度.

# 11. Exam Decision Framework | 考试判断框架

## Step 1: Identify the Authority | 第 1 步: 识别决策主体

- Central bank action affecting rates, reserves, money, or credit -> Monetary policy.
  中央银行影响利率, 准备金, 货币或信贷的行动 -> 货币政策.
- Government action affecting taxes, transfers, or spending -> Fiscal policy.
  政府影响税收, 转移支付或支出的行动 -> 财政政策.

## Step 2: Determine the Tool Direction | 第 2 步: 判断工具方向

- Bond purchases, rate cuts, and lower reserve requirements -> Expansionary.
  购买债券, 降息和降低准备金要求 -> 扩张性.
- Bond sales, rate increases, and higher reserve requirements -> Contractionary.
  出售债券, 加息和提高准备金要求 -> 紧缩性.

## Step 3: Compare the Policy Rate with the Neutral Rate | 第 3 步: 将政策利率与中性利率比较

- Below neutral -> Expansionary.
  低于中性利率 -> 扩张性.
- Above neutral -> Contractionary.
  高于中性利率 -> 紧缩性.

## Step 4: Trace the Transmission | 第 4 步: 追踪传导

Policy rate -> market rates and credit -> asset prices, exchange rate, and expectations -> consumption, investment, and net exports -> output and inflation.

政策利率 -> 市场利率与信贷 -> 资产价格, 汇率和预期 -> 消费, 投资和净出口 -> 产出与通胀.

## Step 5: Identify the Shock | 第 5 步: 识别冲击

Distinguish excess demand from an adverse supply shock before deciding whether tightening is appropriate.

在判断是否应当紧缩前, 区分需求过强与负向供给冲击.

## Step 6: Check Constraints | 第 6 步: 检查约束

Consider credibility, the lower bound, bank willingness to lend, borrower demand, policy lags, and interaction with fiscal policy.

考虑可信度, 利率下限, 银行放贷意愿, 借款需求, 政策时滞, 以及与财政政策的互动.

# CFA Memory Points | CFA 记忆重点

## 1. Central Bank Roles | 中央银行职能

Currency supplier, government and bankers' bank, lender of last resort, payments regulator, monetary policy conductor, and possible banking supervisor.

货币供应者, 政府与银行的银行, 最后贷款人, 支付系统监管者, 货币政策执行者, 以及可能的银行监管者.

## 2. Three Tools | 3 种工具

- Buy bonds, cut rates, or lower reserve requirements -> Expansionary.
  购买债券, 降息, 或降低准备金要求 -> 扩张性.
- Sell bonds, raise rates, or raise reserve requirements -> Contractionary.
  出售债券, 加息, 或提高准备金要求 -> 紧缩性.

## 3. Four Transmission Channels | 4 条传导渠道

Interest rates, asset prices, exchange rates, and expectations.

利率, 资产价格, 汇率和预期.

## 4. Effective Central Bank | 有效中央银行

Independence + Credibility + Transparency.

独立性 + 可信度 + 透明度.

## 5. Neutral Rate | 中性利率

$$
r_N=g_T+\pi_E
$$

Policy rate below neutral is expansionary. Policy rate above neutral is contractionary.

政策利率低于中性利率属于扩张性. 政策利率高于中性利率属于紧缩性.

## 6. Exchange Rate Target | 汇率目标

To defend a weakening currency, sell foreign reserves, buy domestic currency, reduce domestic money supply, and raise short-term rates.

为维护面临贬值压力的本币, 出售外汇储备, 买入本币, 减少国内货币供应量, 并提高短期利率.

## 7. Key Limitations | 核心局限

The central bank cannot force banks to lend or households and firms to borrow. Near the lower bound, a liquidity trap can make conventional policy ineffective.

中央银行不能迫使银行放贷, 也不能迫使家庭和企业借款. 接近利率下限时, 流动性陷阱可能使常规政策失效.

## 8. Policy Mix | 政策组合

- Easy fiscal + tight monetary -> Public sector share tends to rise.
  宽松财政 + 紧缩货币 -> 公共部门占比倾向上升.
- Tight fiscal + easy monetary -> Private sector share tends to rise.
  紧缩财政 + 宽松货币 -> 私人部门占比倾向上升.
- Easy fiscal + easy monetary -> Strongest expansionary combination.
  宽松财政 + 宽松货币 -> 扩张性最强的组合.
- Tight fiscal + tight monetary -> Strongest contractionary combination.
  紧缩财政 + 紧缩货币 -> 紧缩性最强的组合.

## 9. Common Mistakes | 常见错误

- Do not treat the 4 transmission channels as independent.
  不要将 4 条传导渠道视为相互独立.
- Do not judge a policy rate as high or low without comparing it with the neutral rate.
  不要在未与中性利率比较时判断政策利率高或低.
- Do not assume more bank reserves automatically create more loans.
  不要假设更多银行准备金会自动创造更多贷款.
- Do not tighten automatically after every inflation increase. First identify whether the shock comes from demand or supply.
  不要在每次通胀上升后自动采取紧缩. 应先判断冲击来自需求还是供给.
